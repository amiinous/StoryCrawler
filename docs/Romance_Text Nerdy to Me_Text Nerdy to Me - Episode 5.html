Text Nerdy to Me - Episode 5

Erin and Kimmy arrive home, greeted by an ecstatic Nymeria.

Erin dumps her suitcase in the foyer and collapses on the sofa.

Before she can relax, the bell rings.

Erin

Kimmy, can you get that?

Kimmy

Why? You know it’s for you.

Erin

Please?

Kimmy

Sorry. I’m…in the bathroom

Erin

Liar.

Erin sighs and plods to the door.

She opens the door to Charlie, who shoots her a smile and gives her a hug.

Charlie

Hey, baby. You’re finally home.

Erin

We just got in.

Charlie

I’ve been calling you.

Erin

Sorry.

Charlie

What did you do all day?

Erin

Um, flew home, then went out to dinner with Kimmy and Tiffany…

Erin stops talking when she realizes Charlie is looking down at his phone.

He looks up.

Charlie

That’s nice, baby.

Charlie

Do you have any beer?

Erin

In the fridge.

Charlie fetches himself a beer and sits across from Erin.

Nymeria sits by her side, keeping her gaze on Charlie.

Charlie

So how was Vegas?

Erin

Fine.

Charlie

What did you do?

Erin

Nothing much.

Erin

How was your weekend?

Charlie

Busy. I worked most of the time.

Charlie proceeds to tell several stories about things that happened at work.

Most of them are about people she’s never met.

Erin tries to focus, but keeps replaying her conversations with Kimmy and Tiffany.

And her conversation with Will.

Will made her laugh and engaged her in conversation that she found interesting.

Will made her happy.

Will KNEW her.

Erin

I’m sorry, Charlie. Can we talk?

Charlie blinks.

Charlie

I thought we were talking.

Erin

I mean, can we talk about us?

Charlie

Okay.

He stares expectantly at Erin.

Charlie

Well? What do you want to talk about?

Erin

How do you think things are going between us?

Charlie shrugs.

Charlie

Great.

Erin

Great?

Charlie

Yeah. I mean, we get along. Don’t fight. Our schedules mesh—

Erin

Is that enough for you?

Charlie

Enough? Of course it is.

Erin has more questions to ask, but Kimmy enters the room so she holds her tongue.

Charlie

Hey Kimmy. Look at you.

Charlie

I barely recognized you with the blonde hair.

Kimmy

It’s new.

Charlie

I like it. It’s…normal.

Kimmy

Hmm. I think it’s boring. I’m gonna change it soon.

Erin laughs.

Erin

Then why did you dye it in the first place?

Kimmy

I had a job interview on Friday.

Erin

How’d it go?

Kimmy

I didn’t like the vibe in that office. I’m not that anal.

Charlie

Sometimes you’ve got to play the game to fit in.

Kimmy

And sometimes you’re happier if you don’t play the game…

Charlie

Oh. Speaking of games…It’s about time.

Charlie grabs the remote and smiles at Erin.

Charlie

The Mariners are playing the A’s.

Charlie

I don’t want you to miss your favorite sport.

Kimmy

She hates baseball.

Charlie ignores Kimmy and turns on the game.

Charlie

Go make us some popcorn, okay, baby?

Erin pinches the bridge of her nose.

Erin

I’m sorry Charlie. I actually don’t want to watch the game.

Erin

I think I’m getting a migraine.

Kimmy

Go watch the game at your place.

Charlie

Your screen is larger.

Erin

Ugh. I think I’m going to barf.

Charlie leaps to his feet.

Charlie

Maybe you’re right. I better go.

Charlie

Feel better, baby. I’ll see you tomorrow.

Charlie

Glad you had fun on your little ladies’ trip.

The door closes behind him and Erin sighs.

Kimmy

Do you need a bowl?

Erin

No. I’m fine.

Kimmy grins.

Kimmy

You lied about feeling sick?

Kimmy

There’s hope for you yet.

After a few hours of introspection, Erin decides to text Will.

Erin

Hey you.

Will

Hey! It’s been a while.

Will

You back home now?

Erin

Yup.

Will

You, ah, thought about meeting up again?

Will

IRL?

Will

No pressure or anything.

Erin

Before we do that, I have a confession to make.

Will

This is becoming a habit.

Erin

It’s not good.

Will

Okay. I’m ready.

Erin

I kind of have…a boyfriend.

Erin

I’m sorry I didn’t mention it before.

Erin

You there?

Will

Yeah. I’m here.

Will

Why didn’t you mention it before?

Erin

I don’t know.

Erin

I mean, I do. I was…enjoying our chats.

Erin

But if I brought that up, you would have bolted.

Erin

Right?

Will

Probably. Fidelity is important to me.

Erin

Me too. I’m sorry.

Will

I shouldn’t be surprised — you’re too hot to be single.

Will

I guess I hoped…

Will

Shit.

Erin

I’m sorry.

Will

How serious is it?

Erin

Serious?

Will

Yeah. Are you engaged or something?

Erin

Nothing like that.

Will

Do you live together?

Erin

No.

Will

Do you love him?

Erin’s fingers freeze over her keyboard.

Does she love Charlie?

Will

Erin?

Erin

I thought I did. But lately…

Will

Trouble in paradise?

Erin

LOL, it’s hardly paradise.

Erin

I find myself questioning whether or not he makes me happy.

Will

Does he?

Erin

I…I don’t know.

Will

You know — deep inside.

Will

You have to be honest with yourself.

Erin

That is easier said than done.

Will

Okay, I have a question.

Erin

Shoot.

Will

Is he the person you want for life?

Erin

For life?

Will

Yeah. For the rest of your life.

Will

Your choice of mate kind of defines you.

Will

Is he what you want…forever?

Was he?

A man who didn’t know by now that she hated baseball?

A man who never made her laugh?

Who never laughed with her?

The simple answer was no.

Will

IMHO, you deserve the best.

Will

Choose what is best for you.

Erin

Are you saying I should dump him?

Will

That decision is up to you.

Will

But if you do decide to dump him…

Will

I’m here.

Will

I’m right here.
