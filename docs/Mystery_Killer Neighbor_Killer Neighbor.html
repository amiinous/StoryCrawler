Killer Neighbor

Ella

Mom, I’m so scared.

Mom

What’s wrong, sweetie?

Ella

I just saw our neighbor get shot!

Mom

Which neighbor?!

Ella

Jason — from across the street!

Mom

Get away from the window!

Mom

Are you away from the window?

Ella

Yeah.

Ella

Please come home. Are you still at the store?

Mom

Yes. But I’m leaving right now.

Ella

I’m going to call the police.

Mom

Don’t do that!

Mom

You’re probably just imagining things.

Ella

Then why did you tell me to move away from the window?

Ella

That doesn’t make sense!

Mom

I’m just trying to get you to calm down.

Mom

So you don’t get hysterical the way you do.

Mom

Keep texting me.

Mom

I’m calling the police now.

Mom

They’ll be there soon.

Ella

Okay. Tell them I saw a man shoot our neighbor.

Mom

A man?

Ella

Yeah. He looked like some kind of construction worker—

Ella

He was wearing a hard hat.

Mom

I just called 911 and gave them the information.

Ella

Tell them to send an ambulance, Mom!

Ella

Jason might still be alive.

Mom

Really?

Ella

Idk, I guess the chances aren’t very good.

Ella

Mom, I just thought of something.

Mom

What?

Ella

That creepy guy Bill who lives at the end of the block…

Ella

Isn’t he a construction worker?

Mom

Honey, that doesn’t prove anything.

Ella

Yeah, but I saw him arguing with Jason recently.

Ella

He looked really angry. You should’ve seen the look on his face—

Ella

It scared me.

Mom

Oh my god. Do you really think he did it?

Mom

Do you think Bill killed Jason?

Mom

That is so sick.

Ella

Mom, what about Cindy, Jason’s wife?!

Ella

What if she’s in danger?

Ella

What if she was home and Bill shot her too?!

Mom

Ella, I’m sure Cindy is alright.

Ella

How can you be sure?

Ella

Should I call 911 too?

Mom

No. Don’t do that!

Mom

I already did. The police are on their way.

Mom

Just stay away from the window.

Ella

I keep trying to call you.

Ella

I need to hear your voice.

Ella

Why aren’t you picking up?

Ella

Oh no!!

Mom

What is it?

Ella

Cindy just ran out of the house.

Mom

I told you to stay away from the window!

Ella

This is so weird.

Ella

I don’t understand.

Mom

What, Ella? What?

Ella

She’s wearing a construction worker’s uniform.

Ella

And a hard hat.

Ella

And she’s holding a gun.

Ella

That means…

Ella

It must have been Cindy who killed Jason!

Mom

No. There must be some other explanation.

Ella

Mom, it’s her! I recognize her face under the hat.

Ella

She must have worn the hat and the uniform to avoid being recognized!

Ella

But why would she kill her husband?

Ella

OMG, I think…

Mom

What, Ella?

Ella

I think she’s coming over here.

Ella

I don’t hear sirens yet, Mom!

Mom

That’s probably because the police don’t want the killer to hear them.

Ella

Mom, I hear a key in the lock.

Ella

Does Cindy have a key to our house?

Ella

She’s opening the door, Mom!

Ella

What should I do?

Ella

I think she wants to kill me too!! Why?!!

Mom

Ella, I want you to pay very close attention to what I tell you.

Mom

At the top of your lungs, I want you to shout…

Mom

THIS IS ELLA.

Ella

But then she’ll know where I am!!

Mom

Ella, you know I love you with all my heart, don’t you?

Ella

Yeah.

Mom

Please. Shout THIS IS ELLA.

Mom

At the top of your lungs.

Mom

Now.

Ella

Okay, Mom…

Ella

I trust you.

Ella

But if I don’t make it out of here alive…

Ella

I love you so much.

Mom

I love you too — with all my heart, Ella.

One hour later.

Mom

Ella, are you there?

Mom

Are you okay?

Mom

It’s Mom.

Mom

It’s REALLY Mom this time.

Ella

Mom? I’m okay.

Ella

I did what you told me to do.

Ella

And Cindy ran out of the house.

Ella

But the police got her.

Ella

Jason is dead.

Ella

And they arrested her for his murder.

Mom

I know, honey.

Ella

Wait a minute…

Ella

What do you mean, it’s really you?

Mom

Let me call you to explain.

Ella’s phone rings, and she answers it on the first ring.

She’s so happy to hear her mother’s voice.

Mom

I’m at the police station now.

Mom

I just gave them my statement.

Ella

What happened to you?!!

Mom

That was your father before, texting you.

Mom

He was pretending to be me.

Ella

What?! Why?

Mom

Cindy and your father were having an affair.

Ella

Dad was? With Cindy?

Mom

I know it’s hard to believe.

Mom

It took me awhile to wrap my head around it.

Mom

They wanted to run away together.

Mom

That’s why she killed Jason.

Ella

Oh my god.

Mom

It gets worse. Ella, your father…

Mom

He kidnapped me.

Mom

And he…he was going to kill me too.

Mom

He needed money before they ran away, so he planned to collect my life insurance.

Mom

I guess Cindy and your father both had the same idea independently.

Ella

Mom, that’s crazy! You must have been so scared.

Mom

I was. Ella, he followed me to the store.

Mom

He came up to me as I was leaving my car. He looked crazed.

Mom

I asked him what was wrong.

Mom

He forced me into the backseat of the car. He tied my hands behind my back.

Mom

Then he drove me to a deserted area…

Mom

He was going to shoot me and dump my body in the river!!

Ella

What?! Dad was going to do that??

Mom

I know. I can’t believe it either. I think I’m still in shock.

Mom

Thank God someone saw us in the parking lot and realized I was in trouble.

Mom

They called 911.

Mom

The police found me just in time.

Ella

Oh my god, Mom! I still don’t get how Dad could do that to you!!

Ella

And how did he know I was texting you?

Mom

I’d left my phone in the cupholder.

Mom

And he heard it go off when he was driving.

Mom

That’s when he started texting you, pretending to be me.

Mom

He wanted you to stay away from our living room window…

Mom

So you wouldn’t be a witness to what Cindy did.

Mom

Then he realized that Cindy thought I was home…

Mom

And that she was coming over to kill me too, right after she killed Jason!

Mom

He thought she might kill you by accident instead.

Mom

He couldn’t let that happen. He loves you too much.

Ella

Oh my god.

Mom

I know. I’m so glad you’re alright.

Ella

I’m so glad YOU’RE alright.

Ella

Please come home.

Mom

I’ll be leaving the police station soon.

There is a pause over the phone line.

Mom

Ella, there’s something else.

Ella

What is it?

Mom

God, it feels strange to even tell you this.

Mom

Before the police took your father away, he said…

Mom

Tell Ella I love her with all my heart.

Ella

Oh wow. He texted that to me today, Mom.

Ella

Except I thought it was YOU saying that.

Mom

You need to always remember something, Ella.

Mom

Both he and I love you so much. We always will.

Mom

And even though that man is evil to his core…

Mom

Even though he and Cindy will be in prison for life…

Mom

There will always be one good thing about him.

Mom

His love for you.
