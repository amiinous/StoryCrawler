Our Adventure - Episode 2

Martha hears her doorbell ring and jerks her head up from her phone.

Not long after, she hears her mom answer the door.

Mom

Hi Caroline! Great to see you, honey.

Mom

Martha’s in her room.

Martha’s heart starts hammering in her chest as she hears Caroline and her mother walk down the hall.

She knows that she should answer Juan’s text, but she doesn’t know how.

It’s definitely too early to meet in person, right?

As Martha’s fingers hover over the screen on her phone, she sees her bedroom door open.

Quickly, she throws the phone on her desk. It lands with a clatter.

Mom

Honey, are you okay?

Martha

I’m fine!

Martha’s mother studies her carefully.

Martha

Seriously Mom, I’m fine.

Martha turns her attention to Caroline, focusing on her intently.

While speaking slowly and deliberately, she hopes to send her friend a message with her eyes.

Martha

Caroline, want to take a walk?

Caroline rushes to answer.

Caroline

Of course! It’s so nice outside.

Caroline

Let’s go around the lake.

Mom

I don’t know, Martha. Are you sure you girls don’t want to stay in? I’ll make cookies.

Martha

We’re sure.

Mom

Okay, but take a blanket, all right? You know it’s cold out there.

Martha

Seriously, Mom?! We’ve gone on walks before. We’ll be fine.

Martha’s mother sighs.

Mom

I guess you’re right.

Once she leaves the room, Martha turns to Caroline, rolling her eyes.

Martha

Jeez. Is she ever going to treat me like an adult?

Caroline

Aww, she’s just worried about you.

Caroline

Here, let me move you. It really IS gorgeous out there by the lake.

Caroline slips her arm under Martha’s armpits and, in one fell swoop, puts her in her wheelchair.

At this point in their relationship, the process is second nature to Caroline.

Caroline

Comfy?

Martha

Yup. Oh, can you please grab that blanket for my lap?

Martha

We’d better keep my mom happy.

Caroline

Haha, sure. Oh! And we DEFINITELY need to take this too…

Caroline picks up Martha’s phone, dangling it between her thumb and pointer finger.

Caroline

I saw you throw it on your desk. What are you hiding…?

Martha

Give me that!

Caroline shakes her head. A sly smile spreads across her face as she scrolls through the phone.

Martha

Oh my God, Caroline! You’re the worst, haha. You realize that you’re legit taking advantage of a cripple, right?

Caroline

I see it as legit HELPING a cripple. I knew you’d text him!

Martha

Shh! I don’t want my mom to hear! She doesn’t know I’m on the app.

Martha

Come on. Let’s go outside.

Caroline

K. But only if you promise to spill — everything.

Martha

Obviously. Now hurry!

Martha starts wheeling herself away and Caroline races to keep up behind her.

They call out a goodbye to Martha’s mother as they leave the house…

But they don’t say another word to each other until they’re down by the lake.

Then the words spill out of Martha’s mouth.

Martha

He wants to meet!

Caroline

What?!

Caroline

Rewind. Didn’t you text him for the first time today?

Martha

Yeah. But Caroline…we had SUCH a good convo.

Caroline

What’d you guys talk about? Did you tell him…?

Martha rolls her eyes.

Martha

Come on, Caroline. You of all people should be able to say the word ‘paralyzed.’

Caroline

Haha, fair. Did you tell him you’re paralyzed?

Martha

I DID.

Caroline

OMG! What’d he say?

Martha

He was completely cool with it. Like, it seemed like it wasn’t a big deal to him.

Martha

He even asked me questions about it.

Martha

It was…normal.

Caroline

Wow, he seems…

Martha

Great, right?

Caroline

I don’t know. I was going to say ‘too good to be true.’

Martha

Um, that’s kinda mean.

Caroline

No. I don’t mean to be. You know you’re my best friend.

Caroline

To me, you’re Martha, and it doesn’t matter that you’re in a chair…

Caroline

But I also saw what happened with Justin.

Caroline

He didn’t think the accident was gonna change anything, but…

Caroline

He hurt you, Martha. He didn’t realize…

Martha

He didn’t realize WHAT, exactly?

Caroline shrugs.

Caroline

Look, I just want you to be careful, okay?

Caroline

I don’t want to see you get hurt again.

Martha

God, what’s wrong with everyone?

Martha

You know what?

Martha

I’m so over being careful.

Martha takes out her phone, and with a few angry clicks, opens the dating app on her phone.

Caroline

Martha, please. Don’t do something just because you’re mad at me.

Martha

God, this is so not about you, Caroline.

Martha

This is about me, finally connecting with a guy after a shitty couple of years.

Martha

It’s about me, finally going on a date after…forever.

Martha

So warn me all you want. But I’m texting him.

Martha types out a message on her phone, reads it once, and nodding, clicks send.

Martha

That’d be awesome, Juan. When should we meet?

Juan

Is tomorrow too soon?
