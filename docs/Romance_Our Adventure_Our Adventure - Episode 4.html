Our Adventure - Episode 4

Martha

I…

Martha sits — frozen — staring at Juan’s outstretched hand.

Does she trust him?

How the HELL does she know?

But Caroline’s words keep sounding in her mind, in sync with her heartbeat.

You deserve to be in a happy relationship.

You’ve gotta put yourself out there.

Martha sighs.

Martha

Okay.

Juan

Okay??!

Beaming, Juan jumps up from the table.

Martha instantly returns his smile because his joy is infectious —

It’s like Juan has transformed into a labradoodle puppy right before her eyes.

And Martha’s never been able to say no to a labradoodle puppy.

The words race out of Juan’s mouth.

Juan

We don’t have to go very far — just around the corner to the Sports Piers.

Juan

So do I, like, wheel you there?

Juan

I’m not sure what to do but I can—

Martha

Juan, deep breath. I wheel myself around all the time.

Juan

Haha, okay. I just can’t wait to show you this place!

Martha

Okay, but not so fast. First, we need to tell Caroline we’re going somewhere else.

Juan

Caroline’s here? I get to meet her?!

Martha

Yes, of course she’s here. What if you had been a murderer?

Juan

Haha, how could you think I’d be a murderer?

Martha

Well, you DID agree to go out with a cripple.

Juan’s excited energy instantly disappears and his face goes solemn.

Juan

I never called you—

Martha

Hahaha. Juan, again, deep breath.

Martha

You’ll learn that I like to make jokes about my disability. It helps me cope.

Juan takes a step closer, and then another.

He keeps his eyes glued on Martha’s, and they’re so impossibly brown it makes her heart swell.

He places both of his strong hands on the arms of her wheelchair to stabilize himself...

And slowly…he leans his head down, so his lips are inches from hers.

Martha’s heartbeat quickens.

Juan

I’ll learn that, will I? How—

WHAM!

A blur of color comes catapulting toward Juan.

In an instant, thin pale arms with knobby elbows are flailing and jabbing at one hundred miles a minute.

Caroline

Get away from her, you creep!

Caroline

The woman is paralyzed and I will NOT let you take advantage of her.

Caroline

Sir, do NOT take her into the woods! Somebody help! HELP!!

Martha

Caroline. Caroline! Chill.

Martha

It’s cool. Juan is…cool.

Caroline stops.

Caroline

Martha, you’re okay?

Martha blushes furiously.

Caroline

Oh my God! Did I just interrupt—

Caroline

Were you going to—

Juan

Hi Caroline. I’ve heard a lot about you. I’m Juan.

Juan holds out his hand and Caroline gapes down at it, completely stunned.

Caroline

I — I just — I was looking out for my friend…

Juan

I know. That’s a good thing.

Juan

And to answer your other question — yes. I WAS just about to do this.

And with the grace of a bird soaring through the sky, Juan turns back to Martha…

And gently kisses her on the lips.

His mouth is soft, and when he opens it against hers, she can taste his soul.

He ends the kiss, and Martha finds herself gasping for air.

Then Juan claps his hands together once, freeing them from the spell.

Juan

Now! You said you wanted to run something by Caroline?

Martha recovers her breath but there is a telltale flush on her face.

Martha

Oh! Yeah. Caroline, we’re going down the road to the Sports Piers. I didn’t want you to think I’d been abducted.

Caroline

Oh, cool. Are you okay on your own? Want me to come with?

Martha gives Juan a sidelong glance and a coy smile.

Martha

Nah, I think we should be good.

Martha

He doesn’t seem like a murderer…most of the time.

Juan

Haha, you certainly weren’t calling me a murderer when my lips were against yours.

Martha

Hence why I said ‘most of the time.’

Caroline

You guys! You’re so cute it makes me want to gag.

Juan

Haha, thanks?

Caroline

Yes, that was definitely a compliment. All right, now get going.

Caroline

Have your adventure.

The three say their goodbyes and Caroline settles back into her table at the coffee shop, enveloping herself in her book.

She doesn’t even look up as the couple heads out the door.

Once they’re on the sidewalk, Martha clears her throat to quiet her hammering heart.

Martha

So…what are we going to do at the Sports Piers?

Juan

Oh man, I kinda wanted it to be a surprise — but I’m too excited. I have to tell you.

Juan

I made an appointment at the rock climbing wall.

Martha stops wheeling.
