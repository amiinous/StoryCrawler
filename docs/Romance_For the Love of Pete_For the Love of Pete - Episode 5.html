For the Love of Pete - Episode 5

The next afternoon, Hayley is sitting in her office with Pete in her lap, looking out the window.

Hayley

I’m not sure how I got here, Pete.

Hayley

Should I have let Travis go?

Hayley

I’ll still get to see him because of you, thank God.

Hayley

But I’m afraid I’m always going to want more.

Hayley

A lot more.

There’s a knock at the door.

She opens it and a delivery man enters the office.

He hands her a gold box.

She opens it and finds fifty long-stemmed roses.

Just then, Travis steps into Hayley’s office.

Pete jumps out of her lap to greet him.

Hayley

Come in and shut the door.

Travis follows her inside.

Hayley

Thank you for the roses.

Travis

Perfect timing.

Hayley

How was your father when you left?

Travis

He’s much better. The doctor expects a full recovery.

Hayley

Oh I’m so glad, Travis.

Travis

Me too.

Travis

Are you free for dinner?

Hayley

As a matter of fact I am.

Travis

Good, and I was thinking…

Travis

Maybe we could go to your place and watch Aliens on Netflix afterward.

Hayley

Sounds like a plan.

Travis

Listen, before we go eat, I wanted to say something.

Hayley

Yes?

Travis

I know we both have our reasons for wanting to keep our distance…

Travis

But you’re the first person in a long time who I’ve wanted to get closer to.

Hayley

I feel the same way.

He steps closer to her and takes her in his arms.

Travis

I propose a new arrangement.

Hayley

What is it?

Travis

We both keep Pete permanently and we spend the weekends together.

Hayley

And any other time you’re in the city?

Travis

Yes.

Travis

And we forget my family.

Hayley

You have a deal.

He smiles and leans down to seal the deal with a kiss.

His kiss is slow, deliberate, and filled with the promise of more to come.

At their feet, Pete barks and wags his stump of a tail.
