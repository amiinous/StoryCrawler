Out of My League - Episode 2

Maria

Wow.

Auric

Wow yourself.

Auric holds out his arm for a long minute before Maria registers

that this handsome man in a freshly pressed tux is waiting for her.

She wraps her arm around his, and the pair walks toward his Audi R8.

Maria

You’re driving us in that?

Auric

Yeah, but don’t be worried. I actually…

Maria

What?

Auric

I don’t drink.

Auric

Is that okay?

Maria

Of course it’s okay.

Auric opens the door on the passenger side,

And waits for Maria to get in.

Then he races around the other side, and once he’s behind the wheel,

He lets out of a big sigh of relief.

Auric

I’m so glad you’re cool with the no drinking thing.

Maria

Why wouldn’t I be?

Auric

I don’t know—it’s college. A lot of people want to party and get drunk.

Maria

I mean, I DO like to party and drink…

Maria

But that’s not the only thing I like to do.

Maria

And it’s your choice what you do.

Auric

Yeah, it’s just—tennis is the thing that makes me special.

Auric

If I don’t condition my body to play the best tennis it can…

Auric

I’m throwing away the biggest gift I was given.

Maria

Wow, that’s really cool that you consider tennis a gift.

Auric

What else would it be?

Maria

Well, you’ve probably worked pretty hard to play at your level.

Maria

I think some people would be a little egotistical about that achievement.

Auric

I mean, yeah, I worked hard.

Auric

But I was given a huge leg up by being born with my genes.

Auric

And we all work hard at what we do.

Auric

Look at you—you’re one of the best public speakers in the class.

Maria

Well, that’s just because I come from a big family.

Maria

You have to be a good speaker to survive in it.

Maria

But you’re right…I do take all the assignments seriously.

Auric

So see? Same thing.

Auric

You were given a natural gift, but then you worked hard to make it great.

Auric

Oh, here we are.

Maria

La Soirée des Étoiles?

Maria

Are you kidding me?

Maria

It’s impossible to get a reservation here.

Auric

It’s no big deal—I know the owner.

Auric pulls the car up the valet and meets Maria at the entrance.

When she starts walking toward the main dining room,

Auric grabs her hand, leans in close, and whispers in her ear.

Auric

Not there.

Auric

This way.

He takes her up a secret staircase in the corner,

That leads them all the way up to the roof.

There’s nothing but stars for miles—and a single table set for two.

Auric moves two steps closer to Maria as she takes in her surroundings.

Auric

God, you look—

He captures her mouth with his

Before he says another word.

The feeling is exhilarating—

The hot passion of the kiss in stark contrast with the cool night,

Making Maria understand the romance of a rooftop…

And it sends her into a panic.

Abruptly, she breaks off the kiss.

Maria

I can’t.

Maria

I-I need to go home.

Auric

What?

Auric

We should have dinner first.

Maria

No, I just remembered…

Maria

I have to go.

Auric

Can I at least drive you home?

Maria

No, no. I’ll take a cab.

Maria

Um…bye.

Maria races down the stairs, two at a time, and flies out the door.

Down the street a bit, she hails a cab,

As the car pulls out with her inside, she sees Auric behind her—

Standing on the curb with his hands hanging at his sides in defeat.

Two minutes later, her phone buzzes.

Auric

Maria, I’m sorry.

Auric

I didn’t mean to kiss you if you weren’t ready.

Maria

No, that’s not it.

Maria

I wanted you to kiss me.

Maria

But the rooftop, the dinner, this dress…

Maria

It’s kind of a lot.

Maria

And I’m not sure it’s me.

Auric

Yeah, I guess it was a little over the top.

Auric

But I picked that rooftop because it was under the stars.

Auric

I remembered that story you told in class…

Auric

About how your family would go camping under the night sky.

Auric

So I thought you’d like it.

Maria

Oh, Auric. I did. I REALLY liked it.

Maria

And that was the problem.

Auric

What?

Maria

Look, I’m just a regular girl who likes camping in the woods

Maria

I wear yoga pants, even when I’m not working out.

Maria

I can’t be like Kristin Kelly.

Maria

I’m not the kind of girl who gets dressed up for dates…

Maria

Or has sex on rooftops.

Auric

Maria.

Auric

I’m not that guy either.

Auric

That rooftop thing with Kristin…

Auric

It was a rumor.

Auric

And ugh, SHE’S the one who made it up.

Auric

That’s when I realized I couldn’t be with her anymore.

Maria

Really?

Auric

Yeah. I can’t be with someone who’s over-the-top like that.

Auric

I need a regular girl.

Auric

A girl who’s more like me.

Maria

But I don’t think I AM like you, Auric.

Auric

You are. And if anything, you’re better.

Auric

Maria, you’re a good person from a good family,

Auric

You didn’t judge me for not drinking,

Auric

You’re naturally talented, and you work hard at what you do.

Auric

Please—I want to see you. Where are you right now?

Maria

I just got to the parking lot of Winters.

Auric

Okay, stay there.

Five minutes later, Auric pulls into the lot and parks his car.

He gets out, and as he strides over to Maria,

She feels her body hum. She wants him.

Or at least, she wants another mind-numbing kiss.

When she finally speaks, her voice comes out soft and shy.

Maria

Hi.

Auric

Hey there—so can we do this? Please?

Auric

Can we take this slow?

Maria

Yes. I think we can.

And this time, it’s Maria who reaches out

And grabs Auric by the collar of his shirt.

She pulls him down to her and kisses him,

Pouring her soul through his mouth and into his very being.

She’s ready to build a constant, steady foundation…

Strong enough to last the rest of their lives.
