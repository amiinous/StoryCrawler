Off Limits - Episode 5

Jacey is walking into The Drake Hotel to meet Kim and Kim’s mother, Alice, for brunch.

She is five minutes late.

She repeats a single sentence in her head as she passes through the revolving door:

I’m going to tell her today about Ty.

I’m going to tell her today about Ty.

She approaches the table where Kim and Alice are already sitting.

JACEY

Hi! Sorry I’m late.

KIM

We ordered you a mimosa.

ALICE

I brought brochures. I’m thinking for our winter getaway: Spain!

KIM

Hell yeah. I’m in.

JACEY

Spain? What does Spain cost?

ALICE

Now don’t even start. Dad and I are going to foot the bill. Per usual.

KIM

She’s not worried about the money.

KIM

After all, she was promoted after the plaid-satin-hipster-panty launch.

KIM

There’s a different reason she doesn’t want to go.

KIM

She’s thinking that Spain is awfully far away from her new boyfriend.

ALICE

Boyfriend?

Jacey turns to Kim.

JACEY

Can we talk about this after brunch?

KIM

I know you met him yesterday.

JACEY

How do you know that?

KIM

Um…hello?

KIM

I KNOW you, all right?

KIM

When you don’t reply to texts for hours, you’re busy.

KIM

And when a girl’s busy on a Saturday night…

ALICE

What’s his name?

KIM

Where’d he take you? The beach?

ALICE

You ARE sporting a tan, young lady.

JACEY

I was outside all day.

KIM

With him? Doing what?

JACEY

Tanning, apparently.

ALICE

Well, do tell.

KIM

Here’s what we know so far. His code name is Hercules.

ALICE

Impressive.

KIM

Every time he contacts her, she gets this silly smile on her face.

JACEY

I do not.

KIM

You try to hide it.

ALICE

When do Dad and I get to meet him?

JACEY

I don’t know.

KIM

She said she broke up with him.

JACEY

Yesterday we sort of…didn’t break up.

ALICE

How long have you been together?

JACEY

Not long.

ALICE

How long have known him?

JACEY

A few months. Six, give or take.

KIM

Where’d you meet him?

JACEY

Um…at a bar.

ALICE

You’re right, Kimmy. She’s blushing. Must be Hercules.

ALICE

Maybe this year, the two of you should bring dates on our trip.

JACEY

Hypothetically, if bringing guys we’ve only just begun to date on a winter getaway is actually an option—

JACEY

Which it isn’t—

JACEY

You could bring Russ.

Kim rolls her eyes.

KIM

I’d be better off finding a date in Barcelona, thank you very much.

ALICE

Oh, no. Not going so well with Russ?

ALICE

A shame. He does quite well for himself.

JACEY

And he’s a good-looking guy.

KIM

He’s a metrosexual for sure. But I prefer rugged.

KIM

Besides, he’s so regimented and has zero sense of humor.

KIM

I need to laugh, you know?

ALICE

And you have your own money, and the power to earn more.

ALICE

No need to rely on a man for that.

KIM

That’s right. From now on, I’m looking for funny and ruggedly handsome.

ALICE

Well, it’s months away. We have time to decide.

ALICE

By then, you could be two boyfriends past this Russ.

KIM

Actually…I called someone yesterday.

KIM

An ex. Do you remember Ty?

JACEY

You called him?

ALICE

I can’t keep up. Who’s Ty?

KIM

He looks like a lumberjack. Works in marketing.

ALICE

Oh, right! I liked him.

KIM

I just think lately…I broke it off with him partly because I thought I had feelings for someone else, and it wasn’t fair to him.

KIM

But it never panned out with that other guy.

KIM

Ty and I were both preoccupied with other things at the time.

KIM

I’m thinking I’ll feel him out.

KIM

See if he might be interested in trying again.

ALICE

Do it. He’d be crazy not to give you a second chance.

ALICE

Have you met this guy, Jacey?

JACEY

Yeah.

Jacey swallows hard.

JACEY

Yeah, I have. Ty’s a great guy.

KIM

Oh give me a break.

JACEY

What? I like Ty.

KIM

Think about it. When we were dating, you rarely tagged along.

KIM

And when you did and he’d try to talk to you, you wouldn’t engage.

KIM

Let’s face it. You were less than supportive of me reaching out to him again.

JACEY

I think Ty is great. It’s just that—

ALICE

Just think of it! Kim and Ty. Jacey and her Hercules. In Spain.

JACEY

We’re getting ahead of ourselves.

ALICE

What man wouldn’t want to take a chance on a beautiful girl with all expenses paid to Spain?

KIM

I’ll text Ty.

JACEY

You’re going to invite him on vacation over text?

KIM

Why not?

JACEY

Because you broke up.

JACEY

And you have a boyfriend.

KIM

Probably not for long.

KIM

I’m just putting some feelers out there.

ALICE

Jacey, tell us more about Hercules.

JACEY

I think it’s just about over.

KIM

Not if you saw him yesterday.

JACEY

There’s someone in the way.

KIM

On your side or his?

JACEY

It’s complicated.

KIM

No girl could possibly be more important than you.

JACEY

I think this girl is pretty important, actually.

KIM

Let me meet him.

KIM

I’ll help him see what a catch you are.

KIM

And what a loser this other girl is, compared to you.

JACEY

I’m not sure you could do that.

KIM

Are you kidding? I’m your biggest fan.

KIM

And if this guy doesn’t choose you, he’s probably not worth it anyway.
