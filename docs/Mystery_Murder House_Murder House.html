Ajay?

EVERY NIGHT, SOMEONE DIFFERENT WHILE I’M AT HOME WITH THE KID! I CAN SMELL THEM ON YOU WHEN YOU GET HOME!

?

IF YOU WERE ME! IF ONLY WE COULD TRADE PLACES. WHAT I WOULDN’T GIVE TO HAVE SOMETHING ON MY MIND BESIDES THIS FAMILY FOR ONCE.

HENRY, GO BACK TO BED. WE’RE JUST HAVING A LITTLE TALK HERE IS ALL.

SON, LISTEN TO YOUR MOTHER. THERE, MARIANNE, I PUT DOWN THE GUN, ARE YOU HAPPY?
