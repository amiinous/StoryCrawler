Blabbermouth - Episode 3

Beth

What the hell?!

Beth

You’ve abducted Carter too?!

Unknown

Look, I’m not the one whose doing something wrong here.

Unknown

In fact, I’m making the world a better place by teaching you a lesson.

Beth

But I learned my lesson -- please, just stop.

Beth

Let them go and I won’t talk about anyone ever again.

Unknown

Now now, Beth. How do I know you’re not lying?

Unknown

I could let them go, even after all my hard work…

Unknown

But then you can go right back to gossiping.

Unknown

I can’t have that, so I’ve decided going to keep them here for a month.

Unknown

Do you think you can keep quiet for that long?

Unknown

Because if you do, your friend and your precious sister will be set free.

But Beth hasn’t looked at her phone because she’s sprinting to Roy’s house.

The unknown caller may have tried to get her off his scent,

But she knows in her heart that it’s Roy.

She’s offended him too many times, and now he’s striking back.

Suddenly, Beth feels her cell phone vibrate again. She opens her messages and reads.

Unknown

Oh, Beth. Tsk tsk tsk.

Unknown

I just opened our friend Carter’s phone.

Beth

You can do that?

Unknown

You seriously shouldn’t underestimate the things I can do.

Unknown

Not after today.

Beth

I’m sorry -- I don’t.

Unknown

Shut up with your apologies!

Unknown

I can’t believe you had a plan to go to the police.

Unknown

Maybe you don’t understand that I’m honest about my threats.

Unknown

But I’m going to show you that I am.

Unknown

Take a look at this photo, Beth. And know that you caused it.

Beth

OMG that’s disgusting!

Beth

Is that Carter’s bloody hand? Did you cut it?

Beth

What the hell is wrong with you -- isn’t he supposed to be your friend??

But Beth doesn’t wait for an answer.

Instead, she approaches Roy’s house. She has to hurry to stop him.

She drops down to her stomach and slithers to the back door to his basement,

And all the while, she feels the vibrations of texts coming in.

Unknown

Nothing’s wrong with me.

Unknown

The real question you should be asking is, what is wrong with YOU, Beth?!

Unknown

Yes, that was Carter.

Unknown

Don’t you know how painful it was for me to do that to him?

Unknown

And yet you’re the bitch who put me in that position!

Beth takes a deep breath and sends the kidnapper a couple of texts to distract him.

Beth

You’re right. It’s not your fault. Let’s make it better. Together.

Beth

You already have -- because you taught me not to lie.

Beth

Now quick, can you wrap up Carter’s hand up with something?

As soon as she presses send, Beth jumps to her feet,

Cringing when a lose patio stone clinks underneath her sneakers.

She presses on the handle of the door and with a squeak, it slides open.

Roy never locks his basement.

Beth steps inside, scans the room, and checks her texts.

Unknown

I’m going to let Carter bleed for a while…

Unknown

To make him realize what a cancer you are.

Unknown

You did this to him, Beth.

Unknown

It’s about time he stopped taking your side.

Taking a breath, Beth starts to type another calming text.

But suddenly, a blur of color catapults into her peripheral vision,

And she gets tackled to the ground.

Beth thrusts forward, knocking her attacker back, but then he rolls back on top of her.

He sits back on his heels, and when he speaks, there’s confusion thickening his voice.

Roy

Beth? Why didn’t you come through the front door?

Beth

Because you have to let them go!

Beth

You can’t torture my sister -- and our FRIEND -- like this!

Roy

What are you talking about?

Beth

I know you’re mad at me for embarrassing you in sixth grade,

Beth

And that you blame me for what happened with Alice Parker,

Beth

But don’t take it out on Cindy and Carter.

Beth

Take it out on me.

Roy

Wait, you think I abducted your sister?

Beth

Yeah…

Beth

I mean, if it wasn’t you, how would you know about it anyway?

Roy

Because Carter told me, Beth.

Roy

When I was at his house, earlier today.

Beth

Oh. I guess that makes sense.

Roy

I’d never hurt Cindy, Beth. She’s like a sister to me.

Roy

But back up -- did you say Carter got ABDUCTED?

Beth

Yeah. And it’s worse -- the kidnapper sliced up Carter’s hand with a knife!

Roy

What?! That’s so gross.

Beth

I know. And it’s all my fault.

Roy scoots closer to Beth and puts his arm around her shoulder,

And speaks to her in a low, soothing voice.

Roy

It’s not your fault.

Beth

Sure it is. I’m such an asshole.

Beth

Who do I think I am, walking around telling people’s secrets?

Roy

Beth, listen to me.

Roy

I’ve never once gotten mad at you for telling a couple silly secrets.

Roy

I mean, that stuff that happened in sixth grade and a couple weeks ago with Alice Parker…

Roy

It won’t really bother me in the long term.

Roy

It was actually…funny.

Beth

That’s what I said!

Roy

Haha, see?

Roy

It’s definitely not a good reason to abduct your sister and Carter.

Beth

I know.

Beth

But it doesn’t stop me from being worried about them.

Roy

I’m worried too. So let’s fix this.

Roy

Carter said the guy was texting you?

Beth

Yeah, look…

Beth pulls out her phone and the two friends watch in horror as the texts roll in.

Unknown

Beth?

Unknown

Beth, are you ignoring me?

Unknown

Because if you are, there will be consequences…
