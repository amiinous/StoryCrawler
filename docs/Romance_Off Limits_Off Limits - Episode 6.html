Off Limits - Episode 6

It’s late in the evening after brunch.

Ty and Jacey are in Ty’s bed, naked.

The windows are open, and the sounds of the city rise up from the street below.

TY

I’m glad you came.

TY

Pun intended.

TY

We’re so good at this.

JACEY

We are, aren’t we?

JACEY

But I need to stay focused.

JACEY

Like I said, I came here to break up with you.

TY

If this is breaking up, let’s do it twice a day.

JACEY

I’m serious. Kim—

TY

I thought you decided to come clean with Kim.

JACEY

I was going to. At brunch with Alice.

TY

What happened?

JACEY

Kim can’t stop thinking about you, that’s what.

JACEY

We have no choice. We have to stop.

TY

There’s always a choice.

JACEY

And she’s choosing you.

TY

So? I’m choosing YOU.

JACEY

She has her heart set on taking you to Europe.

TY

What?

JACEY

Every year around Valentine’s Day, Kim’s parents take us on a winter getaway.

JACEY

This year, we’re going to Spain, and Alice suggested we bring dates.

JACEY

So Kim started talking about this guy I was dating — code name: Hercules.

TY

Hercules?

JACEY

That’s my name for you on my phone.

TY

I’m honored.

JACEY

And Alice thought it would be great if Kim could reconnect with you.

JACEY

She doesn’t know you and Hercules are the same person!

TY

Wow. I’m Hercules.

JACEY

You’re focusing on the wrong details.

JACEY

Kim’s sure now: she wants you back.

JACEY

She even said she texted and called you last night.

TY

She did, but I didn’t answer the text or take the call.

JACEY

Yesterday, at the winery, was amazing.

JACEY

But if you had been there, at brunch, you’d understand.

JACEY

They’re my FAMILY.

JACEY

If I have children someday, Kim’s going to be their only auntie.

TY

Not true. I have a sister.

Jacey rolls her eyes.

JACEY

Nice attempt at diverting.

TY

So…you’re just going to turn your back on this? On us?

Jacey starts getting dressed.

JACEY

We never should’ve started this in the first place.

TY

I would have gone with you to meet her.

TY

You didn’t have to tell her alone.

JACEY

God, you don’t get it.

JACEY

Every time I see you, I think: I’m not going to do this again.

JACEY

And every time, I end up in bed with you, regardless.

JACEY

I feel out of control when it comes to you.

TY

You aren’t alone anymore.

JACEY

If I’m not careful, I’ll be as alone as I’ve ever been.

TY

I’ll go with you.

TY

We’ll tell Kim we didn’t plan on anything happening between us.

TY

That it just sort of spontaneously combusted.

TY

That we would never deliberately hurt her.

TY

And honestly, I can’t imagine she’ll be hurt anyway.

TY

She’ll understand. She will.

JACEY

I can’t risk it. I’m done.

TY

Jesus, Jacey. Stand up for yourself. Stand up for us!

JACEY

There you go again. Assuming I’m a doormat.

TY

That is NOT what I’m saying.

JACEY

You know, I don’t like the way you think of me.

TY

Dammit, Jacey.

Ty runs his hands through his hair.

TY

What do you mean?

JACEY

You think I just lay down and let everyone tell me how it is.

JACEY

That I just let people walk all over me.

TY

I think you’re crazy!

JACEY

Thank you.

TY

But then again, I’M also crazy.

TY

About you.

TY

And we’ve got something good going, here.

TY

We’re the real deal.

JACEY

You can’t possibly know that already.

TY

Yes. I can.

JACEY

It’s been ten days.

JACEY

And they’ve been great, believe me, but—

TY

Try INCREDIBLE.

JACEY

Still.

TY

I’d been making love to you in my mind since the day I first saw you.

TY

So maybe you see this as ten days.

TY

But I see it as half a year of foreplay leading up to the sexiest scene to occur on a fire escape. Ever.

TY

I’ve never felt this way before.

TY

I’m not going anywhere, pretty lady.

JACEY

That’s what you say now.

JACEY

Eventually, you all leave.

Ty shoves a leg into his jeans.

TY

Thanks for the confidence.

TY

You know, I’m not just any old guy.

JACEY

No, you’re a guy my best friend used to date.

TY

Let’s just talk to her, all right?

JACEY

You don’t know her like I do. She won’t understand.

JACEY

She’ll be furious with me.

TY

If she’s furious with you for falling for me—

JACEY

I never should have fallen for you in the first place.

TY

—Then she’s not much of a friend.

JACEY

She shared her bedroom, her wardrobe…

JACEY

Hell, she shared her PARENTS with me when I had nothing!

TY

Then why wouldn’t she want you to be happy?

JACEY

You don’t understand.

JACEY

It’s about loyalty, and protecting our friendship.

JACEY

You’re just off limits, okay?

JACEY

Kim’s a good judge of character.

JACEY

I like everyone. I’m too trusting.

JACEY

And every time she’s told me to be on the lookout, I’ve dodged a bullet.

JACEY

This is over, Ty. We’re done.

TY

I will fight for this, Jacey. Believe me I will fight.

JACEY

Then you’ll only be beating yourself up.

TY

Listen to me.

Ty cups Jacey’s face in his hands.

TY

Tell me you’re not crazy about me, too.

Through tears, Jacey stares up at him, and kisses the palm of his hand.

JACEY

Ty.

She sighs.
