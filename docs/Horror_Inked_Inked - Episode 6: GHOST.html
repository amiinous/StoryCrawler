Inked - Episode 6: GHOST

Suddenly, the girl in black snaps her head to the right.

She looks at Brooke with empty, black eyes.

Spiders slowly crawl out of the hollow sockets.

The Girl

You can’t save him.

Her voice is scratchy—like nails on a chalkboard.

The Girl

He will get what he deserves—just like my father.

Brooke

Della?

Brooke

DELLA DOSSETT?

Della

You know me?

Brooke

Yes. And I know what Tate did to you.

Brooke

But Ethan did nothing—he’s innocent.

Brooke

Please don’t hurt him.

Della

No man is innocent.

Della

Not Theodore Tate…

Della

Not the men who executed me…

Della

Not THIS man, your precious little Ethan.

Her mouth curves into a smile, revealing her rotten, yellow teeth.

Brooke swallows, summoning the last bit of courage she has.

Brooke

Please, Della, you already got your revenge on Tate.

Brooke

Why are you doing this?

Brooke

What do you want?

Della

Facium ut mei memineris.

Brooke

You want to be remembered?

Della

Yes. They all forget Della.

Della

Even my own father forgot me!

Brooke

But Della—if you let Ethan go, he will remember you.

Brooke

I’ll remember you, too.

Della

No.

Della

If he dies, your whole town will remember me.

Brooke

It doesn’t work like that, Della.

Brooke

You killed Tate…

Brooke

You were hanged for witchcraft…

Brooke

And yet, the whole city of New York STILL forgot you.

Brooke

What makes you think this town will be any different?

Della hisses, her bones cracking as she inches forward toward the edge of the Falls.

Ethan inches forward, too.

Instinctively, Brooke cowers back, her whole body shaking.

Della

Everything is different now.

Della

News spreads like a disease in these times.

Della

You get information from a box with a screen.

Brooke

You mean a TV?

Della

Yes, and from messages that beep into on a tiny rectangle…

Della

That you hold in the palm of your hand.

Della

So after Ethan dies…

Della

They WILL know my name.

Brooke

Please! I will tell the whole town about you.

Brooke

Just let Ethan go!

Della

No! He is guilty.

Brooke

Of what?

Brooke

He hasn’t done anything wrong.

Brooke

You hate him just because he’s a man.

Brooke

But he’s not Theodore Tate.

Brooke

Ethan’s not your father.

Della

Silence!

Lightning strikes a nearby tree.

The oak cracks at the roots and tumbles into the depths of the Falls.

Brooke opens her mouth to scream, but her voice is suddenly gone.

She falls to the ground, sobbing, while Della cackles.

Della

He will die here.

Della

And then more men will die.

Della

ALL men will die.

Brooke

But why Ethan?

Della

He mocked me!

Brooke

He… what?

Della

He painted my symbol onto his body like it was PRETTY.

Della

Like it was nothing but a silly decoration.

Della

But it’s powerful.

Della

I am powerful!

Della

And I will teach him not to mock me!

Brooke

No, wait!

Della twists her head slowly to look into Brooke’s eyes.

Della

Yeeessss?

Brooke

Ethan… he put that tattoo… that symbol on his body…

Brooke

Because he thought it was cool.

Della

Cool?

Brooke

Beautiful. Amazing. He was awed by it.

Brooke

He is awed by you, Della.

Brooke

Do you know what you can do with a follower like that?

Brooke

How much MORE powerful you can become?

Della

Go on…

Brooke

He could put your diary in a museum.

Brooke

You could have your own exhibit.

Brooke

Millions of people could visit and learn all about you.

Brooke

Ethan’s father—

Della

ENOUGH!

Della

Nothing good can come from men!

Della

I won’t let you sway me.

Della creeps closer to the edge of the cliff once more, and Ethan follows suit.

Ethan

Down, down, down.

Ethan

Drown, drown, drown.

Brooke throws a rock across the Falls at Ethan, hoping to snap him out of Della’s trance.

The rock hits his temple and blood trickles down his cheek.

Ethan

Down, down, down.

Della

You fool.

Della

You can’t break the curse.

Della

This is his punishment.

Brooke

Let… him…

Brooke

Go…

Brooke struggles to speak.

Della

If I let him live…

Della

He would only betray you.

Della

He would hurt you.

Della

He would… forget you.

Brooke

Please…

Della peers over the edge of the Falls.

She cocks her head to look directly into Ethan’s eyes.

Della

Down, little boy.

Della

DROWN.

Ethan’s body goes stiff.

He falls, straight as a board, over the edge of the cliff.

Brooke

Ethan… no…

Brooke tries to scream, but collapses with the effort.

And when she looks up, Della Dossett is gone.

There is no one in sight.

A few minutes later, Brooke hears the shouts of her father and Dr. Owens.

But it’s too late.

Her world goes black, and she lays unconscious in the dirt.

That night, Della’s diary disappeared from the museum.

Even now, no one knows the whereabouts of her mysterious book of ancient symbols.

But, whoever finds it—

caveat lector.

Let the reader beware.
