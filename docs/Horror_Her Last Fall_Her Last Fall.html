Her Last Fall

Amber

Hey baby bro. How have you been? We haven’t talked in a while.

Luca

Good.

Amber

It sucks that I’m so far away for college because I heard you killed it at your last game a few weeks ago.

Luca

Yeah I guess.

Amber

Luca? What is it? You okay? You’re not still made at me for leaving for school are ya buddy?

Luca

No. Mom and Dad are fighting again.

Amber

Like yelling fighting?

Luca

Yeah. What other kind of fighting?

Amber

Nothing. They could be not talking to each other or something.

Luca

I heard like a squishy thud sound. A couple of times. Like on TV when people are fighting each other.

Amber

What do you think that was?

Luca

IDK I went to go peak and Dad saw me and said mom fell down the stairs.

Luca

It didn’t sound like when she did the last few times though.

Amber

I’m sorry buddy. Did you see mom? Was she ok?

Luca

Only a little. Dad was in front of her. He yelled at me for not staying in my room.

Luca

Mom didn’t say anything. She didn’t even move.

Amber

Okay well do that. Stay in your room and don’t do anything but talk to me.

Amber

Get out a book or something and if Dad comes in just pretend to read.

Luca

Why? That would be lying and I could get in trouble more.

Amber

I don’t want him to get mad at you anymore.

Amber

You just need to trust that as your big sister I love you and I won’t let anyone hurt you.

Luca

Who would want to hurt me Amber?

Amber

No one buddy. And no one will.

Luca

I think everything is okay now. Daddy’s friends’, Will and James are here now.

Amber

What is mom doing? Don’t get caught looking and don’t leave your room.

Luca

She’s taking a nap on the couch.

Amber

She never does that. Where is Dad?

Luca

He’s outside with his friends? What’s wrong?

Amber

Nothing but I need you to listen to me carefully...

Amber

quickly and quietly go to mom and hold her wrist and tell me if you feel something.

Luca

I know what a pulse is. Moms not dead. You’re acting strange.

Amber

Do it Luca! Then go back to your room alright.

Luca

Amber I’m scared. I didn’t feel one.

Amber

Check her breathing. See if you feel air coming from her nose.

Luca

Nothing! Nothing at all! What do I do? Dad is gonna think I did this. Is she going to be okay Amber?

Amber

Just stay calm for me sweetie. Go to your room and turn on the TV alright.

Amber

Stay calm. He is going to come in to check on you soon. Just keep watching TV

Luca

He came in just now. He didn’t say anything just looked around.

Amber

Good. You’re going to need to go to the train station.

Luca

Why? I don’t know how to ride the train. Dad is going to be mad at me Amber. You’re not even here.

Amber

Just listen to me Luca. I’m trying to keep you safe.

Luca

From what? Does someone want to kidnap me?

Amber

Yes! And you can’t tell Dad because they said so. So listening to me is how you don’t get kidnapped ok.

Luca

I’m going to be brave like a super hero Amber. I promise. I’m going to sneak out through the back.

Luca

I’ll text you when I get there.

Amber

Okay. Stay safe. When you get there get on the red line. It will say it is going to Doraville.

Amber

When you get there come out the front of the train station and take a right to keep going straight.

Amber

3 blocks down you will see a red brick building on the left with no signs. It’s a home for abuse victims.

Amber

Call me and I will explain everything to them.

Luca

You think mom was abused?

Amber

We will talk about it later. Just stay alert. I’ve got your back little man.

Luca

I’m on the train now. I don’t see anyone weird.

Luca

Amber!!! They’re here. They’re sitting right across from me.

Luca

Mom is leaning on one of them. She probably came to look for me.

Amber

My God! He is truly sick. Stay calm. Pretend like you don’t see them.

Luca

Should I move away? You don’t want me to help mom?

Amber

We will just trust me. On the next stop get off at the last minute and catch the next train going in the same direction.

Luca

They know it’s me. They’re pointing at me. Will just picked up mom’s hand and waved it at me.

Luca

Amber can you just come get me?

Luca

I can’t do this. Can’t we call the police?

Amber

We can, but not yet. You have to get to that place. They will help you to get to me.

Amber

I’m not really away at college I’m only 16 Luca.

Amber

Dad did the same thing to my real mom. I know how to help you. Just stay brave and trust me.

Luca

Alright. I’m on the next train. Why didn’t you tell me before? And what did he do?

Amber

You’re my little brother. I have to protect you. But we will talk about it later. Just do as I said. Doraville station.

Amber

Don’t forget.

Amber

Luca It’s been almost an hour. It doesn’t take this long. Let me know you’re okay.

Amber

Luca. Where are you? Are you in a bad service area or in one of those tunnels?

Amber

At least type something so I know you’re okay.

Luca

I’m sorry Amber.

Amber

It’s okay. I’m just relieved you’re alright.

Amber

Did you make it to the place I told you about?

Luca

Yeah. Dad was there.

Luca

I’m in the trunk now. He said he knows I had help and if it was you to tell you goodbye from me and he will see you soon.

Amber

Luca. Where are you? Kick out a tail light. Luca! Don’t give up.

Luca

I feel really light headed. He hit me before he put me in here. I’m bleeding a lot too.

Amber

You aren’t alone. I love you Luca. Just think about that. Don’t be afraid.

Luca

I know I’m not alone. Mom is back here too. She’s asleep again though.
