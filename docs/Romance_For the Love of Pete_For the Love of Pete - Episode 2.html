For the Love of Pete - Episode 2

The very next day, Travis gets a call from Hayley.

Hayley

So how’s little Chubs? I miss him.

Travis

It’s been one day, Hayley!

Hayley

I know, but I’m worried about him.

Hayley

Does he know your house? Does he seem nervous?

Travis

Jack’s summer home was down the road.

Travis

Pete’s been here often. He’s fine.

Travis

I have a cat. They’re good friends.

Hayley

You have a cat?

Hayley

What if your cat scratches Pete’s eye out?

Travis

Haha! She won’t.

He sends a photo to Hayley.

It’s Pete stretched out asleep beside an orange tabby on a navy blue sofa.

Hayley

Sweet. I didn’t know Pete liked cats.

Travis

Jack and I got them at around the same time.

Travis

Pete and Ripley grew up together.

Hayley

Did you happen to name your cat after Ripley from the Alien movies?

Travis

Sure did.

Hayley

Which one was your favorite?

Travis

Easy. Aliens. My favorite movie of all time.

Hayley

Really?! Mine too!

Hayley

The life and death countdown when she went back to find Newt.

Travis

When she stepped into the Power Loader and fought the alien on the Sulaco.

Hayley

Oh my gosh, yes! So badass.

Travis

We should watch it sometime.

Is he asking her out?

Should she accept?

Things don’t need to be more complicated than they already are with Travis.

Hayley

It’s probably not a good idea.

Hayley

It’s not a good time for me.

Travis

No problem. I understand.

Hayley

I came out of a bad break-up a few months ago.

Travis

I understand.

An awkward silence descends for a minute or two.

Hayley

Okay, well I’d better go and get some work done.

Travis

Yeah, me too.

Hayley puts down her phone and then picks it up again.

She opens her photo gallery and taps the picture of Travis and Pete at the park yesterday.

Truth be told, she’s looked at it a hundred times already.

Studied Travis’s rugged features and sapphire blue eyes.

She tries to put him out of her mind and get some work done.

Travis texts her two hours later.

Travis

Pete’s been sitting under a tree looking up into the branches for the last 20 minutes.

Hayley

Haha! A squirrel?

Travis

Yes. He chased it up there and hasn’t lost focus since.

Hayley

He just wants to play with it.

Travis

Yeah, he wants to play with everything.

Travis

Even the snakes.

Hayley

Wait. Snakes?

Travis

Small ones. Ripley keeps most of them away.

Hayley

Travis, you don’t think this is something you should have told me sooner?

Travis

No. Why?

Hayley

Seriously?

Hayley

Snakes?!!

Travis

They’re garter snakes, Hayley. Not venomous.

Hayley

He shouldn’t be in a place with snakes.

Travis

I’d never let him near one.

Hayley

How do I know that?

Hayley

I don’t know you. We spent a few hours together.

Hayley

And then I had to hand over my dog to you.

Travis

Because he’s my dog too.

Travis

I’m a responsible pet owner.

Travis

Ripley’s done just fine with me.

Travis

And Pete is never out of my sight.

Travis

He’s like my kid. I’m not an idiot to let him near a snake.

Hayley

I didn’t mean to insult you.

Hayley

It’s just that I love that little bat-dog, you know?

Travis

Yeah, I do.

Hayley

I know you do. I’m sorry 🙁

Hayley

I’d better go now.

A few minutes later, her phone buzzes.

It’s a picture of Pete sitting on his backside with his wrinkly head tilted upward.

His large dark eyes are filled with anticipation at the leaves overhead.

She texts Travis.

Hayley

Is he not the cutest thing you’ve ever seen?

Travis

He was.

Travis

Until yesterday.

Travis

And, hey, you didn’t insult me.

Travis

I’m a big boy. I can take your honest feelings.

Travis

In fact, I prefer them.

Hayley

That’s rare to hear these days.

Hayley

The last guy I was completely honest with…

Hayley

Told me to get the hell out of his life.

Hayley

And I was happy to do it.

Hayley

I was going anyway after I told him what I thought of him.

Travis

Ha! You didn’t hold back, I bet.

Hayley

It was the truth. He couldn’t handle it.

Travis

How long were you with him?

Hayley

Almost a year.

Travis

Why’d you leave?

Hayley

He cheated.

Travis

Wow, what a loser.

Travis

That’s rough.

Hayley

It was hard, but I got through it 🙂

Travis

I like that you’re no-nonsense and still…

Travis

Compassionate and sweet.

Travis

Those are rare qualities.

Hayley

Sweet? I’ve heard that before.

Hayley

Hasn’t worked out well for me so far.

Travis

It’s never too late.

Travis

You just haven’t found the guy who will appreciate it yet.

Hayley

Maybe.

Hayley

What about you? Why are you still single?

Travis

Hmm, I think it’s my lifestyle.

Travis

I go to some of my charity events around the city…

Hayley

You run a charity organization?

Travis

Yes, I started it a few years back.

Travis

We feed and shelter the homeless who have pets.

Travis

Many shelters don’t allow pets. We’re one of the few that does.

Hayley

Wow, that’s really amazing.

Travis

Yeah, sometimes the events get pretty fancy.

Travis

Some women believe I live like that all the time.

Travis

But I don’t.

Travis

The last two I got involved with dumped me after a few months.

Travis

Apparently they didn’t like the quiet life of Connecticut.

Travis

City women 🙂

Hayley

Not ALL city women.

Travis

No?

Hayley

I wouldn’t mind a slower-paced life if I was financially stable.

Hayley

Who wants to raise kids in the city?

Travis

Yeah.

Travis

Hey, I need to go do some jobs and then I’m having some friends over for dinner.

Travis

I might not be able to keep you updated on Pete.

Hayley

Oh, no problem. Have fun with your friends 🙂

Travis

Hayley?

Hayley

Yes?

Travis

I wouldn’t mind driving into the city tomorrow…

Travis

So you can hang out with Pete.

Hayley

I’d like that.

Travis

Great. Meet you at the dog park at three.
