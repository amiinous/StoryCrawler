Streetrat - Episode 9

Meanwhile, at his company’s headquarters, Milton sits behind his desk, phone ringing, papers overflowing.

He’s clearly overwhelmed.

He picks up his buzzing phone.

Milton

No, no Marsha. No. I’ll deal with it when I’m back.

Milton

I have a plane to catch in three hours. I can’t right now.

Suddenly, Alex bursts into the room.

Alex

Milton, I need your help.

Milton

What are YOU doing here?

Alex

Milton, this is time sensitive. I can’t tell you what it is right now, but I need you.

Milton

That’s awfully bold for someone who just quit, don’t you think?

Alex

That wasn’t me!

Milton

Kid, I don’t know what this is about, but I have a flight to catch.

Milton grabs his suitcase and prepares to leave.

At the same time, at Mohammed Investments…

The goons drag Jazz and her father, tied and bound, into a place that was once theirs.

Jack

Throw them in storage and lock the door!

Jack

We’ll keep you close in case we need anything.

Jack

And then…

Jack

In the meantime, there’s redecorating to be done!

Jack

This way, boys!

The goons throw Jazz and Ezra into a small storage closet and flip the lock.

Shortly afterward, workers enter with tools of all variations.

Jack

Let’s start with that hideous sign.

Jack points to the huge lettering above the reception desk that reads MOHAMMED.

Jack

I think it needs a bit of a facelift, wouldn’t you agree?

The men begin chipping away at the lettering, slowly and painfully starting to dismantle everything that Ezra built.

Jack holds up new lettering: PROUST—his last name.

Jack

Much better.

Back at MH Group, Alex still pleads with Milton.

Milton

I don’t have time to mess around, kid.

Milton

I’m trying to run a business here—not play games.

Milton heads for the door.

Alex

The favors!

Milton stops.

Milton

What?

Alex

The favors—I saved your life. You promised me three favors.

Milton

I think we’ve cashed in on those.

Alex

No—I only cashed in on one. You brought me to the gala.

Alex

I still have two favors.

Milton

Later, kid.

Alex

Are you saying you’re not a man of your word?

Alex

Give me one more favor, and the last favor will benefit you.

Milton is intrigued...

Meanwhile, Jack and Eduardo are continuing to plot the future of Proust Enterprises.

Eduardo

Where’s my office, boss?

Jack

As a thank you for a job well done, you can take my old office.

Jack

Now to find my new home.

Jack heads straight for Ezra’s corner office.

Piece by piece, he begins taking it apart.

Picture frames in the garbage cans, letterhead in the shredder, name plates torn down.

All to be replaced with his own belongings.

Suddenly, Alex appears in the doorway.

Alex

You don’t waste much time, do you?

Jack’s face darkens.

Jack

You stupid boy—what are you doing here?

Alex

Just came to check out the new digs. Making yourself at home, I see.

Jack

You want to know the sad thing?

Jack

You could have been a part of all of this.

Jack

If you hadn’t been such a fool, I might have made you my new assistant.

Jack

But instead, you’ll join the rest of them.

Jack

Wanted to be with your love, did you? Let me arrange that.

Jack

Guards!

Jack’s goons enter—ready for battle.

Jack

Lock him up with the others!

Alex

You know what’s really a shame?

Alex

You’re not half the businessman that Ezra and Milton are.

Jack

That’s rich, coming from the orphaned streetrat.

Alex

No, it’s true.

Alex

You have no business sense at all, really.

Jack’s goons close in on Alex, who dodges them.

Alex

If you did, you wouldn’t just take over an existing company.

Alex

And you wouldn’t just try to acquire a Holt’s business to do it.

Alex

You’d start your own company, if you were a true businessman.

Alex watches Jack carefully as his words wash over him.

Jack is quiet for a moment, stone faced.

And then, a small, wicked grin appears.

Jack

Not a businessman, huh?

Jack

I’ll show you. I’m not just going to acquire the Milton Holt Group.

Jack

I’m going to beat Ezra Mohammed and Milton Holt at their own game.

Jack

With the information you so generously provided on your laptop—

Jack

Thank you, by the way—

Jack

With the help of Ezra’s files, I’m going to create the most powerful investment and venture capitalist firm in the world!

Jack

Get me Ezra, NOW! I’ll show you, streetrat.
