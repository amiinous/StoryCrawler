The Intruder - Episode 4

Dad

Eddie?

Dad

Eddie, are you there?

Eddie

Hey, I’m just waking up. I was so out of it.

Dad

I’m sorry to wake you, but I just wanted to make sure that I got you early enough.

Dad

Are you still in the cabin?

Eddie

Yes. I had soup and slept, just like you told me to do.

Dad

Good. You should be at full strength now.

Dad

The good thing is, the game probably recognizes your phone as part of you, so your phone should be fully charged now, too.

Eddie

Yeah, it is! That’s amazing!

Dad

Okay, just be careful now.

Dad

You can only recharge at the designated stations. You don’t want to run out of battery. You’ll need it.

Eddie

Dad, I don’t get how I could be in a game.

Eddie

And even more, I don’t understand how you could be a video game character.

Dad

The game has glitches. I don’t know how I got out, but I did.

Dad

But now it’s trying to fix itself by getting me back.

Dad

Only it got you instead. It’s angry that I left.

Eddie

Dad, you got out by playing it all the time. I haven’t. I don’t know what I’m doing.

Dad

Don’t worry. That’s why I’m helping you. You’re going to be okay.

Dad

Now listen to me. We don’t have much time.

Dad

In a few minutes, an old wizard is going to come barging through that door.

Eddie

A WIZARD?

Eddie

Are you kidding me?? Like for real, a wizard? I have to deal with a wizard?

Eddie

Can he get me home? Like do some magic or something?

Dad

Yes and no.

Eddie

What does that mean?

Dad

It means that he’s going to try to help you, but his powers aren’t very good at first.

Dad

You’re going to help him with that.

Eddie

I’m going to help him??

Eddie

I have no idea what I’m doing! How can I HELP anybody?

Dad

He just needs confidence. That’s what you’re going to help him with.

Dad

You help him get his confidence back, and he’ll help you get home.

Dad

It happens every time. The only thing is, at first he’s not going to love that you broke into his home.

Eddie

WHAT?? But YOU told me to break in!

Dad

Will you please relax? You’re supposed to break in. It’s part of the game.

Dad

You’ll explain to him who you are. It’ll be okay. His name is Norville.

Eddie

Norville?

Dad

And he’s not a very good wizard.

Dad

But, he will be. You’ll help him become one.

Dad

Norville IS an excellent fighter. He’ll quickly train you and give you some pointers.

Eddie

Can’t he fight all our enemies? I’ll just tag along.

Dad

No, the only way for you to get back is to defeat the GAME.

Dad

And YOU will have to fight trolls, orcs and mercenaries.

Dad

You can’t rely on someone else to get you to the end. You’re going to have to know how to fight.

Eddie

I’m going to have to kill more trolls?

Eddie

Because the last time was disgusting.

Dad

You’re going to have to kill all three things. And then the only way to get the last amulet to get home is by defeating the Leader.

Eddie

That’s what he’s called? The Leader?

Dad

Yes. The only ones in the game with names are Norville, the Leader and Mankill.

Dad

Mankill is the mercenary who showed up at our house.

Dad

And me. And now you. In the game, I was called Dirk Dogood.

Eddie

What? LOL!! Seriously? Dirk Dogood? So where did you get the name Drew?

Dad

I obviously couldn’t go through life with the name Dirk Dogood, so I changed it.

Dad

Our last name, Vinorell, is an anagram of Norville. I named us after him.

Eddie

Really?? You named us after a video game character?

Dad

He was my best friend. Without his help, I wouldn’t have survived.

Dad

Without his help, you won’t either.

Eddie

Dad, there’s somebody at the door! I hear footsteps. Is that him?

Dad

Yes, that’s probably Norville.

Eddie

What should I do?

Dad

It doesn’t really matter what you do. He’s going to get in any moment.

Eddie

So should I just open the door?

Dad

No, it’s probably better to let him come in.

Dad

The thing is, he’s going to agree to help you, but only because he feels bad about what he did.

Eddie

What do you mean? He didn’t do anything yet.

Dad

No, but he will. Eddie, I’m really sorry about this, but it needs to happen in order for the game to go on.

Eddie

What needs to happen? What are you talking about?

Dad

By now, Norville should be in the house.

Eddie

How do you know? I don’t see him anywhere.

Dad

No, you wouldn’t. He probably heard you inside and shifted into some small creature.

Dad

A mouse, maybe an insect. Doesn’t matter.

Dad

The point is, he’s probably inside with you already.

Dad

And he’s not going to be happy to find an intruder in his house.

Eddie

Wait, so he’s in here with me now?

Dad

Yes.

Eddie

I’m calling him.

Dad

No, don’t! He’ll be more suspicious if he realizes that you know his name!

Eddie

Too late. I called his name. He didn’t answer.

Dad

I wish you hadn’t done that.

Dad

It’s just going to make him angrier and want to hurt you more.

Dad

Too late now. I’m very sorry that you’re going to have to go through this.

Eddie

What? Go through what? You haven’t told me anything. You’re making me nervous!

Dad

Eddie, when you get up, in twenty minutes, text me.

Eddie

When I get up?? What do you mean??

Dad

You’re going to see in about twenty seconds. And it’s going to hurt.

Eddie

Dad! WHAT IS HE GOING TO DO??

Dad

If my calculation is right, it should be happening right around now.

Dad

I’m sorry, Eddie. Text me when you get up.

Dad

Oh, and say hello to Norville for me.
