Ensnare - Episode 7

Angie

What the HELL have you gotten me into?

Kyle

What’s wrong? Are you okay?

Angie

Your secret society just threatened to KILL me.

Kyle

What did they say exactly?

Angie

They said I have to get an abortion. If I don’t, they’ll kill me to protect *your* reputation.

Kyle

So get the abortion.

Angie

What?

Kyle

You don’t really want to keep the thing, do you?

Angie

I don’t know…maybe.

Kyle

You can’t be serious, Ang.

Kyle

Is this some kind of a religious thing?

Angie

It’s not a religious thing.

Angie

It’s a “being responsible” thing.

Angie

Seriously. I’m not just going to abort the baby that’s growing inside me without thinking it through.

Angie

I’m certainly not going to let someone else decide what will happen to my body—

Angie

And my baby.

Kyle

You’re making way too big a deal out of this.

Kyle

It isn’t even a baby yet.

Kyle

It’s a group of cells that shouldn’t be there.

Kyle

Like a tumor.

Kyle

You’d cut a tumor out, wouldn’t you?

Angie

That’s really how you think of it?

Kyle

How else am I supposed to think of it?

Angie

Is that how you’re going to think of our future children?

Kyle

How can you even ask me that?

Kyle

Those kids will be planned. It’s totally different.

Kyle

This situation wasn’t in either of our plans.

Kyle

It needs to be taken care of so that we can PROPERLY take care of the children we do plan for in the future.

Angie

And it doesn’t bother you at all that the Source just threatened to kill me?

Kyle

Of course it does.

Kyle

You have to understand—

Angie

They threatened me. I don’t have to understand anything.

Kyle

But you do. This isn’t the first time you’ll be threatened if you marry me one day.

Kyle

This is how government works.

Kyle

If you want to be in the game, you need to start playing.

Kyle

They’re showing you their hand.

Kyle

They’re saying this situation means enough to them to kill for it.

Angie

So I’m just supposed to agree to play?

Kyle

You’re supposed to back off and do what they say.

Kyle

Reap the rewards for your loyalty.

Kyle

Because the cards in “your hand” don’t include a willingness to die for a fetus.

Angie

No, but my cards do include the President of the United States.

Angie

I could tell my dad everything.

Angie

He could take the necessary steps to replace his security detail.

Angie

We don’t have to let them have this much power.

Kyle

I wouldn’t do that if I were you.

The pit in Angie’s stomach grows.

Angie

Why not?

Kyle

The Source is everywhere—

Kyle

Not just on your father’s security detail.

Kyle

They are literally ingrained in every single aspect of the federal government.

Kyle

Even if your father exposed them and fired his entire secret service…

Kyle

They would just be replaced with more Source members.

Angie

How does this seriously keep getting worse?

Kyle

They would assassinate your entire family for exposing them.

Kyle

Along with the Vice President, the Speaker of the House…

Kyle

They’d move down the line of presidential succession, until a loyal Source member assumed the presidency.

Angie

I don’t believe this.

Kyle

Believe it, Angie.

Kyle

You can’t beat them.

Kyle

The society is too big, and their power is too strong.

Kyle

So if you want to make it in this city—

Kyle

If you want to stay ALIVE—

Kyle

You have to join them.

Angie

Wait — if they’re so powerful, why didn’t they just assassinate my family at the inauguration?

Angie

Why go through all of this backdoor bullshit?

Angie

Why involve me?

Kyle

Because if the American people saw your family die, it would have caused chaos—

Kyle

Marches, riots…maybe even a rebellion.

Kyle

That’s why the Source approached you.

Kyle

You can stop all of that horrific violence from happening.

Angie

Hold on. You said the Source “approached” me.

Angie

But they didn’t — you were the one who first mentioned them to me.

Kyle

Your point?

Angie

Did you ask me out because you liked me—

Angie

Or because the Source told you to?

Kyle

Does it matter?
