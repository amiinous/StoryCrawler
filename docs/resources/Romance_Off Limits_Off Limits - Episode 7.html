Off Limits - Episode 7

It’s Monday evening.

Kim is waiting for Jacey at the Frayed Knot.

She texts someone else while she’s waiting.

KIM

Are you free?

KIM

Frayed Knot. Ten minutes.

KIM

Jacey is on her way.

KIM

Must talk.

Jacey walks in and kisses Kim on the cheek.

JACEY

Who are you texting?

KIM

Not Russ.

Kim looks up from her phone.

She sees that Jacey’s been crying.

KIM

Honey, what’s wrong?

JACEY

It’d be easier to tell you what’s NOT wrong.

KIM

Rough day?

JACEY

The worst.

JACEY

I officially broke up with Hercules last night. For good this time.

Kim slides a whiskey and diet to Jacey.

KIM

Misery loves company.

JACEY

Why? What happened to you?

KIM

Oh, it’s embarrassing.

KIM

I was going to contact Ty, right? Invite him to Spain?

KIM

Well, when he didn’t answer, or call me back, I asked Russ instead.

KIM

And he turned me down.

KIM

Even though it was all expenses paid.

KIM

One of the best golf courses on the planet — and this guy loves to golf.

KIM

And he said no.

KIM

Apparently, he just wasn’t that into me.

JACEY

Maybe he was uncomfortable with the whole family trip angle.

KIM

No. It gets worse.

KIM

After he refused the trip…he broke up with me.

KIM

Said I was too cranky for him.

JACEY

You’re not cranky. You’re bitter. And bitterness works for you.

KIM

He’s a stick in the mud, and HE broke up with ME.

JACEY

He beat you to the punch. You were going to end it anyway.

JACEY

It still sucks, but…

KIM

Aren’t we a pair? Shitty all around.

Jacey raises her glass.

JACEY

To things looking up.

Kim nods toward the door.

KIM

Things already are.

Jacey looks over her shoulder and sees Ty entering the pub.

JACEY

What’s HE doing here?

KIM

You’re going to think I’m crazy, but I texted him to meet us.

KIM

I know you’re not wild about him.

KIM

But he’s the most decent guy either of us has dated in at least a year and a half.

KIM

I have to try.

JACEY

Kim, no.

KIM

Shh…pretend we’re talking about something else.

JACEY

Maybe I should give you some privacy.

KIM

I need you here.

JACEY

I don’t want to be here.

KIM

Be nice.

Ty approaches.

TY

Kim. Jacey.

JACEY

Hi.

Kim leans in and kisses Ty’s cheek.

Ty steps back.

KIM

You look great.

Jacey speaks into her whiskey.

JACEY

As always.

TY

I’m a little confused.

TY

I thought I was here to talk about—

KIM

You don’t mind if Jacey’s here, do you?

KIM

I always put my girl first, and she’s had a rough couple of days.

TY

Is that right?

JACEY

They have been difficult.

TY

The past twenty-four hours haven’t been so great for me.

TY

They started with a bang yesterday evening. But then they fizzled quickly.

TY

Would you believe I had a foot in the door with this amazing new opportunity?

TY

And it turns out the person in charge got cold feet and decided not to pursue it.

JACEY

Maybe it wasn’t the right time.

TY

This girl I’ve been seeing suggested the same thing.

Kim chases her ice with her straw.

KIM

You’re seeing someone?

TY

I am, but I’m not sure how to define our relationship at the moment.

KIM

She’s not very smart, if she let you out alone looking the way you do.

TY

Actually, she’s brilliant.

KIM

I’d say that’s up for debate. Obviously.

Jacey motions to the bartender.

JACEY

Could I have another whiskey please?

KIM

How long have you been seeing her?

Ty looks at Jacey.

TY

Not too long, actually.

KIM

So it’s a new relationship.

TY

More of a quality versus quantity thing.

KIM

Lucky girl.

TY

I like to think I’m the lucky one.

KIM

Sounds serious.

TY

I thought it was, but maybe it isn’t.

Jacey looks over at Kim.

JACEY

You don’t have to interrogate him. He’s obviously—

TY

No, I don’t mind. I like talking about her.

TY

She’s stronger than she realizes.

TY

And so inspiring.

TY

It’s like…if this girl puts her mind to something, you just know she’s going to accomplish it.

Jacey discreetly catches a tear on a knuckle.

KIM

You seem pretty hooked on her.

Kim puts on a flirty pout.

KIM

And to think I called you here to get you back.

TY

I almost believed you for a second. Kim. Always the joker.

KIM

No really. That’s why I called you.

KIM

But you seem happy. He seems different, am I right, Jace?

KIM

Maybe you should call this girl and figure things out before it’s too late.

TY

Hmm. Maybe I should.

JACEY

I don’t think—

KIM

Go ahead. Give her a call.

Ty reaches into his pocket for his phone and begins to dial.

Jacey’s not ready for this.

She grabs her phone and gets ready to bolt.

JACEY

I’ll be right back.

Kim puts her hand on Jacey’s arm.

KIM

Wait! Don’t you want to see what happens?

Jacey’s phone starts ringing.

Ty grins.

TY

It’s ringing.

Kim looks from Jacey to Ty.

Comprehension starts dawning across her face.

Jacey swallows a lump in her throat.

KIM

What’s going on? Jace?

She takes Jacey’s phone out her hand and sees the caller ID:

Hercules.

Jacey closes her eyes and sighs.

KIM

How could you?

Ty terminates the call.

TY

Wow. I guess I misread that one.

TY

I thought Jacey told you about us—

KIM

Jacey.

TY

—and I thought you texted and asked me to meet you guys here so we could work it out.

Kim looks at Jacey.

KIM

You broke the pact.

JACEY

I never meant—

KIM

No, no, no. This can’t be happening.

KIM

Is Ty…? He’s the guy?

KIM

I can’t believe this.

KIM

When Chaz asked me out, I said no.

KIM

Chaz is the hottest guy I’ve ever seen in my life, and I said no out of obligation to you.

KIM

I said no because I honored our pact.

KIM

And this amazing guy you’ve been seeing behind my back…it’s Ty?

TY

Otherwise known as Hercules. Nice to meet you.
