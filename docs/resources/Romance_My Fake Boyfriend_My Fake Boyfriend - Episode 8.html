My Fake Boyfriend - Episode 8

Mike and the other bullies walk off.

Hazel leans over Jack.

He has bruises over his face and one of his eyes is swollen shut.

Jack

Ugh.

Jack

Assholes…

Hazel

Are you okay?

Jack

I’ve had worse.

Jack

It’s not the first time Mike had an issue with me.

Jack

Probably won’t be the last.

Hazel

Does this hurt?

Jack

Ow! Yeah. Don’t touch it…

Jack

But I’ll be all right.

Jack

Did he hurt you?

Hazel

No. I’m more worried about you.

Jack

I said I’m fine.

Jack

Just some bruises. Though my eye will probably be black tomorrow.

Hazel

Don’t touch it.

Jack

I’m just testing…Ouch!

Jack

I need some ice.

Jack

At least Mike probably got it out of his system now, right?

Hazel

I don’t know. What if he comes back tomorrow?

Jack

Nah, I think he’s starting to draw too much attention.

Jack

We’re lucky a teacher didn’t come by.

Hazel

Let me help you up.

Jack

I can do it.

Hazel

Let’s go to the nurse.

Jack

I don’t need to.

Hazel

Then let’s go get some ice. It’ll help. Maybe some aspirin? For the pain?

Jack

I’ll be okay.

Jack

But honestly…

Jack

Hazel…

Jack

This is so hard to say…

Hazel

Jack?

Hazel

What is it?

Jack

I can’t…do this to you anymore.

Jack opens his bag, takes out Hazel’s junk journal and hands it back to her.

Hazel holds it in her hands.

Hazel

What do you mean?

Jack

If you were going to have a boyfriend, you need one that isn’t…

Jack

I just can’t do this.

Jack

I was a bad pick for a fake boyfriend.

Hazel

Don’t say that…

Jack

We should probably stay away from each other…

Jack

Maybe he’ll cool down for a while, but you’re ultimately right.

Jack

He’ll keep coming back. He’s looking for trouble. That’s all he wants.

Jack

And I will always be trouble. And I’m not worth it for you.

Hazel

Why would you say that?

Hazel

It’s my fault. I’m the reason he came up to us.

Jack

It isn’t your fault. I agreed to be your fake boyfriend.

Jack

And I threw the first punch.

Hazel

After he tried to grab me!

Jack

This will only get worse, Hazel.

Jack

If we keep doing this…

Hazel

We don’t have to let Mike stop us from hanging out.

Jack

It’s not about Mike. I mean…it isn’t only about him.

Hazel

Then what’s it about?

Jack

I can’t talk about it right now.

Hazel

You have to tell me.

Hazel

I don’t understand.

Hazel

If it isn’t about Mike, what do you mean?

Jack slowly stands up. Hazel is tearful.

Hazel

Don’t leave, Jack…

Jack

Avoid Mike. Don’t say anything to him.

Jack

I’ll make sure he leaves you alone.

Jack

But stay away from me. So he doesn’t go back to picking on you…

Jack

You and I faking a relationship was a bad idea.

Jack

I should have…when we were together…

Jack

I don’t know. But this wasn’t what we should have done.

Jack walks away, ready to go home and skip the rest of the school day.

He gets into his car and his cell phone buzzes with an incoming text message.

Hazel

Jack.

Hazel

Don’t leave!

Hazel

If you don’t want to stay near me because of Mike, I understand.

Hazel

But don’t get in trouble for missing school.

Hazel

I’m so sorry.

Hazel

Please talk to me.

Hazel

Please, I don’t understand.

Ten minutes, Jack is sitting in his car in a parking lot away from school.

He looks over the messages one last time and then sighs, sending a response.

Jack

I can’t be your fake boyfriend, because I like you too much.

Jack

And you deserve better than me.

Jack turns the phone off and puts it down in the seat. He leans forward against the steering wheel…

And ignores everything.
