Official - Episode 1

Hailey

Do you think anyone noticed last night?

Chris

Noticed what?

Hailey

Very funny. We left the party for a solid 45 minutes.

Chris

Baby, I made it happen in 30.

Hailey

Are you sure that’s something you should be bragging about?

Chris

Yup. You’re hot.

Hailey

I’m also in Statistics — this isn’t the time or the place.

Chris

Yeah, but in 10 minutes we’re both gonna have a free block.

Chris

Your dorm or mine?

Hailey

We can’t keep doing this.

Hailey

But mine. Definitely mine.

Hailey drums her foot as she watches the clock tick down.

Two minutes before the bell rings, her phone vibrates with a text.

She smiles, wondering what Chris has up his sleeve for her…

Tasha

HAILEY. My noon class got cancelled and…

Tasha

Chris Gusman just SHOWED up in our dorm!

Tasha

Like, he let himself in and everything.

Tasha

He started taking his shirt off — he’s ripped btw — and then…

Hailey

Ohmigod, Ohmigod…

Tasha

I KNOW.

Tasha

He’s denying it now, but…

Tasha

Do you think he’s hooking up with someone IN our room?

Tasha

He’s always had a thing for underclassmen. Maybe it’s one of them.

Hailey feels her face burning.

Should she just come clean to her roommate?

Or does she need to admit that she’s been seeing one of their best friends —

On the down-low?

Hailey

Chris is totally hooking up with an underclassman.

Hailey

He didn’t tell you?

Tasha

No…

The bell rings and Hailey jumps out of her chair.

She races toward her dorm.

Her fingers are flying across her phone.

She has to quickly quell her friend’s doubt.

Hailey

Her name’s Persephone.

Tasha

Persephone? What kind of name is that?

Tasha

Chris says he doesn’t know a Persephone.

Hailey

He’s probably bluffing.

Hailey

Tell him that you’re texting me —

Hailey

And remind him that I saw them together at the party last night.

Tasha

He says you didn’t…and that you know who he was with.

Tasha

Hailey, what is he talking about?

Hailey

Hold on, getting in the elevator.

Hailey throws open the door.

She knew Chris was going to be there, but she’s thrown off for a moment —

It’s suddenly SO real when she sees him standing there with her best friend.

She’s busted.

Something squeezes in her chest —

If she knew last night was going to be her last time with Chris…

She would have never let it end.

Chris

Hailey —

Hailey

Why’d you come here?

Chris looks down at his feet, unsure of himself for a moment.

But when he looks up at her, he’s settled back into his relaxed demeanor —

He’s got the same easy smile on his face…

That always unwinds Hailey after a long day.

Chris

To see a girl.

Chris turns to face Tasha.

Chris

I really like this girl, Tash.

Chris

Otherwise, I wouldn’t use your room like this…

Chris

Without you knowing.

Tasha

I don’t get it though. Why wouldn’t you use your own room?

Tasha

Hailey? Back me up here. Isn’t this weird?

But Hailey’s mouth is too dry to speak.

Chris

Because this girl…she gets nervous about being with me.

Tasha

I’m sorry, Chris, but that’s kinda terrible of her.

Tasha

Are you sure she’s worth all this sneaking around?

Chris

Oh, she’s worth it.

Chris

She’s brilliant.

Tasha

Smarter than Hailey?

Chris

No one is.

Chris

But I think her intelligence makes her so nervous.

Chris

She calculates out every possible outcome in her head —

Chris

And she’s worried about the bad ones.

Tasha

Oh my God, she sounds EXACTLY like Hailey.

The room goes silent.

Tasha

Am I…missing something?

Chris gives Hailey a measured look.

His blue eyes are soft and sure.

Hailey stands frozen.

She doesn’t know what to expect…

But something at her core instinctively trusts him…

And it sends Chris the signal to keep talking.

Chris

She looks a lot like Hailey too.

Chris takes two steps toward her.

Chris

She’s got this mane of dark hair that I could get lost in for hours…

Chris

And her eyes light up when she laughs.

He takes another step closer, so he’s just a few inches away.

Chris

Her laughter is my favorite sound in the world.

Chris

So I’ll do whatever it takes to keep her laughing.

Chris

Even if it means I have to keep hiding what I feel for her.

Hailey arches an eyebrow, crossing her arms in mock annoyance.

Hailey

And you call THIS hiding what you feel?

Chris

I went with it, okay?

Chris

I thought it was romantic.

Hailey

You’re lucky that it was.

Chris

No. It was romantic because I’m skilled.

Chris

Because we have something really incredible together.

Hailey laughs.

Hailey

I guess I won’t argue with you…

Hailey

Since you’re giving me some of the credit here.

Tasha

Wait — rewind for a second. You two?

Tasha

You’re seeing each other?

Tasha bounces forward on the balls of her feet.

Tasha

Like, officially?

Chris gives Hailey a questioning look.

She slides against him, threading her arm under his.

She encircles his torso with her other arm, feeling his heart beating —

Slowly, soundly…

And so sure.

Hailey

Yes.

Hailey

It’s time we made this official.
