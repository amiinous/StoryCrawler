Streetrat - Episode 10

The goons drag Ezra and Jazz out of storage and bring them to Jack.

Jack

Eduardo! The paperwork!

Eduardo emerges with a pile of legal documents.

Jack

Alright Mohammed—it’s time.

Jack

Sign your company over to me.

Jack gleefully extends a pen to Ezra, whose bindings are temporarily cut.

Ezra looks between Jack and Jazz, who is held firmly by two goons.

Ezra hesitates.

Jack snaps his fingers, and the goons raise a knife to Jazz’s neck.

Jack

Sign, Mohammed.

With a heavy sigh, Ezra signs away his company.

Jack cackles.

Jack

I present to you, Proust Enterprises.

Jack

Say hello to your new leader!

Alex

And say goodbye to your freedom, Jack!

Jack

What are you talking about, you stupid boy?

Jack

Seize him!

Alex

Now that you’re in charge, your life is not your own.

Just then, Milton Holt emerges from around the corner.

Jack

What the—

Milton

Jack Proust. Good to see you old friend.

Jack

What is he doing here?!

Milton

Still bitter that I beat you out for the Berkshire Hathaway internship all those years ago, huh?

Milton

As evidenced by your obsession with acquiring my business.

Jack

I no longer need you or your business.

Jack

I’ve built my own empire—

Jack

—And it will be TEN times as powerful as yours!

Jack

Guards! Remove this man from my property!

Milton

Not so fast, Jacky boy.

Milton

While Ezra may have transferred his shares of the company to you, Mohammed Investments is still a publicly traded firm.

Milton

And when the board heard that I was interested in working with them, they couldn’t resist.

Milton

You may have Ezra’s share, but the majority share belongs to me.

Milton produces the paperwork—

Signed and sealed by the Mohammed Investments board of directors.

Jack

Bullshit! Impossible! Guards—remove him!!

Milton

I think that makes me your boss.

Milton

And you know what—I’m not too fond of this whole crazy sorcerer thing you’ve got going on.

Milton

I think you might be fired.

Jack

You can’t—this isn’t happening!

Then, members of Milton’s security team enter.

Milton

Oh, it’s happening.

Milton

And for some reason, you’re still here.

Milton

Which actually means you’re trespassing, since I fired you about two minutes ago.

Milton

Guys, can you handle this?

Milton gestures to his security team, who promptly handcuff Jack and Eduardo.

The security team hold the goons at gunpoint.

The goons quickly surrender, releasing Jazz and Ezra.

Jazz immediately runs to Alex.

Jazz

Alex!

Alex

Jazz—I’m so glad to see you.

Ezra

Alexander, isn’t it?

Alex

Sir, so nice to see you again.

Ezra

Thank you so much for this incredible display of courage and determination.

Ezra

I am so grateful to you.

Jazz takes Alex aside.

Jazz

The market.

Alex

What?

Jazz

The Santa Monica Farmers Market.

Alex’s face falls. She knows.

Jazz

You were at the market that day those guys harassed me.

Jazz

I knew I recognized you from somewhere—Ryan Reynolds.

Alex

Listen, I had this hair cut in 2012—

Before he can go on, Jazz wraps her arms around him and kisses him passionately.

And Alex isn’t going to let go anytime soon.

one year later

Alex and Jazz lie on deck chairs on a beach in the Bahamas.

It’s their honeymoon.

At first glance, it seems to be the two of them, but—

There is Milton Holt, sitting on the other side of Alex, drinking a pina colada.

Alex

Having fun, babe?

Milton

Tons.

Alex rolls his eyes.

Alex

See? I told you I would use my third favor to benefit you.

Milton

Not sure what kind of weirdo wishes for his boss to join him on his honeymoon.

Alex

The kind of weirdo who knows what you need.

Alex

You need to reclaim some of your life—and your freedom.

Milton’s phone buzzes. He reaches for it, but—–

Alex

Ah, ah, ah!

Alex

What did I tell you about phones?

Milton

They’re not conducive to a restful break.

Alex

Right. And what do you need more of?

Milton

Breaks.

Just then, Jazz’s phone buzzes.

Alex

Not you, too.

Jazz

Alex, what do you expect after my dad handed the company over to me to run?

Jazz

I may have one or two things to do...or twenty.

Alex takes both of their phones and throws them in the ocean.

Jazz

HEY!

Milton

NO!

Alex gestures to the waiter.

Alex

Another round of pina coladas, please!

Milton

Come Monday, I’m still your boss, you know.

Alex

Make that two!
