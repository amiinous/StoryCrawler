The Puppeteer - Episode 2

Catherine runs into the study with her mother, who quickly locks the door behind them.

Mom

I’m calling Aunt Agnes.

Catherine

Aunt Agnes?

Catherine

How is she going to help us?!

Catherine’s mother ignores her comment as she picks up the phone, dialing frantically.

Mom

Agnes? The puppet is moving.

Catherine can’t make out Aunt Agnes’s muffled response.

Mom

Yes, of course I’m sure!

Mom

I saw it myself.

There’s a full beat of silence before Catherine’s mother answers Aunt Agnes’s question.

Mom

I’m…not sure.

Mom

I don’t know how this could have happened, Agnes.

This time, as Catherine’s mother listens to Aunt Agnes speak, she eyes her daughter nervously.

Mom

Yes, please, as soon as you can.

Mom

We’re in the study. Thank you.

Catherine’s mother hangs up the phone and fiddles her fingers nervously.

Catherine

What were you guys talking about?

Catherine’s mother jumps at the sound of her daughter’s voice.

Mom

Oh, Catherine! I almost forgot you were here.

Catherine

You…what?

Catherine

Whatever. What were you talking to Aunt Agnes about?

Catherine

Have you seen the puppet before?

Mom

What? No. Of course not.

Mom

Let’s just try to stay calm until Aunt Agnes gets here.

Catherine

Aunt Agnes is coming here?!

Catherine

Whyyy?

Mom

Catherine, stop whining. You’re not a child anymore.

Mom

Aunt Agnes is very smart. She’ll know what to do.

Catherine lets out a dramatic sigh, and then whispers a complaint under her breath.

Catherine

Yeah, except Aunt Agnes is a complete loony…

Mom

Excuse me?

Catherine

Nothing, it’s just…Aunt Agnes is kind of all over the place, isn’t she?

Mom

Aunt Agnes is my sister.

Mom

We should trust and rely on our family.

Catherine

You’re right.

Catherine

Ugh, I miss Dad.

Catherine

He would have known what to do.

Mom

We’re still a strong family, Catherine. Even without your father.

Mom

Us women have to stick together!

Catherine’s mom smiles and embraces her, but Catherine keeps her arms at her side.

Mom

Cheer up, please.

Thirty minutes later, there’s a loud banging on the window.

Catherine

Something’s outside!

Mom

Hide behind the desk!

Mom

I’ll go look.

Catherine

Please be careful, Mom.

Catherine

I’m scared.

Catherine’s mom slowly approaches the window and lifts the curtain to peer outside.

Mom

Oh!

Mom

It’s Aunt Agnes!

Mom

We need to let her in.

Catherine

Geez! That freaked me out.

Catherine

Now do you see what I was talking about?

Catherine

Aunt Agnes couldn’t come through the front door like a normal person!?

Mom

Honey, you know it’s not safe for us to leave the study. She was doing us a favor.

Mom

Come help me open the window. It’s stuck.

Catherine and her mother both pull upward on the window, but it doesn’t budge.

Mom

There’s no time for this.

Catherine’s mother grabs the lamp off of the study desk and smashes the glass window.

Catherine

Whoa!

Catherine

Why did you do that?!

Mom

No time to waste. Come, help me pull Aunt Agnes up.

Mom

You grab one arm and I’ll grab the other.

Catherine and her mom both heave Agnes in through the window.

Once inside, Agnes stands up and dusts off her gaudy dress.

She straightens her spectacles, and readjusts the many trinkets hanging from her neck and ears.

Agnes

Marie, my dear sister! And little Catherine!

Agnes

Oh, I’ve been so worried about both of you!

Agnes

I’m so glad you called me here in this time of grave danger.

Mom

Thank you for coming, Agnes.

Mom

I know this isn’t an…ideal situation.

Agnes

Yes, of course. Where is the puppet then?

Mom

It’s locked in Catherine’s closet.

Agnes

And the puppet is mobile, you say?

Mom

Yes, it moved itself to Catherine’s bedroom.

Agnes

Can it communicate?

Mom

No.

Catherine

It moved its mouth!

Catherine

But it didn’t make any sound.

Agnes

I see.

Aunt Agnes pauses to think for a moment.

In the silence, Catherine can hear her pulse pounding in her ears.

Agnes

The only solution I can see is to destroy the puppet permanently.

Mom

But how?!

Suddenly, a loud crack echoes through the house.

Mom

The closet!

Agnes

Stay put, Catherine!

Agnes rushes out of the study, and Catherine’s mother follows.

Two minutes pass. To Catherine, it feels like an eternity.

Her chest is tight with worry. She strains to hear any sounds from her mother and aunt outside.

Finally, when the anxiety becomes overwhelming, she calls out…

Catherine

Mom? Aunt Agnes?

Catherine

Are you okay?

Catherine

Can I come out?

Catherine pokes her head out first, and when she sees only the empty hallway before her,

She walks out of the study slowly. When she reaches her bedroom…

She sees Aunt Agnes and her mother standing closely together, peering inside.

She looks beyond them and sees the puppet sitting patiently on her bed.

Catherine

It got out of the closet?!

Catherine’s mother and Agnes both jump and wheel around at the sound of her voice.

Mom

Catherine! Go back to the study!

Catherine

No way -- if that thing can break down doors, we need to leave this house.

Agnes

I think that’s an excellent idea, Catherine.

Agnes

You and your mother should leave.

Agnes

Go -- wait at my house.

Mom

I’m not leaving you here alone, Agnes.

Agnes

Marie, it’s best if Catherine is as far from the puppet as possible.

Catherine’s mother nods her head and begins to gather a few items before they depart.

Catherine continues to stare at the puppet. It’s sitting motionless on her bed.

Catherine

You know…

Agnes

Marie, I said that you and your daughter need to leave. Right now.

Mom

C’mon, Catherine. Aunt Agnes is right.

Mom

Let’s get out of here.

Catherine’s mother reaches for her hand.

But Catherine pulls her hand away…

Runs into her bedroom…

And locks the door behind her.
