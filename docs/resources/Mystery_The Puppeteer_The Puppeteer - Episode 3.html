The Puppeteer - Episode 3

Catherine’s mother and aunt rush towards the bedroom door.

Mom

Catherine – NO!

Mom

Get out of there!

Agnes

Unlock the door NOW.

Mom

Agnes, she’s not listening. What do we do?!

Agnes speaks firmly towards the door.

Agnes

Catherine, dear, listen to me.

Agnes

You need to unlock the door and let us in.

Silence.

Agnes

The puppet is very dangerous.

Agnes

If you do not come out, you leave us with no choice but to break down the door.

Mom

She’s still not listening to us!

Mom

Catherine, please come out! Please!

Catherine’s mother begins to cry.

Agnes glares at her sister disdainfully.

Agnes

Don’t be hysterical, Marie.

Agnes

Your sobs aren’t going to solve anything.

On the other side of the door, Catherine stands in silence, listening to her mother’s pleading.

But her eyes are focused on the puppet.

Finally, she hears her mother and aunt’s footsteps fade into the distance.

Catherine

Hello? Mr. Puppet?

The puppet remains motionless on Catherine’s bed.

Catherine

Can you…understand me?

Catherine

I think you might have been trying to tell me something earlier…

Catherine

What…what do you want?

The puppet’s eyes begin to move slowly to the right.

Catherine takes a step back.

Catherine

Oh god. Okay. You’re moving again.

The puppet’s eyes stop and rest on Catherine’s desk.

Catherine

What are you looking at?

Catherine shifts slowly towards her desk until she’s in the puppet’s line of sight.

The only thing laying on her desk is her copy of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone.

Catherine

My book?

Catherine

I’ve read this thing a hundred times.

Catherine

Are you saying that you’re magic or something?

The puppet’s eyes move slowly from the book to the bed.

Catherine

What?

Catherine

You want to read it?

Catherine lets out a laugh.

Catherine

Look, my mom and aunt are sort of trying to kill you right now.

Catherine

Might not be the best time for a casual read.

The puppet’s eyes back to the book, then to the bed again.

Catherine

Fine.

Catherine

Here. Have it.

Catherine plops the book down on the bed next to the puppet

The puppet’s hand shoots up and lands on the cover with a small thump.

Catherine jumps back from the bed, surprised by the sudden movement.

The puppet drags its hand across the cover. It stops at the beginning of the title.

Catherine

What? You want me to read to you?

Catherine

Like I said, now’s not the best time.

Catherine

Please just tell me what you want before my mom and aunt come back.

The puppet’s hand starts to make small circles on the cover.

Catherine leans in towards the book, carefully maintaining distance between her and the puppet.

Catherine

What are you pointing at?

Catherine

That’s an “H”.

Catherine

What about it?

The puppet’s drags its hand to the right and starts circling again.

Catherine

“O”…

Catherine

Oh! It’s like the Ouija board that Aunt Agnes bought for me last Christmas.

Catherine

So you were trying to tell me something!

Catherine feels a rush of excitement.

Catherine

Okay, H-O.

The puppet drags its hand down to the author’s name.

Catherine

“W”

Catherine

H-O-W.

Catherine

How!

Catherine

How what? You need to know how to do something?

The puppet continues to drag its hand across the cover.

Catherine’s heart is racing. She inches closer and closer to the book.

Catherine

“A”. And “R”!

Catherine

H-O-W-A-R.

Catherine

How are? How are what?

Catherine is engrossed in the thrill of the puppet’s puzzle.

She feels her heart thumping out of her chest.

The puppet’s hand stops at the next letter.

Catherine

What?

Catherine

“D”?

Catherine

Didn’t you mean “E”?

Catherine

H-O-W-A-R-D.

Catherine freezes

Her heart stops and she feels the blood rush from her head.

Slowly, she looks up at the puppet. It’s inches away from her face.

Their eyes meet.

Catherine

Dad?
