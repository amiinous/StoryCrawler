Streetrat - Episode 1

This is a magical story of love and deception.

Greed and manipulation.

Desire and determination.

It begins at the Santa Monica Farmer’s Market.

Where beautiful and tempting foods line the stands.

Alex Marcourt and Abby Carson are long-time partners in crime.

In this case, literally.

Alex

Did that cop see you?

Abby

Dude, he’s back again! This guy is a problem.

Alex

He’s definitely onto us.

Alex

Maybe we should try another stand.

Abby

Stop being paranoid, Alex. We always take stuff from this stand.

Alex

Yeah – and I think this cop is starting to suspect that we don’t belong here.

Abby

Maybe we should hit up a Nordstrom next.

Abby

Fix up our look.

Alex

Shit – he’s back again.

Alex

Follow me – let’s go check out the fruit stands.

Abby

Sorry – I’m allergic to healthy food.

Abby

I prefer organic chocolate.

Alex

Watch out!

Alex

You’re going to run right into him!

Alex

Abby! Pay attention!

Abby

Chill, Alex. He just thinks I’m cute.

Alex

No, I think he’s calling for backup.

Abby

No, he’s calling for doughnuts. You need to calm down.

Alex

I really don’t think love is on his mind right now.

Alex

Time to move to the bread stand, Abby.

Abby

Okay, let’s go.

Alex

You approach from behind.

Abby

Are you in position? Tell me when – I’ll flip the cart.

Alex

Is the coast clear?

Abby

I think so.

Alex

You have to be sure! We can’t take any chances …

Abby

I’m sure.

Alex

NOW!

Abby tips the cart and sandwiches go flying. They both grab one.

Abby

GO!

Alex

Did you get one?

Abby

Yes!

Alex

Run! I think the cops saw us!

Alex

There’s three of them now!

Abby

Just keep running!

Alex

Head to the hideout!

Abby

Shit! Field trip. Where did all these kids come from?

Alex

Push through! They’re right behind us!

Abby

Quick, duck into that alley!

Alex

Almost there …

And they are – until someone catches Alex’s eye.

Causing Alex to stop in his tracks.

And Abby to crash into him.

Alex

Whoa – who is that? She’s beautiful.

Abby

Are you crazy?! Keep running! They’re right behind you!

Abby

ALEX! Stop stalking women and RUN!

Abby

ALEX!

The cops collar Alex, while Abby hangs back.

And the woman who bewitched Alex stops to see what the commotion is about.

Alex

Officer, you don’t understand.

Officer

I understand perfectly – you were trying to steal a sandwich.

Alex

You’re right, officer. I’m a horrible person.

Abby

What are you doing!?

Alex

Officer, you need to lock me up. I’m nothing but street trash.

Alex

I should have never even been born.

Officer

Hang on, now. We all make mistakes.

Alex

No – not like this. I’m worthless.

Alex

I shouldn’t be allowed to roam the streets.

Alex

You should lock me up and throw away the key.

The officer is moved by Alex’s display of remorse.

Officer

Son, you just need to think your actions through more carefully.

Alex

I know. I’m horrible.

Officer

You’re not horrible – we’ve all been there. Just don’t let it happen again.

Alex

Officer, you are too kind to me. I don’t deserve it.

Officer

Just return the sandwich and make sure you learn from this.

The officer pats him on the back.

After making sure that Alex gives back the Officer:sandwich, he returns to his squad.

At which point Abby gives Alex a look of undisguised admiration.

Abby

And the winner for best actor is … Alex Marcourt!

Alex

That WAS an Oscar-worthy performance.

Abby

Once again, we escape the clutches of the law.

Just then, before Abby can share her sandwich with Alex, the two overhear a commotion coming from the other side of the street.

The woman who enchanted Alex earlier is now surrounded by three thugs.

Her name is Jazz.

Thug #1

Who sent you?

Jazz

What? What are you talking about?

Thug #2

Which Besterboy sent you?

Jazz

I have absolutely no clue what you are saying right now.

Jazz tries to make her way around the gang, but they block her path.

Thug #3

That necklace – don’t act like you ain’t know.

Jazz doesn’t realize that the three overlapping stars on her necklace are the gang symbol of the Besterboys.

The thugs are Pummelins.

Their arch-enemies are the Besterboys.

Thug #1

Besterboys ain’t welcome in these streets.

Abby

Look at that girl. We’re not the only ones making mistakes.

Abby

Let’s get out of here.

Alex

But she needs our help!

Abby

You do not want to get mixed up in that – let her figure it out.

Alex

No – that’s not the right thing to do.

Abby

We just robbed a farmer’s market – NOW you’re concerned with right and wrong?

But Alex cannot resist trying to be a knight in shining armor.

Alex

There you are!

Jazz looks at him, confused.

Alex

I told you to wait for me at home.

Thug #1

A-man. You know her?

Alex and the thug exchange some kind of crazy handshake.

Alex

Dude, this is my sister. You know that.

Alex

She’s not all there, you catch?

Alex makes the “cuckoo” sign with his finger, which Jazz does NOT appreciate.

But after Alex shoots her a look, she quickly begins to play along.

Jazz

Big brother – why did you leave for so long?

Jazz

The pigeons and I missed you!

Alex

I know, I know.

Alex

See man? She didn’t mean anything by it. She doesn’t even know.

Thug #3

Best to keep her off of the streets, bro.

Alex

I know. C’mon, sis.

And Alex leads Jazz away from clear and present danger.

Jazz

Thanks for that.

Alex

You should really be more careful – don’t you know this part of town?

Jazz

Well …

Alex

If you walk on the wrong side of the street, they’ll cut you open.

Jazz

Yeah – I knew that. I come here all the time.

Alex

It sure doesn’t seem like it.

Alex looks at Jazz closely, which makes her uncomfortable.

Jazz

Anyway, thanks again – I have to run.

And she disappears around a corner.

Alex

Wait – I didn’t get your name!!

He sighs and catches up with an exasperated Abby.

He doesn’t see Jazz climb into a shiny black limo.

With a chauffeur in an expensive black suit.

Chauffeur

Ms. Mohammed – did you have a nice afternoon? Where to?

Jazz

Yes, I did, Chaz. To the mansion, please.

And Chaz drives off toward opulent Beverly Hills...
