Blood On The Shore - Episode 6

As Chloe and Trevor leave for Chloe’s bedroom, Anna and Matt look confused.

Helen looks down at the floor.

It’s a dead giveaway that she knows something.

Anna

Helen, what’s this about?

Matt

Agreed—what’s going on, Helen?

Helen

Why don’t I fix us all some coffee?

Helen escapes to the kitchen.

But as she’s preparing the coffee she feels Matt’s hands on her shoulders again.

She can’t help it. She turns around and kisses him.

As she pulls back from his lips, Helen catches a glimpse of Anna,

who quickly retreats back to the living room.

Matt

Helen, do you think…

Matt

Can you give us another chance?

Matt

We were so young.

Matt

I wish…

Helen

What?

Matt

That I hadn’t gotten married again.

Matt

That was a rebound mistake that lasted way too long.

Helen

I don’t know, Matt…

Matt

It’s not too late, is it?

Matt

Can we give this another try? Now that we’re older and wiser?

Matt

Whenever I’ve tried to get closer recently, you’ve pushed me away.

Helen

It’s just…I’m afraid of getting hurt.

Helen

I hear the stories.

Helen

I still keep in touch with my friends at the station, you know.

Helen

They say you’re quite the ladies’ man now. That blonde paralegal…

Matt

You mean Natalie?

Matt

We did go out on a few dates.

Helen

See? I knew you were interested in other women.

Matt

Helen Olsson…I do believe you are jealous!

Matt

Does that mean I have a chance?

Helen

Maybe.

Matt

Let’s take it slow.

Matt

The station in Grand Marais reached out to me…

Matt

They said they have an opening for a detective.

Matt

I was thinking of applying.

Helen

That would be nice.

Helen

But no guarantees, Matt.

Matt

No guarantees, but…

He gives Helen a long kiss.

Chloe

Mom! Dad!

Chloe has entered the kitchen.

Trevor is beside her, looking dazed but happy.

Chloe

I’ve been waiting for this day!

Helen

Chloe, I don’t want you to jump to any conclusions here.

Helen

It’s not like we’re rushing into anything…

Matt

But I’m going to move up to Grand Marais.

Matt

And we’ll see what happens.

Matt

Right, Helen?

Helen

Right.

Chloe

Oh, that’s great, Dad!

Chloe

Isn’t that awesome, Trevor?

Trevor

What?

Trevor

Oh, uh…yeah.

Just then, Anna re-enters the kitchen.

Chloe

Um, Mom, Dad, Anna…

Chloe

Trevor and I have some news.

Trevor

Yes, some really big news.

Anna

What is it?

Chloe

So—

Chloe

I’m pregnant.

Chloe

And Trevor is the father.

Chloe

And I’m going to have the baby in about five-and-a-half months.

Anna’s eyes widen, and Matt’s mouth drops open.

Chloe

I told Mom yesterday.

Anna

But…I didn’t even know you two were dating!

Trevor

We aren’t—at least not yet.

Anna and Matt look questioningly at Chloe and Trevor, who look down at the floor bashfully.

Helen

Look, we don’t have to get into the details now.

Helen

The important part is, a new member of the family is on the way!

Helen

And I think this calls for a celebration!

Matt

Yes, a celebration!

After two hours of eating and drinking and excited conversation,

Chloe goes over to her parents.

Chloe

Mom? Dad? I think I’m going to stay over at Trevor’s tonight.

Chloe

We have a lot to talk about.

Helen

Chloe!

Matt

Helen, let her go.

Matt

Let’s face it. That horse is out of the barn.

Helen

Oh, I guess you’re right. I’ll see you tomorrow, Chloe.

Anna

And Helen? I hope you don’t mind.

Anna

But I made a reservation at a hotel tonight.

Helen

What? I told you, you can stay here as long as you like.

Matt

Let her go, Helen.

Helen

Oh, alright.

And instead of sleeping on the couch, Matt stayed in Helen’s room that night.
