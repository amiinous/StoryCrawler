The Best Mistakes - Episode 4

Skye

You what?

Caleb

I like you. I always have.

Caleb

You had to have known.

Skye

I didn’t.

Caleb

Come on, you practically lived at our apartment—

Caleb

You didn’t notice how much time I spent around you?

Skye

It was a small space, it wasn’t like you had much choice.

Caleb

I had a choice, and I chose to be around you.

Skye

Caleb…

Caleb

Think about it. I’d watch Real Housewives episodes with you. Why do you think I did that?

Skye

Because it’s a really good show.

Caleb

No, because I loved seeing your reactions to it.

Caleb

You’d talk to the TV, shake your head, gasp at every fight.

Skye

You liked it too. There’d be nights we’d stay up talking about it for hours.

Caleb

That was the part I liked, when we’d just talk.

Caleb

It wasn’t just about the show, remember?

Caleb

We got to know each other.

Skye

I loved Scott.

Caleb

And that’s why I never said anything or made a move.

Skye

Until now.

Caleb

Like you said, it’s been years…

Caleb

I was hoping that now, it’d be different since you and Scott weren’t together.

Skye

We’re not together because *you* told him not to call me.

Caleb

I told him not to call IF he wasn’t serious about you—

Caleb

There’s a big difference.

Skye considers this.

Caleb makes a good point.

Besides, Scott could have still reached out, but he chose not to.

Caleb

Skye, when I found out you were the one texting me…

Caleb

It really felt like fate.

Caleb

Like it was finally our chance.

Skye

I don’t know…

Caleb

Think about it. When we were texting just now, we had a connection. A spark.

Skye

I thought I was texting a stranger.

Skye

That’s what was exciting. It wasn’t real.

Caleb

But it WAS real.

Caleb

It is — and I’m not just talking about our connection today.

Skye

There was nothing between us back then.

Caleb

That’s not true. I know you felt something.

Skye

Caleb…

Caleb

Remember that period when Scott would run out to the gym every morning?

Caleb

You’d roll your eyes about it, but I think you preferred it being just the two of us.

Skye

He was in his no carbs phase. I was a cereal girl.

Caleb

I remember. We’d each have a giant bowl of Lucky Charms.

Caleb

We’d talk about the day ahead and make wishes whenever we found the clover marshmallow.

Caleb

I made the same wish every day.

Skye

What was that?

Caleb

I think you know.

Skye

I want to hear you say it.

Caleb

Okay. I’d wish that you were with me instead of Scott.

Skye

This is a lot to take in.

Caleb

Is it? Deep down, I think you’ve known it all along.

Caleb

And I think you may have had some feelings too.

Skye

I was all about Scott.

Caleb

Maybe, but remember when I started dating Dari?

Caleb

I saw the way you’d look at us.

Skye

Because of the way you doted over her. It was so over the top.

Caleb

Ahhh, you do remember.

Skye

How could I not? You were sickeningly sweet together.

Skye

Scott was always on his phone or playing some stupid game. You guys were a team.

Skye

You’d cook meals together, and really listen when you told each other stories—

Skye

It was like you were each other’s world.

Skye

I’d watch you go up and give her a kiss on the back of neck, and I knew she meant everything to you.

Caleb

She didn’t though.

Skye

Sure seemed like it.

Caleb

I knew you were watching us.

Skye

Please, of course I was. We already established that I like reality TV…

Skye

This was just like a live version right in my living room. That’s why I watched.

Caleb

That’s the only reason?

Skye

That’s it.

Caleb

Well, I seem to remember how you lit up when I told you I ended things with her…

Skye

She was dull. If there was going to be someone else hanging out in the apartment I wanted someone fun.

Caleb

You can’t admit a tiny part of you was jealous of her?

Skye

Jealous? You doted on her and then broke it off.

Skye

Not exactly the behavior I look for in a partner.

Caleb

I had to end it. It wasn’t fair to her. I knew she wasn’t the one.

Skye

Yeah? And who was?

Caleb

You, Skye. It’s always been you. Can you please just give us a chance?

Caleb

Don’t you think we deserve it?

Skye

I don’t know…

Caleb

We can do something small to start.

Caleb

I’m not asking you to marry me. Yet 😉

Caleb

Just meet me for dinner.

Skye

This is a lot to take in. I need to think.

Caleb

I can give you time if that’s what you need.

Skye

I do.

Caleb

Okay, but just promise me that this won’t be the last that I hear from you.

Skye bites her lip.

She knows she shouldn’t promise him that, but she can’t help it.

There’s something inside her that intrinsically trusts Caleb…

And always has.

Skye

I promise.

When Skye wakes up the next morning and checks her phone, she sees that she has a text.

Scott

Hey, Skye.

Skye does a double-take.

Because even though she assumed it’d be Caleb texting her…

This time, the text actually DID come from Scott.
