Kiara's Game - Part 13 - The First Bite

And so I waited to see who Mr. Archivolt would come after first: me or Abby.

But then a strange thing happened…

Connor nodded his head, just slightly, in Zoe’s direction.

And then he winked.

Kiara

Mr. Archivolt?

Mr. Archivolt

Yes.

Kiara

I’ll do it. I’ll drink Abby’s blood.

Mr. Archivolt

I knew you’d—

Kiara

IF… I get to say goodbye…

Kiara

To everyone. Even my friend Zoe who died.

Mr. Archivolt

Very well.

Then Connor nodded, almost imperceptibly, and I knew that I made the right decision.

I knew that what I felt for him—that connection—was real. He was always on my side.

Sure enough, when I reached Zoe, I could sense her heart beating.

It was weak, but she was alive. And her voice was scarcely a whisper.

Zoe

I know what he is, Ki.

Zoe

And I know what you are too.

Kiara

Zo, I am so sorry.

Zoe

Don’t be.

Zoe

You didn’t choose this.

Zoe

It’s just what you are.

Kiara

I should have blown you off the day we met.

Kiara

I should never have let you get close me.

Zoe

I would never have stopped trying.

Zoe

You’re my best friend, Ki.

Kiara

I don’t want this, Zo.

Zoe

What you want doesn’t matter.

Zoe

We are what we are.

Zoe

All we get to choose is what we do with that.

Kiara

I don’t want to be like him.

Zoe

You aren’t. Stay that way.

Kiara

How?

Zoe

You get to CHOOSE, Ki.

Zoe

You get to choose what you use this gift for.

Zoe

And not just for games.

Zoe

Can you use it for good? Because that’s who I think you are.

Kiara

That’s who I’m going to try to be, Zo. Every day.

Kiara

I promise.

Zoe

I know.

Mr. Archivolt strode toward us from the other side of the room.

Mr. Archivolt

Finish your goodbye, Kiara.

Mr. Archivolt

She’s just a dead human, after all.

Mr. Archivolt

We need to get on with this.

Zoe whispered in my ear.

Zoe

Will you tell my mother I fought, Ki?

Kiara

Don’t say that!

Kiara

You’re gonna get out of here.

Zoe

We both know that’s not true.

Zoe

I can barely lift my head.

Zoe

But I can give my life to save Abby’s.

There was a flash of clarity in Zoe’s eyes.

Zoe

You understand that, right?

I smiled at Zoe as the tears ran down my cheeks.

I understood exactly what she meant.

And I was certain it would work.

Suddenly, Mr. Archivolt knew it too.

He started racing toward me with terror in his eyes

While Connor started chanting from the book, in a voice that was loud and clear.

Kiara

Goodbye, Zo.

Zoe

Goodbye, Ki.

Mr. Archivolt

Kiara, NO!

I put my lips to Zoe’s neck…

And I sucked the last drops of life from her veins.

And in an instant, everything changed.

Time seemed to slow down.

The world became more vivid.

I could feel the energy in my body… the power.

And I knew it was nothing like the force Mr. Archivolt possessed.

He reached me and very simply, I tore his arm off his torso…

And sunk my teeth into his throat…

And I drank.

With each swallow, I felt myself growing ever stronger…

Inconceivably more powerful in every way. The rush was exhilarating.

And so was the sound of Connor’s voice, cheering me on.

Connor

Kiara, you did it.

Connor

You drank from a willing human at your Becoming—from someone who sacrificed herself for you and her friends.

Connor

Mr. Archivolt has never been given a gift like that…

Connor

And for that reason, you were given more power than he’d ever know.

Connor

Keep drinking, Kiara, finish him off.

And I that’s what I did.

I knelt there, draining Mr. Archivolt of his blood…

And though I felt powerful, though I felt victorious,

Though I felt… love…

All I could think was that I was incredibly grateful

That Abby wasn’t awake to see the monster I became

I pulled my teeth from Mr. Archivolt’s neck and let his corpse slip to the ground.

But as I turned to go to Abby, I saw Maya’s horrified face

Staring at me from the mouth of the tunnel.

She had slung Abby’s body across her back and they were creeping out into the night…

And her expression haunts my memory for all eternity.

Though I’ve never seen Maya or Abby again,

At least I know that I am out here in the world,

Making it a better place with my newfound powers,

As my friend Zoe would have wanted.

THE END
