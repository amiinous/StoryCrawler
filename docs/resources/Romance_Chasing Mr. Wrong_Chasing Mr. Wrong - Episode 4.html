Chasing Mr. Wrong - Episode 4

It’s Friday night…

Ryan sits across the table from Steph.

They both sip at their drinks, still silent after their brief introduction.

Ryan

So you probably want to start having kids right away, being as old as you are.

She almost spills her drink.

That’s a heck of a lead-in.

Steph

I’m not that old.

Steph

And eventually yes, I think I’d like to have kids.

Ryan

I’d want you to have them as soon as possible.

Steph stares at her date.

At his suit jacket and pressed slacks.

His manners have been impeccable since they sat down…

But he’s really intense.

And now he wants her to have hypothetical kids on their first date!

Steph

Shouldn't we get through this date before we pick out names for our children?

She laughs but he doesn’t join in.

Ryan

Children. Two. A boy and a girl. Ryan Jr and Evelyn, after my mother.

Ryan

She’ll want to be in the delivery room too.

Steph

Um, good to know.

Ryan

So, you’re an editor?

Finally, he’s asking about her.

She starts to tell him how much she loves her job…

Steph

Yes, for clients who self publish…

Ryan

That sounds…interesting. I’m in retention.

Steph

I’m not sure I know what that is.

Ryan leans across the table.

His eyes light up in excitement.

Ryan

You know when you want to cancel something?

Ryan

A credit card or a subscription?

Steph

Yes.

Ryan

I’m the guy who convinces you not to.

Steph

Oh, that is really…

She remembers trying to cancel her cable last month.

And how good the guy was at keeping her on the line.

Ryan

I love it. I can see myself doing it forever really.

Ryan

I want to run my own retention company.

Ryan

That’s my dream.

Ryan

Is that something, maybe someday…

He reaches his hand across the table and places it over hers.

Ryan

You could see yourself being a part of?

Steph barely succeeds in stifling a laugh.

She gently removes her hand from Ryan’s grasp.

Steph

Ryan, you’re a very…intriguing man.

Steph

But I’m not ready to talk about stuff like this on a first date.

Ryan

But your profile said you were looking for a serious relationship.

Steph

Well, yes, but I need time to get to know someone first.

Ryan

I can give you some extra time.

Ryan

How does three weeks sound?

Steph

I don’t know how much time I’d need.

Ryan

Okay, how about two months then?

Steph

I just don’t think we’re looking for the same things, Ryan.

Ryan

I’m not sure you’ve weighed all the benefits of being with me.

Steph holds back another laugh.

Was he trying to ‘retain’ her right now?

Steph

You may be right, but I think I’m going to go now.

Steph

It was nice meeting you.

Steph

Thank you for the drink, Ryan, but I don’t think I can stay for dinner. Good night.

Ryan

Goodnight, Steph. Let me know if you change your mind.

Steph gets us and all but sprints out the door.

What has she been doing?

The man she wants to be with is Dave.

He makes her laugh.

Makes her palms sweaty and her heart beat faster.

He makes her want more.

She needs to find him ASAP.

She orders an Uber and has it drop her at the grocery store…just in case.

But of course Dave isn’t there on a Friday night.

She goes home and texts Kylie.

Steph

I figured it out.

Kylie

Where are you?

Steph

Home. I…

Kylie

It’s 7:30 on a Friday. Why are you home?

Kylie

Oh! Is your newest date there with you? That didn’t take long! 😜

Steph

God no, he was WAY too intense. I left him at the restaurant.

Kylie

You? Ms. Too Nice for Words left someone in the middle of dinner?

Kylie

Gah, my little girl is growing up and becoming a woman. 😂

Kylie

So are you going to try again?

Steph

That’s why I texted you. I think I’m going to delete my profile.

Kylie

What? Why? You gotta kiss a few frogs before you find a prince.

Steph

That’s the thing. I think I have found my prince.

Steph

At least I hope so.

Steph grins to herself.

She thinks about Dave’s buff body.

About his gorgeous eyes.

His awesome sense of humor.

He’s the total package.

Her heart flutters and she giggles.

Why didn’t she ever think to get his number?

But she knows where he works, where he likes to get his coffee and where he shops.

Sometime tomorrow she will find him.

Kylie

Are you pausing for dramatic effect?

Kylie

Because I gotta say it’s working.

Kylie

WHO are you talking about?

Steph

Remember the guy from the first night?

Kylie

Jason? But I thought…

Steph

No! Dave. The waiter.

Kylie

The one you blew off to leave with another guy after you told him you’d go home with him?

Steph

I apologized, okay?

Steph

Look, I’m going to find him tomorrow and tell him that I like him.

Steph

See if he’d like to go see a movie or something.

Steph

God Ky, I am flipping out so hard. I really like him.

Kylie

You know all this from one drunk night?

Steph

I’ve seen him since. We had coffee.

Steph

And he lives practically next door and we shop at the same stores…

Steph

And love the same movies.

Kylie

You’ve been holding out on me! Tell me everything.

Steph

He almost kissed me in the middle of the produce aisle.

Steph

I thought I was going to die waiting…

Kylie laughs.

Kylie

You’ve got it bad.

Steph

I really do. Tomorrow is the first day of the rest of my life!

Steph tosses her phone on the bed and lies back.

She can’t help but think about Dave lying next to her.

Him touching her.

Her touching him.

As she closes her eyes, her thoughts and dreams are filled with Dave.

Two weeks later,

Kylie and Steph are sitting at the restaurant where Dave works.

The place is crazy busy.

But he’s not there.

He hasn’t been at the coffee shop.

Or the grocery store either.

No matter how hard she looks,

She can’t find him.

Kylie

I’m starting to think you made the guy up.

Steph

I am too. What if I don’t find him, Ky?

Steph

What if I’m too late?

When the waiter comes to their table she asks when Dave is working.

The frazzled server has no idea.

Steph

I think this is the universe's sign that I should stop with all of it and get ten cats.

Kylie

You’d make a terrible cat lady. And you’re allergic.

Steph

See, I’d even fail at being a cat lady. I suck.

Kylie

Well not lately you haven’t, but we’re trying to fix that right?

Kylie winks and Steph can’t help but laugh.

Steph grows quiet as she looks around the dining room.

The last time she was there,

Dave kept her company.

He made sure she didn’t feel like an idiot,

Sitting there by herself.

Steph

I wish it was Dave that first night. The guy I was supposed to meet.

Kylie

Don’t give up. The city is big but not that big.

Kylie

If it’s meant to be, it will happen.
