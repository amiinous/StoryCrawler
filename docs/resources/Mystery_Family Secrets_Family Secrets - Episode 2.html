Family Secrets - Episode 2

Hannah immediately recognizes the man whose car she dented earlier in the day.

Hannah

What…How?

Man

You again!

Man

You seem to have a habit of crashing into people.

Hannah

But…

Man

Fortunately, there was no damage this time!

Whenever Hannah is in close proximity to a man she finds attractive,

She gets tongue-tied.

She stutters—struggles—but…

Finally finds her voice.

Hannah

But…what are YOU doing here?

Man

I should be the one asking you that question.

Man

But I’ll humor you.

Man

My name is Tyler Grant, and I’m here by order of Virginia Pynchon.

Man

Now would you mind telling me who YOU are?

Man

Wait, let me find a light switch.

Suddenly, light fills the hallway,

Which is lined with sullen family portraits.

They all seem to be staring at Hannah.

Hannah

I’m Hannah Bright.

Hannah

Virginia Pynchon has asked me to be here too.

Slowly, realization dawns on Tyler Grant’s face.

He breaks into a smile that Hannah finds dazzling.

But she does her best not to show it.

Tyler

So you’re the one who’s responsible for ruining my weekend!

Hannah tries to suppress her strong attraction to Tyler Grant

By kicking into journalist mode.

Hannah

You’re the CEO of Greathall Industries, aren’t you?

Hannah

One of the youngest CEO’s of a Fortune 500 company, as I recall.

Tyler

Guilty as charged. And you’re Hannah Bright.

Tyler

The journalist who’s been chosen to write Virginia Pynchon’s biography.

Tyler

The very one who ruined my weekend plans.

Hannah

But I had no idea you would be here!

Hannah

Or that I was ruining anyone’s plans.

Tyler

But Ms. Pynchon knew.

Tyler

And what Ms. Pynchon wants, Ms. Pynchon gets.

Hannah

Well, since you’re here, I look forward to interviewing you about the company.

Tyler

And I look forward–

Both Tyler and Hannah jump as they are interrupted by a loud moaning noise.

The moan seems to have come from one of the bedrooms down the hall.

Hannah

What was that? WHO was that?

Tyler

I don’t know. I think it came from that room down there.

Hannah

Oh my god! That one’s right next to my bedroom!

Together, they hurry to the room.

The door is open. The room is dark.

Tyler

Hello, is anyone there?

Tyler

Here’s the light.

He turns on the bedroom light switch and they both walk inside.

There’s a four-poster bed in the middle of the huge gloomy room, but otherwise—

It’s empty.

Tyler

Let me check the closet to be sure we’re alone.

Hannah

I’ll look under the bed.

Tyler

Nothing.

Hannah

Nothing.

Tyler

Well, we couldn’t have imagined it.

Tyler

We both heard it, after all.

Tyler

Maybe it came from the room above?

Hannah

Maybe…

Suddenly Lucinda enters the room, wearing a low-cut black silk robe.

Hannah can’t help but notice Tyler eyeing Lucinda.

Lucinda

Do you mind if I join the party?

Lucinda

And what a very late party it is!

Tyler

Lucinda! Did you hear that loud moaning noise?

Tyler

We were investigating it.

Lucinda

Tyler, what are you doing in the Blue Wing?

Lucinda

Your room is in the Red Wing. Near mine.

Tyler

You know it’s my first time at the estate.

Tyler

And I’m a natural born explorer.

Tyler

I ran into Hannah here—literally.

Tyler

And then we heard a noise. It was as if someone was in pain.

Lucinda

Yes, that’s why I came by again.

Lucinda

I wanted to tell Hannah not to worry about any strange noises.

Lucinda

Bernadette has the room above this one.

Lucinda

And sometimes she has nightmares.

Hannah

I could have sworn it came from this room.

Hannah

On this floor.

Lucinda

No. Sounds carry easily in this old house.

Hannah

Oh. Well…thanks for coming by to let me know, but…

Hannah

Is there any way I could change rooms?

Hannah

I might have trouble sleeping with those noises.

Lucinda

Hannah, Bernadette has a very sad past.

Lucinda

We all try to make allowances.

Lucinda

Do you think you could too? It would mean a lot.

Hannah

I…

Tyler

Oh, I feel bad for Bernadette. That’s very sad.

Lucinda

Yes. She sleepwalks sometimes too.

Lucinda

Just so you know, Hannah.

Tyler

Listen, how did you happen to hear her, Lucinda?

Tyler

All the way from the Red Wing?

Lucinda

I was checking on her.

Lucinda

I know she doesn’t like any change in her routine.

Lucinda

Sometimes visitors upset her.

Lucinda

Tyler, should we return to our rooms?

For a moment, Hannah wonders if she sees regret on Tyler’s face.

Is he upset that he isn’t able to say goodnight, alone, to Hannah?

No. She must be imagining it.

It must be wishful thinking.

Tyler

Goodnight, Hannah.

Tyler

It was nice running into you. Again.

Lucinda looks at Tyler quizzically.

Tyler

I’ll explain later.

Lucinda

Goodnight Hannah. Again.

Hannah

Goodnight you two.

Hannah hates herself for feeling stirrings of jealousy

As she watches Lucinda and Tyler walk together toward the Red Wing.

Tired at last from her adventurous day, she goes to her room,

And pulls back the satin sheets on her canopied bed.

Suddenly, she jumps back in horror.

Because in the middle of the bed is a large dead bird.
