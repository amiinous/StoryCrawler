Ensnare - Episode 6

Later that evening, after Angie texts pictures of the file to the Source, she writes a new text.

Angie

I’m done.

Angie

Seriously. I can’t do this anymore.

Angie

I don’t care if the Source can get me everything I want — I’m out.

Angie

And I want that video back.

Kyle

Woah, wait.

Kyle

What video?

Angie

They have a video of me from that party you took me to back in November.

Angie

When I drank too much and we played that game.

Angie

They blackmailed me with it.

Angie

They said they’d release the video if I didn’t take pictures of a file on Dad’s desk.

Kyle

Did you do it?

Angie

Of course I did. They were threatening to DISMANTLE my entire future…

Angie

And my dad’s legacy.

Kyle

Ok, so what’s the problem?

Angie

Um, besides the fact that they can use this video to blackmail me anytime they want?

Angie

How about the fact that the file ended up being a top-secret military report?

Angie

It detailed the code names and locations of every single American special ops soldier in the world.

Kyle

So?

Angie

That kind of information could get people killed!

Angie

Do you realize what could happen if the file fell into the wrong hands?

Kyle

But it’s in the Source’s hands, so it’s fine.

Angie

It’s NOT fine.

Angie

Those people aren’t above using blackmail to get what they want.

Angie

Forgive me if I don’t feel all warm and fuzzy about your beloved Source.

Kyle

Angie, the Source would never let any of those soldiers get hurt.

Angie

How do you know that?

Angie

These are the people who blackmailed Senator Lynthrop into joining.

Angie

They blackmailed ME to do their bidding.

Angie

Is that how they always operate?

Angie

Do they blackmail all of you into doing what they want?

Kyle

I wouldn’t call it blackmail.

Angie

What would you call it?

Kyle

When you join the society, you learn a huge secret about every other member.

Kyle

And you give them a secret of your own.

Kyle

Something devastating enough that it would ruin you if anyone ever found out.

Kyle

We use those secrets to help each other succeed.

Angie

You mean, you use it as blackmail.

Kyle

We use it as motivation.

Kyle

It gives us a reason to help each other out.

Angie

Did you take that video of me at the party?

Kyle

NO.

Kyle

How could you think that?

Angie

I don’t know what to think anymore.

Kyle

I would NEVER do that to you, Ang.

Angie

Then how did they get it?

Kyle

I don’t know.

Kyle

There were several Source members there that night.

Kyle

Anyone could have taken it.

Angie

I can’t believe you put me in that situation.

Kyle

I didn’t think anything like that would happen.

Angie

You told me I could get out at any time because I hadn’t been initiated yet.

Angie

I want out.

Kyle

It doesn’t really work like that.

Angie’s brow furrows, but before she can respond, her phone dings with a message from the Source.

Unknown

We were pleased with your work today and are thrilled to offer you an invitation for membership.

Unknown

We have already outlined plans for your future that we are starting to put into effect.

Unknown

We plan to offer you key networking connections at your internship this summer.

Unknown

Those will carry out into your undergraduate and law school years at Georgetown.

Unknown

We will have you working for one of the three biggest law firms in the country upon your graduation from Georgetown Law.

Unknown

By age thirty-five, you will receive a Supreme Court nomination.

Unknown

Mr. Whitfield will run for the presidency in the election following your nomination.

Unknown

When he wins, you will become the first Supreme Court Justice First Lady.

Angie can barely breathe. Paralyzed, she watches the texts come in, one after another.

Unknown

We cannot properly convey how pleased we are regarding this prospect.

Unknown

We are putting the majority of our resources behind both you and Mr. Whitfield.

Unknown

Our first order of business is the abortion.

Unknown

The procedure is scheduled for two o’clock Friday afternoon.

Unknown

One of our personally-placed secret service members will be covering for you during that time.

Unknown

We are giving Mr. Whitfield permission to accompany you to the appointment.

Unknown

Full discretion in this matter is our top priority.

Unknown

No one can ever know you were pregnant.

Angie takes a deep breath.

Angie

I’m not doing it.

Unknown

Have you reconsidered the abortion?

Angie

I don’t know if I’m going to have one or not.

Angie

But I’m not joining your society.

Angie

I’m sorry. I understand you’ve put a lot of time and effort into this…plan…for me and Kyle.

Angie

But I can’t accept your invitation.

Unknown

You aren’t comprehending the situation.

Unknown

The word ‘invitation’ is a courtesy.

Unknown

You became a member the moment we first contacted you.

Angie

No, I didn’t.

Angie

Kyle told me I hadn’t been initiated yet.

Angie

That I could back out at any time.

Unknown

Mr. Whitfield was mistaken.

Unknown

The arrangement we have outlined for you and Mr. Whitfield will proceed as planned.

Unknown

By force, if necessary.

Angie

Look, I *really* don’t want that video to come out…

Angie

But I’m not going to let you force me into something I’m not even sure I want to do.

Angie

So, if you need to release the video, go ahead.

Angie

I’m done.

Unknown

Perhaps you do not understand the full implications of what we have told you.

Unknown

We have Source members in your family’s security detail.

Unknown

The very people keeping you and your family safe report to us.

Angie

You’re lying.

Unknown

One of your secret service members could kill you in your sleep and make it look like a heart attack.

Unknown

We could have you publicly assassinated and blame it on a group of rioters.

Unknown

We cannot let you jeopardize the investment we’ve made in Mr. Whitfield.

Unknown

If you value your safety, we suggest you agree to our stipulations.
