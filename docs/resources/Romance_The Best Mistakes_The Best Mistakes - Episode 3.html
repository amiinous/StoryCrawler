The Best Mistakes - Episode 3

Skye

What is it? What do you have to tell me?

Caleb

You’re not going to like it.

Skye feels a lump in her throat.

What is Caleb hiding?

Why does she care?

Skye

Are you married?

Caleb

What?! No!

Skye

Girlfriend?

Caleb

No, nothing like that.

Skye

Then what is it?

Skye

You’re making me nervous…

Caleb

I’m living with Scott again.

Skye stares at her phone in disbelief.

Skye

No.

Caleb

We barely see each other. Our schedules are completely different…

Caleb

But yeah, we share an apartment.

Skye

That liar! He told me at the reunion that he moved—

Skye

He said he was in a nice two bedroom in midtown now.

Caleb

Technically, he wasn’t lying.

Skye

Technically?

Caleb

Well, he does live in a two bedroom in midtown. It’s just — I live with him.

Skye

I thought you moved to DC.

Caleb

Keeping tabs on me, I see. I like that. 😉

Skye

Clearly I’m not. Otherwise I would’ve known you live with my ex.

Caleb

Ouch. Hey, a guy can hope.

Skye catches herself smiling as she shakes her head.

She doesn’t know what to make of all this.

Holding her breath, she waits for Caleb to write something else.

Caleb

You were right. I did move to DC, but I came back last year. Scott saw I was looking for a place and asked me to move in.

Skye

And you did.

Caleb

Afraid so.

Skye

So let me get this straight—

Skye

Your friend Scott asks you to be his roommate. You say yes. Then, when his ex texts you, clearly looking for him…

Skye

You try to get her for yourself instead of fessing up?

Skye

You do realize this doesn’t paint the best picture of you, right?

Caleb

It’s not what you think.

Skye

Then what is it, Caleb?

Caleb

He’s not the type of guy you deserve.

Skye

Says the guy trying to steal me from his friend.

Caleb

He is not my friend. As soon as this lease is up, I’m moving out.

Caleb

And I’ve always been looking out for you. I swear.

Caleb

That’s why I told him not to call you.

Skye

YOU TOLD HIM WHAT?!

Skye

OMG, what kind of game are you playing?

Skye

You know what, I don’t want to know, just leave me alone.

Caleb

Skye, please, let me explain.

Caleb

It’s not what you think.

Caleb

When Scott got back from the reunion, the first thing he said was — you’ll never believe who I hooked up with.

Skye

This is so messed up. You knew?

Caleb

I’m getting there, please just hear me out—

Caleb

He was being obnoxious and saying all these things. I don’t even want to tell you what they were.

Caleb

But I told him that if he wasn’t ready for something real, he shouldn’t call you.

Caleb

I told him you’re worth more than a booty call.

Skye

That wasn’t your decision to make.

Caleb

I knew how you felt about him.

Caleb

Remember? I was there when he broke it off with you.

Caleb

I saw how much he hurt you. I didn’t want you to go through that again.

Skye

We were practically kids then. It’s been years. What if he’s changed?

Skye

That happens, you know. Sometimes people grow up.

Caleb

Not Scott. Believe me—

Caleb

I live with him, so I know.

Skye

I don’t even know what to say.

Caleb

Say you understand.

Skye

I don’t. At all. I don’t get why you would do any of this.

Caleb

Don’t you see?

Caleb

I had feelings for you, Skye.

Caleb

I still do.
