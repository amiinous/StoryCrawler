Our Adventure - Episode 6

One month later, Martha and Caroline are bundled in their winter coats on a walk around the lake.

Caroline

Listen, I ran into Juan at Spoonful again…

Martha

Ugh! Why do you have to keep going there?!

Caroline

Hey now — you know Spoonful is my favorite café.

Caroline

I’m not giving it up because your puppy-dog ex-boyfriend keeps trying to get back together with you.

Martha

Oh my God! He is NOT my ex-boyfriend. We were never even dating.

Caroline

Martha, come on. You’re really only kidding yourself right now.

Caroline

You obviously mattered a lot to him.

Caroline

And I know you — so I know that he mattered to you too.

Keeping her hands in her pockets, Caroline nudges Martha with her elbow.

Caroline

Go on. Text him.

Martha

No way. I told him I wouldn’t speak to him again, and I always keep my promises.

Caroline

Martha, that’s a dumb excuse and you know it.

Caroline

I think you’re AFRAID to text him.

Martha

Afraid? What would I be afraid of?

Caroline crosses her arms in jest.

Caroline

You tell me.

Then her arms fall to her sides.

Caroline

Seriously. You know that you can tell me.

Caroline

Anything.

Martha’s shoulders visibly slump.

Martha

I know. It’s just…

Martha

I don’t know how much longer I can do this to myself.

Martha

Just being paralyzed is difficult enough.

Martha

I mean, if I could choose between the paralyzed me and the old me, I’d choose the old me, every single day.

Martha

How can I expect someone to choose this me?

Martha’s voice cracks, and then she lets herself do something she NEVER does.

In front of Caroline, she starts to cry.

Martha

Why would anyone want to be with me when I don’t even like myself?

Caroline

Oh, Martha. I had no idea you felt like that.

Caroline bends down and wraps Martha in a hug. They stay like that for a while.

Then Caroline pulls back.

Caroline

Martha, you are one of the strongest people I know.

Martha

Ugh, I—

Caroline

I know. I know that you hate being called strong — but honestly, I don’t give a damn right now.

Caroline

Because you need to hear this.

Caroline

You are SO strong.

Caroline

You can’t move your LEGS, Martha. And in the whole time you’ve been living this nightmare…

Caroline

I’ve never seen you cry. Not once. You never let it get you down.

Martha

But I complain all the time! And I’m dramatic—

Caroline

Yeah, but complaining and being dramatic — it’s just your way of venting.

Caroline

You make jokes and laugh about what you go through. It’s how you cope.

Caroline

And it…it makes it easy to be your friend.

Martha

You’re the one who makes friendship easy, Caro—

Caroline

Martha? Will you just shut up and take the compliment?

Martha

Haha, okay. Thanks, Caroline.

Caroline

You’re welcome. And now — will you shut up and text Juan?

Caroline

He really wants to see you. He said that he realizes he made a mistake by forcing you to rock climb.

Caroline

That he should have checked with you in the first place.

Martha

Ugh, fine. I’ll text him for you.

Caroline

Haha, suuuure — I’ll let you pretend you’re texting him for me.

Martha juts out her jaw, giving Caroline her best glare before the two women break out in smiles.

Then Martha texts Juan.

Martha

Hey…so I hear you’ve been stalking Caroline at Spoonful recently 😉 That seems awfully serious of you.

Juan

Martha??!

Juan

Didn’t I tell you I was going to be serious? I’m still trying to win over your heart, you know.

Martha

Well lucky for you, I’m willing to give you one more chance.

Juan

Oh really??

Martha

Yes. Meet me at the reflecting pool in Veterans Garden in Balboa park on Saturday at 3pm?

Juan

I see what you’re doing there -- honoring my family roots?

Martha

Something like that.

Martha

Plus, I might have an adventure up my sleeve…

On Saturday, Martha waits at the reflecting pool in Veterans Garden.

She glances down at her watch when she sees Juan’s muscular figure walking toward her.

He’s ten minutes early. She smiles to herself.

Martha

You rushed to get here, huh?

Juan

Well, yeah. Only an idiot would be late to a date with you.

Martha feels the familiar blush spread across her cheeks. Somehow, Juan’s words always seem to have that effect on her.

Juan

Listen, Martha…

Martha

No. It’s okay. You have nothing to explain.

Martha

I completely freaked out at the Sports Piers.

Juan

Yeah, well the only reason you freaked out was because I put you in that position in the first place.

Juan

And I’m sorry.

Martha

I forgive you. And I’M sorry I didn’t give you the chance to apologize in the first place.

Martha

I didn’t give you much of a chance at all, huh?

Juan

Sure you did. You swiped right on me, didn’t you?

Martha

Haha yeah. But I want to do something even MORE special for you.

Juan

Oh yeah?

Martha

Yeah. My cousin Lillian…she owns a hot air balloon business. And she’s bringing one of the balloons to the park for us.

Juan transforms into a labradoodle puppy and Martha feels her heart melt.

Juan

WHAT??!

Juan

I’ve never been in a hot air balloon before — this is AMAZING!!

Martha

I was hoping you would think so.

Juan

Martha, I’m spending the day with you. Of course I think everything’s amazing.

Juan puts his hands on the arms of Martha’s chair — one of her favorite places for them —and he leans toward her.

The tension is so delicious and thick Martha can feel it around her, pressing into every pore in her skin.

Suddenly, a voice interrupts them.

Lillian

All right, you two! Let’s take a ride in my balloon. I have other appointments today, you know.

Martha laughs, and Juan joins in with her. After slipping an arm under her knees and wrapping the other one around her back…

He lifts and carries her over to the hot air balloon.

He and Lillian get her situated on a seat inside the woven basket,

And then Lillian pulls in the ropes and feeds the balloon more fire…

Until they’re gently sailing through the clear blue sky. The view of Balboa Park is breathtaking.

Juan’s voice eases into the silence.

Juan

Martha?

Juan

Can we talk about what happened at the Sports Piers — just one more time?

Martha

Haha, do we really have to?

Juan

Yeah. Because I want to make sure we’re on the same page about something.

Juan

It’s just, I have a lot to learn — about your paralysis, but also about the person you are, Martha.

Juan

And I want to learn everything.

Martha

Juan…

Juan

I’m serious. I want to KEEP learning those things about you.

Juan

I’m not looking for a temporary thing — this isn’t some story I want to tell people.

Juan

I’m looking for something that’s gonna last forever.

Martha

That’s a serious thing to say.

Juan

But I mean it — I want you to be my girlfriend.

Juan

I mean, will you?

Juan

Will you be my girlfriend? Please?

Martha

Of course I’ll be your girlfriend.

Juan

Oh, thank God. I really like you, Martha.

Juan

You fill me with potential energy.

Juan

I mean, look at what we’re doing now — I can feel the energy coming out of every part of me…

Juan

my eyes, my fingertips — my mouth.

Martha raises an eyebrow.

Martha

Your mouth?

Juan

Haha, yes! My mouth. Come here, let me show you —

Juan waits a beat before he tastes Martha’s lips.

The flavor of their kiss is freshly ground coffee beans and cinnamon…

And it sends a strong current of potential energy surging through them…

One they’ve never felt before.
