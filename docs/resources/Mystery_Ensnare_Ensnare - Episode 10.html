Ensnare - Episode 10

Angie switches from her text chain with Will to the one she has with Kyle.

Angie

Kyle. Listen to me — very carefully.

Angie

You’re going to convince the Source to leave me and my family alone, and to let me keep the baby.

Kyle

And why would I do that?

Angie

Because I have these.

Angie sends Kyle the pictures.

Angie

I’ve drafted emails to every major news outlet with these pictures, including every last detail of the hit and run.

Angie

I’ve also attached your entire text chain with the Source, along with your threats to me.

Angie

So yeah, you can let the Source kill me—

Angie

But your entire future will be obliterated the second I send this email.

Angie

Say goodbye to your dream of a presidency, and hello to prison for the rest of your life, asshole.

Kyle

I swear to God I’ll kill you.

Angie

Good. Because if I die, it’ll only prove your guilt.

Angie

In case the manslaughter charge from this hit-and-run doesn’t stick.

Kyle

The Source won’t let you do this.

Kyle

They’ll kill you before you can release this information.

Angie

I have my finger on the send button right now.

Angie

And so does a friend of mine—

Angie

Someone the Source knows nothing about, who’s located somewhere they would never even think to start looking.

Kyle

This is so dumb, Angie.

Kyle

You realize this isn’t going to take down the Source.

Angie

Of course I do.

Angie

And maybe I can’t take down the Source.

Angie

Maybe no one can.

Angie

Hell, they might even kill me tonight.

Angie

But that’s the beautiful thing about a death sentence—

Angie

*I* have nothing to lose.

Angie

You, on the other hand…

Kyle

What do you want, bitch?

Angie

Exactly what I told you.

Angie

The Source is going to leave me and my family alone.

Angie

And I will have this baby.

Angie

So, you and your precious society need to make your future plans work with you as a teen father—

Angie

Because I’m keeping this baby whether it messes with your life or not.

Angie

Any questions?

Kyle

I’ll get back to you.

Angie glances nervously at her locked bedroom door.

Angie

Better hurry.

Angie

I’m not moving my finger away from the SEND button—

Angie

And it’s getting heavy.

Angie gets a text from Kyle an hour later.

Kyle

I’ve spoken with the Source.

Kyle

And they’ve decided that you can keep the baby.

Kyle

I will run as a Republican instead of a Democrat, on an anti-abortion platform.

Kyle

You and the baby will play the perfect props in my rise to the presidency.

Kyle

It’ll be a harder road than if you hadn’t gotten pregnant, but we’ll do it.

Angie

Let’s get one thing straight.

Angie

There’s no “we” in this.

Angie

As of tonight, I’m officially done with you and the Source.

Angie

The only involvement you’ll have with this baby is through child support payments.

Kyle

Unfortunately for you, that isn’t going to fly.

Kyle

The Source doesn’t want to lose me, or the time and resources they’ve put into me—

Kyle

So this is their final offer.

Kyle

If you don’t take it, we’re both dead.

Kyle

So, you’re going to man up and take the deal.

Angie

I’ll do whatever the hell I want.

Kyle

You can, if you want the Source to assassinate your entire family.

Kyle

Forget any other future you planned for yourself, Ang—

Kyle

You belong to me now.

Kyle

And both of us belong to the Source.

Kyle

So — how does getting married immediately after graduation sound?

Kyle

And just in case I wasn’t clear enough — that was a rhetorical question. It’s happening, sweetheart.

Kyle

Whether you like it or not.

With tears in her eyes and shaking hands, Angie switches to Will’s text chain.

Angie

It worked. They’re going to let me keep the baby.

Will

And you’re safe?

Angie

Yes.

Will

Thank God.

Will

I don’t know what I’d do if someone hurt you.

Angie

Will, I just want you to know—

Angie

I’ve always loved you.

Angie

And I always hoped, when the timing was right…

Angie

You and I would find our way to each other.

Will

Oh, Angie….

Angie

I knew it couldn’t happen now, with us living thousands of miles away from each other.

Angie

That’s probably why I stopped texting you, because it was just too hard, but—

Angie

You’re my whole world, Will.

Angie

You always have been.

Will

I love you too.

Will

I always have.

Will

Ever since the fourth grade, on my first day at that stupid school…

Will

I knew I loved you the second I saw you, even before you called me your best friend.

Will

I don’t know if there’s a God, but if there is—

Will

I knew in that moment that He created me just for you.

Angie

Will…

Will

I don’t care about the distance.

Will

We don’t have to wait.

Will

We can be together now. We can make it work.

Will

Angie??

Angie

Goodbye, Will.

Tears stream down her face as Angie blocks Will’s number.

She falls into her bed, surrounded by her pillows.

Three weeks later, Angie is putting on makeup when she gets a text from Kyle.

Kyle

What do you think? Navy tie, or crimson?

Angie

Does it matter?

Kyle

Of course it matters.

Kyle

I’m about to tell the President of the United States that I knocked up his daughter.

Kyle

And that he’s going to be my father-in-law in five months.

Kyle

I might want to look my best when I’m asking him to call me “son.”

Angie

Wear the blue tie.

Angie

Crimson’s a power color. You should try to look as humble as you can.

Angie

That’s never been easy for you.

Kyle

You’re in a mood today.

Kyle

Baby got you acting crazy?

Angie

If that’s your stupid way of asking how I’m feeling, I’ve been better.

Kyle

Come on, Ang. Lighten up.

Kyle

Can’t things go back to the way they were?

Angie

No, Kyle. They really can’t.

Angie

Look, I’ve agreed to marry you.

Angie

We can be the perfect picture of domesticity at every public function from now until the day I die.

Angie

But behind closed doors, you and I are not friends.

Angie

As far as I’m concerned, you are Evil Incarnate.

Kyle

Ouch.

Kyle

Good thing I don’t care what you think.

Kyle

But do reconsider hating me for the rest of our lives.

Angie

No chance.

Kyle

Well, I plan on living a long time…

Kyle

And I don’t want to have a miserable life just because my wife won’t stop being a total bitch for two seconds.

Angie

I didn’t plan on having a miserable life either. You stole my future from me.

Kyle

You seriously need to get over that.

Kyle

I mean, you’re getting what you want.

Kyle

You get to keep the baby.

Kyle

And sure, the Source doesn’t know if they’ll be able to make you a Supreme Court Justice anymore…

Kyle

But at least they’ve promised to help you get through college and law school at some point.

Kyle

It looks to me like you have a lot to thank me for.

Angie rolls her eyes just as a new text pops up.

Unknown

We have a new assignment for you.

Angie

What is it?

Unknown

We have information that Mr. Whitfield may be working as a double-agent for an up-and-coming society calling themselves Project Embolus.

Unknown

We believe he may be using them to bolster his attempt to become president someday.

Unknown

We can’t allow members to join other societies.

Unknown

Our members know too much about our organization.

Unknown

Your assignment is to discover if these allegations are true. If they are—

Unknown

We want you to take him out.
