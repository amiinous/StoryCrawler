Our Adventure - Episode 1

Martha

Hey, I’m Martha. I guess I’m supposed to start the convos on this dating app, so…what’s up?

Juan

I thought you’d never ask.

Martha

Lol, what?

Juan

I swiped right on you two weeks ago. It’s been torture waiting for you to contact me.

Martha

Well, that’s…flattering.

Juan

Haha, it’s supposed to be.

Juan

You seem really cool and adventurous. Did you really climb Machu Picchu?

Martha

Yeah, but that was a long time ago.

Juan

What do you mean?

Martha

I don’t want to talk about it.

Juan

Oh, okay — I get it. I’ll back off. I won’t bother you.

Juan

It was nice talking to you, Martha.

Martha

No, Juan, wait!

Martha

Look, I like you.

Martha

Or at least, I like the same things you’ve listed in your profile. Like traveling, seeing new things…

Juan

Why am I sensing a ‘but’ here?

Martha

Because it’s a hard confession for me to make.

Martha

People always act all weird when I say it and they forget how to be normal around me…

Juan

Martha, just tell me. I want to hear it, whatever it is.

Martha

Two years ago, I was paralyzed.

Martha

It’s okay.

Martha

You don’t have to say anything. I probably should take those pictures off my profile.

Martha

I probably shouldn’t act like I’m still adventurous.

Martha

I’m obviously not that kind of girl anymore…

Juan

Martha, please — let me text you back before you jump to conclusions.

Martha

Okay…

Juan

I have an important question to ask you.

Martha

Okay…

Juan

What’s your favorite thing to do on the weekend?

Martha

Haha, that’s…random.

Juan

No it’s not 😛 I want to get to know you, no matter what.

Martha

Haha, okay. Umm…I guess I like to take long drives with my friends on Saturdays.

Martha

We go all the way up into the mountains, and look out at the scenery below us.

Martha

I like being up there, because it just makes me feel…energized. Like, full of potential energy.

Juan

Haha, I like that — full of potential energy.

Juan

I totally get that feeling when I go on adventures.

Juan

And Martha?

Martha

What?

Juan

Don’t you realize that those long drives are adventurous too? Like, in spirit.

Juan

Adventure doesn’t always have to manifest itself through extreme sports, you know.

Martha

Yeah, I know. But I liked when I got to do those things.

Martha

I was a better version of me when I was bungee jumping and ziplining.

Martha

I really miss the way I was.

Juan

Well, I guess I’m not the best judge since I didn’t meet the old you -- but this new you seems pretty great to me.

Juan

I really like talking to you, Martha.

Martha

I like talking to you too.

Juan

Good. So — I have another important question.

Martha

Haha, such a serious conversation!

Juan

Well, I’m trying to convince you to fall for me, so of course it’s gonna get serious 😛

Juan

So are you sure you’re ready? This next one’s a big one.

Martha

Lol, umm…I hope I am?

Juan

Haha, good enough — do you have any siblings?

Martha

Lol, what a tough question!

Martha

I do. I have one older brother.

Juan

Uh-oh. Do I have to worry about him?

Martha

Actually…yeah.

Martha

Bart’s a D1 linebacker.

Juan

Oh shit.

Martha

Yeah. He’s always been protective of me, but it got a lot worse when I ended up in the chair.

Martha

I had been dating my boyfriend Justin for three years when it happened.

Martha

Within a month, Justin broke up with me.

Juan

That’s terrible. I’m so sorry.

Martha

Yeah, it pretty much sucked.

Martha

Anyway, Bart was furious.

Martha

He came home from college that weekend just to track Justin down.

Juan

Omg — did the kid go to the hospital?

Martha

Haha, no. Bart didn’t actually hit him or anything.

Martha

But Justin DID show up at my house the next evening to apologize.

Juan

Whoa — did you forgive him?

Martha

Honestly…I didn’t. I couldn’t. Not that night, anyway.

Martha

He was only saying sorry because he was scared of my brother.

Martha

He wasn’t saying it because he actually meant it.

Juan

Wow — good for you.

Martha

What?

Juan

It’s just — I really respect you for not letting Justin walk all over you that night.

Martha

Thanks for saying that, Juan. It really means a lot.

Martha

Justin told a bunch of people at school and not everyone agreed with my decision.

Martha

People said some pretty mean things…

Martha

They said that because one bad thing happened to me, I suddenly thought the whole world was against me.

Juan

Wow that IS terrible — people suck, huh?

Martha

Yeah, a lot of them do.

Martha

Some don’t though.

Martha

My friend Caroline, for example. We weren’t THAT close before everything happened.

Martha

But after it did, she always stood up for me when I needed someone…and that happened a lot.

Martha

Now she’s one of my really good friends.

Juan

She sounds cool. I’m glad you have her around.

Martha

Me too.

Juan

So — can I ask how it happened?

Martha

How I was paralyzed?

Juan

Yeah. But of course we don’t have to talk about it if you don’t want to.

Martha

No, it’s okay.

Martha

I mean, I know you’re probably wondering if I was doing something awesome, like jumping out of a plane, right?

Juan

Haha, kind of. Is that a bad thing to admit?

Martha

Haha, no it’s not. I mean, I wish that was the story.

Martha

But it’s not. I was on my bike.

Martha

And it was so ordinary…I was riding to the grocery store to get milk. The ride takes all of five minutes.

Martha

Like every day, I stayed in the bike lane, I signaled at the traffic stops…

Juan

But at one of them, a driver didn’t see you?

Martha

Exactly. She didn’t.

Martha

It wasn’t like in the movies, where the driver hits you and runs off.

Martha

She stayed there, and the whole time she was freaking out.

Martha

And I kinda wanted to…comfort her?

Martha

I know it sounds absurd because I was the one lying on the ground.

Martha

I’m the one who couldn’t feel my legs and knew my life was about to change forever.

Juan

Oh, Martha. That’s not absurd. That’s amazing.

Juan

Because even in that terrible moment, you were worried about the people around you.

Martha

No. It wasn’t that…

Martha

I wasn’t necessarily trying to help her because I’m a good person.

Martha

I mean, I try to be good…but that wasn’t what it was that day.

Martha

I think maybe I wanted to soothe her to distract myself?

Martha

She had this screechy voice and I think some part of me just wanted to stop her from screaming.

Martha

Like, if I had enough control to fix one little thing, I would be better. I would make it through the nightmare.

Juan

I totally get that feeling. Well, maybe not to that extreme — but I get it.

Juan

My family moves a lot because of my mom’s job. At first, I had a hard time dealing with it.

Juan

Whenever we moved into a new house, I’d tell my dad we had to…

Juan

Idk, paint the door red or grow a vegetable garden —

Juan

Just to make each new place feel like home.

Martha

Did it work?

Juan

Haha, not exactly.

Juan

No matter how many things we tried to keep the same, it always took me a while to adjust.

Juan

I couldn’t force it.

Martha

Yeah…I’ve come to that conclusion about accepting my paralysis over the past couple years too.

Juan

Haha, we seem pretty similar, huh?

Martha

Yeah…I guess so 😊

Juan

Martha, can I ask you another serious question?

Martha

Sure…

Juan

It’s just — you seem awesome. Like, I know we’ve only had one conversation…

Juan

But I already feel like you’re one of the strongest people I’ve met.

Juan

So maybe — can I take you on a date?
