My Fake Boyfriend - Episode 5

Hazel

Jack.

Five minutes later.

Hazel

Are you there?

Hazel

Maybe you’re busy. Or driving.

Hazel

Hope you’re okay.

Hazel

Just wanted to see if you wanted to meet up at our spot again.

Hazel

But if you’re busy, don’t worry. I kinda miss my fake boyfriend though…

Jack stares at the messages on his phone for ten minutes before he texts Brady.

Jack

Brady, I don’t know if I should talk to her.

Jack

She keeps texting me.

Brady

Are you ghosting her?

Jack

No! But, I don’t know how to tell her that we shouldn’t talk to each other.

Brady

I thought you liked her.

Brady

You were JUST asking me how to ask her out and stuff.

Jack

But with Mike harassing us, I don’t know if I should.

Jack

I mean, Mike is right.

Brady

You’re crazy.

Jack

I’m not. She’s too good for a guy like me.

Jack

You even said it yourself. She’s out of my league.

Brady

Who is she right for then? She doesn’t like Mike.

Jack

That doesn’t mean she deserves a guy like me.

Jack

Like you said, she doesn’t take me seriously enough to actually go out with me.

Jack

I think this whole fake boyfriend thing has made things so much worse.

Jack

I’m just fake.

Brady

Look, I’m not Hazel, but from everything I’ve heard from you…

Brady

It seems she really likes you.

Brady

I think you need to just chill.

Brady

You’re making yourself a target for Mike.

Brady

And you’re making Hazel a target by shoving it in Mike’s face.

Brady

You guys keep holding hands in front of everyone and she’s always blabbing about her new boyfriend.

Jack

She is?

Brady

You’re all she talks about.

Brady

I feel this is just going to end badly for everybody.

Brady

You probably need to talk to her, but I get it if you need to think about what you need to say.

Brady

We have an entire school year to go. You don’t want to be on Mike’s shit list.

Jack

I don’t know what to do.

Jack

I know we need to talk. I just don’t know what to say.

Brady

I think she likes you, but if you don’t think it will work out, then you have to let her go.

Brady

You have to break off the fake dating thing.

Brady

You need to do it before Mike makes the whole thing worse.

Brady

If he was picking on Hazel before, it will be worse if he finds out this whole thing was fake.

Jack

He’d make it worse, yeah.

Jack

But do you think there’s a chance Mike will just chill out eventually?

Brady

I’ve never known Mike to just chill.

Jack

He has to eventually. That’s the only choice.

Jack

I’m not breaking up with her. If I do, she’ll be all alone.

Brady

She’ll find a boyfriend. She’s pretty.

Brady

She won’t have a problem there.

Brady

Then it’ll become his problem, not yours.

Jack

I can’t do that to her.

Jack

And I don’t want to.

Brady

Come on, man. Don’t do this to yourself.

Brady

But…if you can’t break it off, then there’s only one other alternative.

Jack

What?

Brady

Don’t fake it. Either make it look like you two broke up or cut the fake part.

Jack

I don’t know how.

Brady

Just ask her.

Jack takes time to think about what he wants…

But he can’t get himself to talk about breaking it off with Hazel.

Later that night, Jack works up the courage to text her.

Jack

Hey, Hazel.

Jack

I know it’s late, but I got your messages. I was pretty busy today.

Jack

I can’t sleep.

Jack

Anyway, I wanted to say hello.

Hazel

It’s okay. And hello.

Jack

You’re still up?

Hazel

Yes.

Hazel

I did a few pages in my junk journal earlier.

Hazel

But I don’t know…it was hard not hearing from you today.

Hazel

I’ve gotten used to talking to you.

Hazel

I know it’s probably silly.

Jack

It’s not silly…

Jack

Because honestly, missed talking to you, too.

Jack

I’m sorry couldn’t make it to our spot today.

Hazel

That’s okay. It’s not like we can go every day.

Jack

Yeah, but going to our spot every day wouldn’t be so bad…

Hazel

No 🙂 Actually, it would be kinda nice…

Hazel

It’s like the only place in the world…

Hazel

Where we can just talk.

Hazel

And not being judged by other people at school, you know?

Jack

I know.

Jack

Hey, Hazel…

Hazel

Yeah?

Jack

Can I pick you up before school tomorrow?

Hazel

Sure…I’d like that.

Jack

Maybe a little earlier than normal?

Jack

Can I make up for the time we didn’t have together today?

Hazel

You don’t need to make up for anything 🙂

Hazel

But how about an hour before our usual time?

Jack

Sounds good to me. I’ll be there.

Hazel

I’ll be up and waiting.

Hazel

Will you be able to fall asleep soon?

Jack

Probably. I feel better now that I’ve talked to you.

Jack

But I should let you go if we’re both getting up so early in the morning.

Hazel

I hope you sleep well.

Jack

Me too. Night.

Hazel

Goodnight, Jack.
