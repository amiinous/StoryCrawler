Tell Me Why - Episode 1

Steph

Hey you.

Maya

Hey yourself.

Steph

Tell me why again.

Maya

Why I like you?

Steph

Yes.

Maya

You know why.

Steph

Tell me anyway.

Steph

I could use some reassurance.

Maya

I told you I was scared — not that I’m running for the hills.

Steph

I know.

Steph

But. Just tell me.

Steph

Please?

Maya

Well…you’re thoughtful.

Steph

Boring.

Maya

You have an incredible, wry sense of humor.

Steph

Also boring.

Maya

Because the first time your hand brushed against mine…

Maya

It felt like my skin was on fire.

Steph

Better…

Maya

Because the first time you kissed me…

Maya

I thought to myself…

Maya

How have I gone so long without being kissed like this?

Steph

Probably because you’ve never kissed a woman before.

Maya

Even so…it’s your lips that make me feel that way.

Steph

God.

Steph

You make me melt.

Maya

Steph.

Steph

Yes?

Maya

Come over.

Steph

If I could, I would already be at your door.

Maya

What are you actually doing?

Steph

I’m trying to find this bar to meet a friend.

Steph

I’ve walked three blocks in every wrong direction.

Maya

What friend?

Maya

I mean, it doesn’t matter.

Maya

It doesn’t have to be just a friend. I don’t have to know.

Steph

She’s just a friend.

Maya

But if you wanted to date, if someone else came along…

Maya

I mean, you should be dating.

Steph

I don’t want to be dating.

Steph

You know what I want.

Steph

Who I want.

Steph

Maya…

Steph

What are we?

Maya

We’re seeing each other.

Steph

Unofficially.

Maya

Steph—

Steph

It’s been almost six months.

Maya

I’m just not ready.

Maya

I’ve never had these feelings for a woman before.

Maya

I’m trying to understand what it means.

Maya

It’s a lot. I need time.

Steph

Yeah. I know.

Maya

Hey. Stop that.

Steph

Stop what?

Maya

I like you.

Steph

I like you too.

Steph

Look, I’m at the bar. I gotta go.

Steph

Speak soon?

Maya

I hope.

Steph walks into a low-lit cocktail bar. Her friend Ashley is waiting for her.

Ashley

Finally! I was going to call in reinforcements soon.

Steph

Sorry, I got distracted.

Ashley

Uh-huh. I ordered you a drink.

Steph gulps her beer as if she’s been wandering in the desert for days.

Ashley

Thirsty?

Steph

Only emotionally.

Ashley

What is it this time?

Ashley

Or who, should I say.

Steph sighs dramatically.

Ashley

Still Maya?

Steph

She’s just scared.

Ashley

I understand being scared. I really do.

Ashley

But people can be scared and still be true to how they feel.

Ashley

When is she going to commit? Really let you in?

Steph

When I wake up in a man’s body tomorrow?

Ashley

That’s not funny. And you could never pull off a beard.

Steph

Just a little self-deprecating humor.

Steph

And I could definitely pull off a beard.

Ashley

Has she even told a friend that she’s seeing a woman?

Steph

Yeah, she’s told a few friends.

Ashley

What about her family?

Steph

They know she’s…free spirited.

Ashley rolls her eyes.

Ashley

That means straight.

Steph

Hey. Don’t be judgy.

Steph

You can’t give up on her before you’ve even met her.

Steph

I really like her, Ashley.

Ashley

Fine. I will not judge.

Ashley

But only because you’re pathetic and I’m an incredible friend.

Steph holds up her beer.

Steph

Cheers to my pathetic nature always winning you over.

After a couple of hours at the bar, Steph feels her phone buzz.

She pulls it out and sees a text from Maya.

Maya

I’m thinking of you.

Ashley

If you ditch me right now I will not hesitate to employ violence.

Steph

I won’t, I won’t. I’m just gonna step outside. One sec.

Steph

Order more drinks.

Steph goes outside to call Maya.

Steph

Hey you.

Maya

Hey yourself.

Steph

So I hear you’re thinking of me.

Steph

Is this true?

Maya

Yes.

Steph

Well. Do tell.

Maya

I’m thinking about your eyes.

Steph

Mhmm…

Maya

How they remind me of a deep lake on a cold day. They’re so blue.

Steph

They are indeed blue.

Maya

How when you look at me, I feel like you’re seeing into me.

Steph sighs audibly.

Steph

Come here.

Maya

Aren’t you still with your friend who’s just a friend?

Steph

Yes. Come. She won’t mind.

Maya

I don’t think that’s a good idea.

Steph

What? Why?

Maya

I don’t want to intrude and I don’t know your friend and—

Steph

She’s great. We’ve known each other a long time.

Steph

She’ll love you.

There’s a silence on the line.

Steph

Hello?

Maya

Are you at a gay bar?

Steph

What does that matter?

Maya

I don’t know. I’m not sure I’d be comfortable with that.

Steph

It’s like any other bar.

Steph

Just a place to have a drink.

Steph

It’s called Ginger’s — right off the F train.

Steph

Just come.

Maya

I’m scared that being affectionate with you in public will…

Steph

Will…what?

Maya

Steph, don’t freak out. I’m just—

Steph

Yeah I know. You’re scared.

Steph

I’m scared too, Maya.

Steph

I’m scared that I’m gonna lose you before we even started.

Steph

I’m not going to push you out of the closet, but…

Steph

You’re really sending me some mixed signals here.

Maya

Steph, please—

Steph

No, you know what? Don’t worry about it.

Steph

Wouldn’t want too much gay to rub off on you.

Steph hangs up. She breathes deeply and walks back into the bar.

Ashley

So…how’d that go?

Steph

I invited her out.

Ashley

Of the closet?

Steph

Funny. No.

Steph

I wanted her to come here, to hang with us.

Ashley

And what, she said she's busy?

Steph

She didn’t want to go to a gay bar.

Ashley

Aaaaaannd that calls for more drinks.

They order another round.

An hour later…

Ashley

Maybe she’ll come around.

Steph

I think I need to stop hoping for that.

Steph

She pulls me in when we’re alone and then drops me in the real world.

Steph

I can’t hide who I am — how I feel.

Ashley

Hell no.

Ashley

We’re here, we’re queer and…

Ashley loses steam.

Ashley

You know the rest.

Steph

I’m such an idiot.

Ashley

Stop beating yourself up. You never know—

The bar door swings open, distracting Ashley from her thought.

Ashley sees a woman walk in and look around.

Ashley

You never know…

The woman sees Steph and Ashley sitting together.

After an initial hesitation, she starts to walk over to Steph…

Who is still waiting for Ashley to finish her sentence.

Ashley smiles.

Ashley

You never know what could happen.

Steph

What are you talk—

Steph stops mid-sentence when she feels a tap on her shoulder.

She whirls around.

Maya

Hey you.
