Streetrat - Episode 7

A black sack is ripped off of Alex’s head.

Groggily, he begins to come to.

He struggles to get his bearings as the world comes back into focus.

He has no idea where he is.

That’s because he was drugged.

Goons hired by Jack, Ezra Mohammed’s scheming right-hand man, have drugged him and dragged him away from the gala.

They have him chained and bound in the basement of Mohammed Investments.

Goon #1

Alright streetrat—give us the goods.

Alex

You must be friends of Jack and Eduardo.

Goon #2

Nice to meet you.

Alex

Now that we’re friends, how about untying me?

Just then Eduardo, Jack’s assistant, enters.

Eduardo

That can be arranged. If you give us the goods on Milton Holt.

Alex

You’re out of your mind.

Eduardo

We set you up with this opportunity for a reason.

Eduardo

And you think you can just cut ties?

Alex

I don’t want your money.

Eduardo

Money went out the window when you disappeared on us.

Eduardo

Traitor.

Eduardo

Traitors need to be taught lessons.

Eduardo gestures to Goon #1, who punches Alex square in the stomach.

Alex

Milton Holt is a good person—he changed my life.

Alex

I’ll never tell you anything about him.

Eduardo

You are pathetic.

Eduardo

Do you think that if you play dress-up, an heiress will fall in love with you?

Eduardo

Do you think one designer suit can hide who you truly are?

Eduardo

You’re nothing but trash. You were born trash, and trash you’ll remain.

Eduardo

But it looks like you need a little more convincing.

Eduardo

Unfortunately, that’s not my area of expertise. I’ll leave them to it.

Eduardo saunters out as the goons continue to beat up Alex.

the next morning

Jack and Mohammed sit together at Mohammed Mansion, sharing a cup of coffee.

Jack

I’ve been thinking more and more about your situation with Jazz.

Ezra

What? That I’m never going to find her a proper husband?

Jack

She’s a difficult one.

Jack

But that’s why she needs someone who will know exactly how to handle her.

Ezra

And do you have someone in mind?

Jack slowly pours them both another cup of coffee.

Jack

Ezra—Jazz needs someone who understands her inside and out.

Jack

She’s distrustful.

Jack

She’s erratic.

Jack

She needs someone to ground her and keep her from flying off the handle.

Jack

She needs someone older and wiser. Someone like me.

Ezra

You? That’s crazy talk—you’re nearly twice her age.

Jack

Jazz is an old soul. She needs an older man.

Jack

And besides, don’t you want to know that she’ll be well cared for?

Jack

Who better to care for her than your oldest friend?

Ezra

I don’t know, Jack. I never wanted her to have an arranged marriage.

Ezra

I did want her to have some say.

Jack

You know Jazz and I have always had a connection.

Jack

This feels right, Ezra. Don’t deny it.

Jack

Besides—don’t you want Jazz to marry another Lebanese man?

This captures Ezra’s attention more than any previous argument.

Meanwhile, upstairs in Jazz’s bedroom,

Jazz looks at her phone.

Seven texts to Alex and no response.

She wonders: Was he leading her on?

Still, something isn’t sitting right with her...

At the same time, in the basement of Mohammed Investments,

Alex tries to recover from a rough night of beating.

Bruised and cut up, he groans, his body aching.

Eduardo enters, followed by his goons.

Eduardo

So, were they persuasive?

Eduardo

What’s it going to be, street trash?

Alex

What? Give you what I know so you can destroy Milton and his company?

Eduardo

Not destroy—acquire.

Alex

No—I’ll never give you anything.

Eduardo gestures to one of the goons, who dials a cell phone.

Goon #2 gags Alex so he’s unable to speak.

Goon #1

Hi Milton, this is Alex Marcourt’s brother.

Goon #1

He asked me to call you because he’s decided that today was his last day at your company.

Goon #1

He’s moving on—he hasn’t learned very much.

Goon #1

He thanks you for your time.

With a CLICK, Goon #1 hangs up the phone.

Goon #2 releases Alex’s gag.

Alex

What the hell was that?!

Alex

Prank phone calls!?

Alex

THAT’S your idea of revenge?

Eduardo

You don’t think we’re going to let you go back to work for him, do you?

Eduardo

The agreement was, we groom you for the internship,

Eduardo

You take the job and report back to us.

Eduardo

You’re unwilling to report back.

Eduardo

So I believe you no longer meet job qualifications.

Alex is silent.

Eduardo

Are thinking about what you’ve done?

Eduardo

In that case, the offer still stands.

Eduardo

Tell us what we need to know, and you can go back to the job.

Alex

Milton is my friend—I’ll never betray him.

Eduardo

Looks like he needs a little more convincing.

The goons unleash their full fury, beating Alex to a pulp.

Alex doesn’t flinch.

Alex

Give me your best shot—I still won’t speak.

Eduardo

Looks like he needs a little more incentive, boys.

Eduardo shouts to someone behind him.

Eduardo

Bring her in!

Two more goons enter holding a bound and wriggling Abby.

Alex

Abby!

Abby: Put me down, you oversized buffoons!

Alex

Let her go!

Eduardo

Oh, you two know each other?

Alex

Let her go—do not touch her.

Eduardo grabs at Abby’s face and pinches her cheeks between his thumb and middle finger.

Eduardo

So pretty. I’d hate to see something happen to such a pretty face.

Just then, one of the goons pulls out a knife.

Alex

No! Put it down!

Eduardo

But, you told us you would never betray him.

Alex

Stop!

Eduardo

You took something precious from us.

Eduardo

So we might just have to do the same.

Eduardo nods to the goon, who raises the knife high in the air...
