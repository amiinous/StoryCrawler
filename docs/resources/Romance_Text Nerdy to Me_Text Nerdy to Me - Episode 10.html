Text Nerdy to Me - Episode 10

After a delightful dinner of Chinese takeout, Erin and Will are curled on the sofa with Nymeria, browsing through Netflix.

The dog is currently obsessed with licking Will’s hand.

Erin

She likes you.

Will scratches Nymeria behind her ears until she makes a doggie O-Face.

Will

I like her too. She and Ghost are going to be best buds.

Will

I can tell.

Erin

Mmm.

Erin snuggles in.

Erin

This is so nice.

Erin

Just watching Netflix together.

Erin

Who knew we’d both dig Black Mirror?

Will

You just happen to have excellent taste.

Erin

As do you.

Will kisses her brow.

Erin

I’m so glad I answered your text that morning.

Will

Me too.

Erin

Can you imagine if I hadn’t?

Erin

We never would have met.

Will

Can’t even think about it.

Erin

What would you have done if I hadn’t?

Will shudders.

Will

Don’t even ask that.

Erin

Seriously.

Will

I probably would have continued to wander alone.

Will

In the wilderness.

Erin

You would have met someone.

Will

She wouldn’t have been you.

He pulls her closer.

Will

How about you?

Will

What would you have done if we hadn’t met?

Erin

Ehh. Probably would’ve slept around.

Will growls.

Erin

I’m kidding.

Erin does her most convincing warble.

Erin

I would have diminished and gone into the West…

Will

Oh my God, like Galadriel.

Will

You’re such a fantasy nerd.

Erin laughs.

Erin

Yeah, but YOU got the reference.

Will grins.

Will

I guess I’m a nerd too.

Erin

That’s why I like you so much.

Will

We’re a pretty good pair.

Erin

We are.

They cuddle in closer.

Erin

Have I mentioned how glad I am that we met?

Will

I believe so. But I could hear it again.

Erin

Why don’t I show you instead?

She kisses him gently.

The kiss deepens and the dog gently pads off the sofa.

They lose sight of the room as the heat rises between them.

But then…the front door bangs open.

Both Erin and Will leap up and quickly adjust various articles of clothing.

Erin

Tiffany! Kimmy!

Erin

I thought you weren’t going to be home tonight.

Kimmy

We weren’t.

Kimmy shuffles her feet.

Kimmy

The ah…work ended early.

Erin’s stomach lurches when she notices that Will is staring — fiercely — at Kimmy.

Will

YOU!

Kimmy grimaces and then laughs.

Kimmy

Yeah.

Kimmy

Me.

Kimmy shrugs.

Erin looks from Will to Kimmy.

Erin

What? What is going on?

Will points at Kimmy.

Will

That’s her.

Will

The girl from the bar.

Tiffany

Oh.

Will

SHE was the one I met.

Tiffany

My.

Will

The one who flirted with me.

Tiffany

God!

Will

She did this to me.

He rips back his sleeve where remnants of sharpie remain.

Erin stares at Kimmy and pales.

Bile rises in her throat.

Will and her sister?

How…horrifying!

Erin

Kimmy. You didn’t!

Tiffany

Oh my God.

Kimmy flashes a grin.

Kimmy

Of course I did.

Tiffany

Oh my God.

Tiffany

She did!

Tiffany pulls Kimmy in to a big squishy hug.

Tiffany

This is so awesome!

Erin

Oh lord. I’m so mortified.

Kimmy

Come on. Nothing happened.

Kimmy smirks.

Kimmy

Especially once he made a Jon Snow reference.

Erin frowns at Will.

Erin

I can’t believe you flirted with my SISTER.

Will

I didn’t even know you then!

Will

Besides, you didn’t tell me you had a twin sister.

Erin

We’re not twins.

Kimmy preens.

Will

You could be twins.

Tiffany

Trust me. They couldn’t.

Erin

Kimmy! How could you do this to me?

Kimmy

Do what? Find you the perfect guy?

Kimmy

I mean, five minutes into our conversation, I knew he was perfect for you.

Kimmy

But you’re such a stick in the mud—

Erin

Why does everyone keep saying that?

Kimmy

I figured you needed a nudge.

Kimmy

I just…helped things along.

Erin

So you pretended to be me.

Kimmy grins.

Erin

I am so mad at you right now.

Kimmy

Huh, I don’t see why.

Tiffany

I don’t either.

Tiffany jerks a thumb at Will.

Tiffany

You got him.

Will

It’s true. You did get me.

Will

Both of us — we got each other.

Erin

You’re not mad?

Will

How could I be mad?

Will wraps his arms around her.

Will

I’m holding you.

Erin

I guess when you say it like that, I can’t disagree.

Kimmy

Well, I’m thrilled you’re together.

Kimmy

And that Charlie is out of the picture.

Kimmy

After all, that was the plan all along.

Erin

Getting rid of Charlie?

Kimmy

No sis.

She hugs Erin.

Kimmy

Making you happy.

Kimmy

You deserve someone who gets your sense of humor.

Kimmy

Someone who lives in your bizarre fantasy world.

At the same time, Will and Erin bellow…

Erin

[Person 2] and IT’S NOT BIZARRE!

Kimmy

See? Perfect for each other.

The sisters share tender smile, then hug.

Kimmy pulls back and winks.

Kimmy

So now it’s time for you to get your ass in gear…

Kimmy

And find the perfect man for me.

Erin

You’ve set the bar pretty high, but…

Erin

I will do my best, sis.

Erin

I’ll do my best.
