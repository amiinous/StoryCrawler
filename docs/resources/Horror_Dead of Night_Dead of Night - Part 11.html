Dead of Night - Part 11

Dad

Christian, are you there yet?

Christian

Yes, sir. Our transport just pulled in.

Christian

This place is like a fortress.

Dad

It IS a fortress.

Dad

Impenetrable and self-sustainable.

Christian

How many of these facilities are there?

Dad

Two dozen.

Christian

So… if they each house a hundred thousand people…

Christian

Then roughly 1.2 million will survive this?

Dad

1.2 million true-blooded Americans. Yes.

Christian

I would have loved for Ada to have been one of them.

Dad

I have good news then.

Christian

She’s alive?

Dad

And in processing now. I took her there myself.

Christian

You left her there?!

Dad

Are you questioning me, boy?

Christian

I’m sorry, sir. It’s just that…

Christian

isn’t she too important?

Dad

She is.

Dad

But that’s hardly common knowledge.

Dad

If I don’t put her through protocols, it’ll bring unwanted attention.

Dad

And risk exposing the group.

Christian

I understand, dad.

Dad

Right now, her father is telling anyone who’ll listen that he’s innocent.

Dad

Fortunately, there are very few people who have access to him.

Christian

Does Colonel Woods know you set him up?

Dad

I don’t think he believes I’d ever betray him.

Dad

Even for this country.

Dad

He’s loyal that way.

Christian

He must suspect something since you didn’t bring Ada to Dallas.

Dad

I’ll know soon enough.

Dad

They’ve agreed to let me talk to him.

Christian

What are you gonna say?

Dad

That Ada will be safe

Dad

if he confesses to causing this disaster.

Christian

Will he do it?

Dad

I’ll make sure he knows I have her.

Christian

How did you get her to come in?

Dad

I promised to take her to him.

Christian

Will you?

Dad

Of course not.

Dad

But she doesn’t know that.

Dad

In the meantime, I’m putting her on the next transport to the facility you just pulled into.

Dad

I need you to keep an eye on her and keep her in the fold.

Dad

Understood?

Christian

Yes, sir.

Dad

Good. Because if this falls apart…

Dad

She’ll be our only leverage.
