For the Love of Pete - Episode 4

Ten minutes later Travis is dropping off Hayley and Pete at her apartment.

Travis

Thanks for taking Pete for the week.

Travis

I don’t know what it’s going to be like at home…

Hayley

It’s no problem. You know I’m crazy about this guy.

Hayley

And you need to focus on your dad.

Travis

Yeah.

Travis

I just hate leaving you after what happened today.

Hayley

Don’t worry. They caught the guy. Pete’s not in danger anymore.

Hayley

We’ll be fine. Go take care of your dad.

She opens her arms to offer him a hug.

He leans over and accepts her comfort.

Finally he moves away.

Travis

I’ll text you as soon as I’m off the road.

After another goodbye to Pete, who wags his stump, Travis takes off.

A few hours later, Hayley gets a call from Travis.

Travis

I’m here at home.

Travis

It’s bad.

Hayley

Shouldn’t he be in a hospital then?

Travis

My parents have a private physician and round-the-clock nursing staff.

Hayley

Oh.

Hayley

Wow.

Travis

I know. But…

Travis

Money doesn’t stop you from getting sick and possibly dying.

Hayley

Don’t think that way, Travis.

Hayley

Your mom needs you to be strong.

Later the night, Travis is sitting at the family table sharing dinner with his mother and his sister, Becca.

His father’s condition has stabilized for now.

Mrs. Callahan

So how’s your love life, Travis?

Travis

What? Fine.

Mrs. Callahan

I’ve heard that before.

Mrs. Callahan

I’d like you to meet the daughter of a friend of mine while you’re here.

Travis laughs and throws up his hands.

Travis

No thanks.

Mrs. Callahan

She’s very pretty. Her name is—

Travis

Mom, no. I’m…already interested in someone.

Becca

Who is it? Anyone important?

Mrs. Callahan

What’s her name? Who is her family?

Travis

You don’t know her. She works in Manhattan.

Mrs. Callahan

Oh, another working girl.

Mrs. Callahan

Just be sure she’s not after you for your money.

Mrs. Callahan

You’ve made that mistake before.

Mrs. Callahan

And I want to make sure, for the sake of this family, that you don’t make that mistake again.

Travis abruptly leaves the table.

Mrs. Callahan

Travis, where are you going?

Travis

I have to check on my dog.

Becca

You have a dog?

Becca

You know I’m allergic!

Travis

Luckily for you, I don’t live here.

He leaves the dining room and heads for one of the terraces for some air.

After pausing for a moment, he texts Hayley.

Travis

Help!

Hayley

What’s going on?

Travis

Every time I come back, I remember why I left.

Hayley

I’m sorry.

Hayley

Remember what you have to come home to.

His phone alerts him to a picture.

It’s a close-up of Pete, who’s looking at the camera with his big, brown, intelligent eyes.

Travis

Thank you for that 🙂

Travis

Any more pics?

Hayley

Sure! I have plenty.

Travis

Not of him. Of you.

Travis

At this moment.

Hayley

But my hair’s in a bun and I’m not wearing any makeup.

Travis

Please. I need to see a friendly face.

Hayley

Okay, hang on.

He receives the pic and opens it.

Travis

You’re so beautiful.

Hayley

You’re not bad yourself.

Travis

Have dinner with me when I get back.

Travis

So…we can talk some things over.

Hayley

Sounds serious.

Hayley

I’m smiling right now, just so you know.

Travis

I’m glad I amuse you.

Hayley

You do.

Hayley

It’s one of the things I like most about you.

Travis

Oh yeah?

Travis

What are the other things?

Hayley

Haha. I’m not going to sit here and tell you what I like about you.

Travis

Why not? It’ll be my turn next.

Travis

Deal?

Hayley

Fine.

Hayley

I like that you have charities and you’re active in them.

Hayley

And that you haven’t let money become your world.

Travis

Thank you for that.

Hayley

That’s it. That’s all you’re getting out of me tonight.

Hayley

Your turn, and then I have to walk Pete.

Travis

Okay.

Hayley

I’m batting my lashes at you btw.

Travis

I like that despite looking the way you do…

Travis

You remain real and honest.

Travis

I like how being with you feels.

Travis

But…

Hayley

But what?

Travis

My family. Calling them snobs is being kind.

Travis

To them, if someone has to work for a living it means they’re a failure.

Hayley

Oh. So what you’re saying is they wouldn’t like me.

Travis

Probably not. And they’ll let you know it.

Travis

And they’ll probably hint that you’re only interested in me because of my money.

Hayley

I see.

Hayley

And is that what YOU think?

Travis

No!

Travis

That came out wrong.

Travis

I just wanted to warn you…

Hayley

Well, it sure sounds unpleasant.

Hayley

For both me and you.

Hayley

Hey, I’d better get going.

Travis

Hayley, wait…

Hayley

You know what?

Hayley

I think we should keep our focus on Pete, and what’s best for him.

Travis

I thought that’s what we were doing.

Hayley

No. It was becoming about us.

Hayley

And that needs to stop.

Hayley

Like I told you before, I don’t have time for a man in my life anyway.

Hayley

I have to go.
