Off Limits - Episode 3

Kim and Jacey are sitting side by side at pedicure stations, sipping cucumber water.

KIM

So you gonna tell me about the guy?

JACEY

What guy?

KIM

You were way late, and you’re wearing the same dress you had on last night.

KIM

Reeks of walk of shame.

KIM

And every time someone texts, you jump on your phone.

KIM

Which usually means you’re falling fast and about to make another mistake.

JACEY

I’m not falling—

KIM

Wait. Tell me you’re not back with Chaz.

KIM

He was ADORABLE, don’t get me wrong.

KIM

But you need someone who can relate to you on an organic level.

KIM

Someone who understands the complications of dating a girl like you.

JACEY

Oh, I’m complicated now?

KIM

You know what I mean.

KIM

Your history is complicated, so you need a man to be simple and honest.

JACEY

There’s nothing simple about dating.

KIM

You need a guy who’s going to bypass all the usual games.

JACEY

That guy exists in this city?

KIM

I mean, I enjoy the game.

JACEY

Ugh.

KIM

But after everything you’ve been through, you get down to what’s real right away.

KIM

We need to find you a guy who’ll lay it all out on the table and say,

KIM

This is the real me. This is the real you. Let’s give this a try.

JACEY

This is a real guy we’re talking about, right? Not a cartoon?

KIM

And what kind of guy hits on his girlfriend’s best friend the second it’s over?

JACEY

Who hit on you?

KIM

Chaz. I told you that.

KIM

He asked me to dinner and drinks when I ran into him at that fruit market on Damon.

JACEY

Did I know this?

KIM

I could’ve sworn I told you.

JACEY

What a jerk!

KIM

I mean, it was less than twenty-four hours since he broke up with you.

KIM

So maybe I didn’t tell you because it was still so fresh.

JACEY

And the breakup came out of nowhere.

JACEY

I don’t care how good-looking a person is, they just don’t do that.

KIM

Well, he IS insanely good-looking.

JACEY

And he knows it.

JACEY

But out of curiosity…

JACEY

Is there anyone I’ve dated that you WOULD be interested in?

JACEY

Hypothetically, of course. Assuming no pact.

KIM

Hmmm.

She laughs.

KIM

Chaz.

JACEY

I’m being serious, Kim.

JACEY

What if the perfect guy comes along, and you can’t date him because I saw him first?

KIM

If a guy dates you when he’s perfect for me, then he’s not as perfect as he seems.

JACEY

Hypothetically.

KIM

No hypothesis necessary. Guys come and go. We come first.

JACEY

I know, but—

KIM

Some guys actually TRY to get between friends, like “I did them both!”

KIM

As if we’re some sort of prize for them to win.

KIM

Then we’re pitted against each other as competition.

KIM

And in the end, the guy walks away anyway.

KIM

Men. They’re all about one thing.

JACEY

Maybe not ALL of them.

KIM

Aha! There IS a guy.

JACEY

NO.

KIM

Are you going to see him tonight?

KIM

Let’s meet him for drinks.

JACEY

No!

KIM

What’s he like? Tell me.

JACEY

There’s nothing to tell. There’s no guy.

KIM

I know you. There is.

JACEY

It doesn’t matter. It’s over.

JACEY

It barely started, even.

KIM

You like him.

JACEY

I did, yeah.

JACEY

But I like everyone.

JACEY

I can’t tell someone’s a jerk until he’s proven it 800 different ways.

KIM

Let me meet him. If I agree with you, fine. Break it off.

JACEY

I already did. It’s for the best.

KIM

So you’re saying he’s less than adequate between the sheets.

KIM

Like that history teacher you dated for a while…What was his name?

Jacey laughs.

JACEY

Fred.

JACEY

That was an awful experience.

KIM

God, if he could’ve found a way to rock your world, he might’ve been the one.

KIM

And this guy?

JACEY

It doesn’t matter.

KIM

Did you sleep with him?

JACEY

If you must know, yes.

JACEY

And that’s all it was, and now it’s over.

KIM

Meaning that’s all he wanted?

KIM

Are you okay?

JACEY

It was my decision, and I’m fine.

JACEY

I feel bad that I hurt him—

KIM

Whoa. YOU broke up with HIM?

Jacey’s phone rings; she grabs it before Kim can see it.

KIM

Is that him?

Kim takes the phone from Jacey’s hand.

KIM

His name is Hercules?

Hercules is the code name Jacey has given Ty.

Jacey snatches the phone back from Kim and sends the caller to voicemail.

KIM

A Roman god, huh? Sounds promising.

JACEY

It’s over.

KIM

I don’t understand why, but…

JACEY

It’s over because I can’t go through another heartbreak right now.

JACEY

And I know that’s where this is heading.

KIM

It’s going to happen for you, you know.

KIM

You’re going to meet this amazing guy.

KIM

And he’s going to see all the incredible things that I see in you.

KIM

And he’s going to sweep you off your feet.

KIM

The real deal.

JACEY

I have zero faith in that possibility.

KIM

Mark my words.

KIM

I’m telling you. I foresee it.

JACEY

How are things going with Russ?

KIM

Eh.

JACEY

That well?

KIM

You know who I keep thinking about lately?

KIM

Who I just can’t get out of my head?

Jacey reaches for her cucumber water.

JACEY

Chaz?

KIM

Do you remember Ty?

Jacey chokes on a sip.

KIM

I think I’m going to give him a call.
