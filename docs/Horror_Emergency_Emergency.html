Emergency

911 Operator

911 What’s your emergency?

Caller

They’re...they’re not going to make it.

911 Operator

Who? Can you tell me where you are?

Caller

I’m in a really bad place that they created.

911 Operator

Alright Sir. You don’t see any signs that could tell me where you are, do you? Is everyone alright?

Caller

I’ll be gone when they get here. Have I pleased you now?

911 Operator

I’m not sure what that means Sir. Could you maybe tell me your name?

Caller

My name? They took that from me. They stripped me of it all.

911 Operator

Okay just tell me what happened. What did they do to you?

Caller

What they did? Like everyone doesn’t already know. It’s me, Jasper.

911 Operator

Alright Jasper is anyone hurt?

Caller

You’re so worried about them. I was too, but that had to stop. They only care about themselves.

911 Operator

Why is that Jasper?

Caller

They were selfish. They laughed as their children would play games imitating me. They joked about the life I gave them.

911 Operator

Jas-

Caller

They do not deserve to lay to rest in my land, but I will see to it that they burn in it. Only the innocent will walk with the king.

911 Operator

Burn? Is this happening at your house?

Caller

No. You don’t understand. The guilty die and the innocent walk with the prophet.

911 Operator

Do you think that you decide if someone goes to Heaven or Hell?

Caller

Well who else would?

911 Operator

Whichever God they choose to believe in?

Caller

Well who do you think I am?

911 Operator

Jasper.

Caller

That was the name chosen for me as a child. I am the Prophet.

911 Operator

Prophet? Were these people your followers – your flock? Did they disobey your teachings?

Caller

Yes, now you understand.

911 Operator

Okay. Do you think that it would be alright if I sent a few officers there to speak with them and get them on the right track? I’m sure you are a forgiving prophet.

Caller

I am, but they did this. I asked for guidance. And he told me to burn them so that they may be cleansed. And for my failure to lead them I will die and personally ask God for forgiveness.

911 Operator

So you plan on killing them and then yourself? Please tell me where you are?

Caller

You are having trouble tracing this call. He won’t allow it until my work is done.

911 Operator

Don’t do anything just yet. Just let me talk to you. Is he around?

Caller

It was already done before I called you, Nancy.

911 Operator

How do you know my name?

Caller

I am the Prophet. He speaks directly to me.

911 Operator

……

Caller

Nancy? You there? It was about 25 years ago remember? We were in love. It wasn’t puppy love like the adults were saying. It was real love. And you wanted out. You said you wanted to be on your own and I made that happen for you. Do you remember now?

911 Operator

You.

Caller

Yes?

911 Operator

Jasper Mulligan?

Caller

Yes Nancy. Do you remember now?

911 Operator

You took so many people with you when you left and we never heard from them again. They said they were following a higher power. They would find eternal happiness with you.

Caller

You could’ve come.

911 Operator

You took my parents! I was a child. I needed them.

Caller

I didn’t take them. They were loyal followers, right up until now.

911 Operator

Why? I was left with nothing.

Caller

Well I didn’t want you to miss out. Closure is what the outside world seeks so much of.

911 Operator

Jasper. Please.

Caller

It’s not over yet, sweet Nancy. I still have to finish with little Jacob. He would be a nice addition.

911 Operator

How do you know about my son? You stay away from him. I will find you.

Caller

Hold on.

Caller

Mommy?

911 Operator

Oh God. Jacob! Honey, it’s going to be okay. Mommy’s here.

Caller

Mommy why did you send a scary man to get me? I think he hurt Christy, for babysitting me.

911 Operator

Honey where are you? Do you see anything?

Caller

We’re at home still. Are you coming home soon?

911 Operator

I love you honey. Officers will be there so very soon. Just stay a brave little boy. Mommy will stay on the phone with you. Can you go hide somewhere for me?

Caller

Of course he can’t. I can be his father. We can be a family. I did this all for you.

911 Operator

Stay away from him or you will regret it.

Caller

But you asked for this Nance. You said you wanted them to burn for what they did. You gave me the orders remember.

911 Operator

I would never.

Caller

When we were kids. They would never let you do as you pleased. You said you wished they would just burn up like the bad people in hell. Those were your exact words. Well now they are.

911 Operator

I was a child then.

Caller

You are the one I did this for. Why didn’t you leave work already if you didn’t want this? You knew it was me…Nancy, you told me to do this.

911 Operator

I was a child then. I want you to stop it now.

Caller

No you don’t. I still have one more person to take with me.

911 Operator

Leave him alone.

Caller

I did Nance. He’s still at home, but you don’t seem worried about that.

911 Operator

Jasper, what do you want from me?

Caller

I want you to come with me. You see those chills you got when I said that? Was it from excitement?

911 Operator

No. I’m perfectly fine, because the police are on their way and I know -

Caller

They will find Jacob safe and sound. I’m with you now Nance. This was the plan all along, it just took a bit longer than expected.

911 Operator

What? W-what do you mean?

Caller

That’s a nice silk blue blouse Nancy. Did you wear it just to look good for me?
