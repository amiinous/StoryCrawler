The Intruder - Episode 8

Dad

Are you there?

Eddie

Yeah, Dad. I couldn’t really sleep.

Dad

That’s okay. The game recognizes that you were idle, so it counts as resting.

Dad

I think you should be at full power.

Eddie

I guess that’s good. What do I do now?

Dad

Here comes the fun part, and you need to be extra careful now.

Eddie

When don’t I need to be careful in this game?

Eddie

You’ve been saying that to me since I started.

Dad

It’s a dangerous game.

Eddie

I’m nervous.

Dad

That’s okay, but you can do this. Is your volume off?

Eddie

Yeah, it’s been off. I had it on vibrate.

Dad

I think that’s okay for now, but if it makes any sound at all, you might want to silence it completely.

Eddie

Okay. What else?

Dad

You still have the necklace?

Eddie

Yes, I’m wearing it.

Dad

Good. It should be fully charged now, so you’ll still be invisible.

Eddie

I kind of like the invisibility.

Dad

It’s the best, but the power in it varies.

Dad

You can’t rely on it totally. But it’s good for now.

Dad

Here’s what you need to do. You need to leave the storage area.

Dad

For the most part, you just need to move.

Dad

Don’t attack anyone. Attacking someone will show your location.

Dad

It’s best that you keep your location a secret as long as you can.

Eddie

Makes sense. What else?

Dad

When you leave the room in the castle, go to the right and keep walking down the hall.

Dad

After around the fifth doorway, there should be a couple of small doorways that lead to spiral staircases.

Dad

Take the second one. Go now and let me know when you get there.

Eddie

My heart is pounding.

Dad

You’ll be okay. Trust me. Just stay close to the wall. Walk quickly. Don’t make noise.

Dad

Orcs might be able to smell you, but just keep moving.

Dad

They won’t know where you are if you keep moving.

Eddie

Great to hear. Okay, I’m heading out now. I’ll text you when I get there.

Dad

Good luck

twenty minutes later

Eddie

OMG that was intense!!

Dad

You’re in the stairway?

Eddie

Yes, there was one orc who smelled me and started walking toward me.

Dad

But another orc called him at the last second.

Eddie

Yes, how did you know?

Dad

You keep forgetting that I played this game once or twice.

Dad

I know just about every single thing that happens by heart.

Eddie

There’s no randomness in the game?

Dad

There is, but the main script is the same.

Dad

It can change slightly based on what you do, but overall the game follows the same patterns.

Eddie

Interesting. So what do I do now?

Dad

You’re in the side stairwell. It’s a spiral, and you can take that all the way up to the top.

Eddie

Is that the end of the game? Where the Leader is?

Dad

Yes.

Eddie

What’s the catch?

Dad

The catch is that Mankill is going to appear right before you see the Leader.

Dad

You’re going to have to fight both of them.

Eddie

WHAT??

Dad

It’s okay. You can do it. You’ve been gaining fighting experience the whole game.

Eddie

Are you sure about this?

Dad

Yes. And besides, it’s the only way back home.

Dad

When you reach Mankill, here’s what you need to do. Memorize this pattern!

Eddie

What are you talking about?

Dad

You know how in your games there are patterns for doing things? Well, the same is true here.

Dad

For Mankill, it’s block left-right-right. Use your axe to strike his side.

Eddie

Seriously??

Dad

I’m not done! Block left-right-left-right-left-left. Use your axe to strike his shoulder.

Dad

And then, block right-left-right-right-left. Use your axe right to his head.

Dad

You MUST remember that pattern.

Eddie

HOW THE HECK AM I SUPPOSED TO REMEMBER ALL THAT???

Dad

Do it. Read this over and over on your phone until you have it.

Dad

Any false moves, and you have to start the sequence again and it just weakens you and makes him stronger.

Eddie

What’s the Leader going to be doing while I’m doing all this?

Dad

He’s going to wait while you fight Mankill and wait for you to get weakened before he appears and fights you.

Eddie

If I make it out of here alive, I’m done with video games.

Dad

I’ll hold you to that.

Dad

Now memorize the order. When you beat Mankill, the Leader will appear.

Dad

First thing is to grab the amulet off Mankill’s chest and use it to help deflect the Leader’s blasts.

Dad

That way you can get close enough to kill him.

Eddie

Sure, sounds like a piece of cake.

Dad

You’re going to do this! Now memorize and go.

Eddie

I hope so.

Dad

There is no hoping here. You’re going to do this!

Eddie

Okay. I’m going

I’m proud of you!!

You can!

The Leader’s there already?

You have Mankill’s amulet??

What’ll happen??

DO IT!!! NOW!!
