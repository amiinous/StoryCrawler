The Best Mistakes - Episode 6

Skye

Why did you bring Caleb up?

Scott

Huh?

Skye

You just mentioned him. Why now?

Scott

What are you talking about?

Skye

Don’t mess with me, Scott. I’m serious.

Skye

What kind of messed up game are the two of you are playing?

Scott

Skye, I have no idea what you are talking about.

Skye doesn’t respond.

She doesn’t know whether to believe him.

Scott

Skye. Come on, tell me what is going on.

Scott

What does Caleb have to do with any of this?

Skye doesn’t know what to do.

She needs help, so she texts her best friend Millie.

Skye

SOS! You are not going to believe this—

Skye

Somehow I wound up sexting with both Scott AND Caleb.

Skye

And yes, they’re roommates again.

Skye

It’s a long story. Will explain later. But I have no idea what I’m doing — or if they know about each other.

Skye

I mean, are they just being assholes?

Skye

Help! Why aren’t you texting me back?!

Skye

Hurry. Guy emergency here.

A couple of minutes pass and there’s still no response from Millie.

Scott, on the other hand, hasn’t given up.

Scott

Hello?

Scott

Skye!

Scott

Okay, talk about messed up. We’re having a conversation, and you just disappear.

Scott

What the hell?

Scott

Skye. Tell me what’s going on.

Skye debates whether to respond.

Eventually, she caves.

Skye

You really don’t know about Caleb?

Scott

I know he’s my roommate.

Skye

You know what I mean.

Scott

No, I don’t. That’s the point.

Skye

Okay, fine. I meant to text you earlier, but I accidentally wound up texting him.

Scott

Okay, so…

Skye

You’re seriously telling me you didn’t know anything about this?

Scott

No. But that means you were thinking about me too, doesn’t it?

Scott

I guess it’s true what they say. Great minds think alike.

Skye

There’s more.

Scott

What do you mean?

Skye

I mean, that Caleb and I got to talking. And…

Scott

And what?

Skye

He told me he used to have feelings for me.

Skye

And he asked me out.

Scott

He WHAT?!!

Skye

He asked me out.

Scott

That little shit. What did you say?

Skye

That I’d think about it.

Scott

How could you do that to me?

Skye

To you?

Skye

*You* never got in touch with me. I don’t owe you anything.

Scott

Okay, you’re right. I’m just pissed—

Scott

But not at you. At him. And at myself.

Scott

Do you know he’s the reason I didn’t get in touch with you in the first place?

Skye

He mentioned it.

Scott

Did he also tell you he hates me?

Skye

He didn’t go into detail, but he mentioned you’re not really friends anymore.

Scott

Understatement of the year. Skye, you can’t listen to a thing that guy tells you—

Scott

He’s full of it. He’ll say anything to get back at me.

Skye

For what?

Scott

It’s stupid. His girlfriend hit on me, and he blames me for it.

Scott

It’s not like it was my fault. Now I guess he’s getting his revenge.

Skye

He doesn’t seem like that type of guy.

Scott

Trust me. You don’t know him the way I do.

Scott

He’ll do anything to get back at me. Even if it means using you.

Skye

Why did you even tell him about us at the reunion if you hate him so much?

Scott

I still live with the guy, and I was kind of in a daze when I got back—

Scott

I hadn’t expected to feel what I felt for you.

Scott

When I saw him sitting in the living room, it just came out.

Skye

How much did you tell him?

Scott

I said that I had been a fool to ever let you go.

Skye

Really?

Scott

Yes. Skye, I don’t know what he told you…

Scott

But I was planning on seeing you right away.

Skye

But you didn’t.

Scott

Yeah, because Caleb made me doubt myself. He reminded me how much I hurt you.

Scott

He said I should make sure I knew what I really wanted first, to take some time and think.

Scott

I thought, wow, he’s actually giving me some decent advice—

Scott

I didn’t know he was trying to sabotage me and steal my girl.

Skye

But I’m not your girl.

Scott

Maybe you would have been if I hadn’t listened to Caleb. Now he’s the one asking you out.

Skye

That was a fluke. I texted him first. He didn’t reach out.

Scott

Big deal. He probably would have if he had your number, just to stick it to me.

Scott

You’re not going to go out with him, are you?

Skye

I don’t know.

Scott

Skye, don’t let some mistake screw us up. You wanted to text me.

Skye

I know, but it’s all so confusing.

Scott

It’s really not. You tried to reach out to me in the first place.

Scott

We should be together—

Scott

Remember how good we were?

Skye

I remember the bad stuff too. You hurt me.

Scott

And I’m sorry for that. But don’t just go out with Caleb to hurt me back.

Scott

I’d hate seeing you with him.

Scott

What kind of guy would do that to his roommate?

Scott

Skye, promise me you won’t talk to him again. It’s like a knife in the back.

Scott

Think about how you’d feel if I went out with one of your friends—

Scott

Or worse, one of your enemies.

Scott

I wouldn’t do that to you. Please, don’t do it to me.

Skye knows he has a point.

She’d hate it if Scott dated someone she knew.

But she’s still torn.

Scott

Let’s meet. Talk this out in person. Can I come over tonight?

Skye knows seeing him in person is dangerous.

She can never control herself around him.

Skye

I don’t know.

Scott

Please…

Skye takes a deep breath.

Skye

I’ll think about it.
