My Fake Boyfriend - Episode 7

Later that day, Hazel finds Jack during a free period.

Hazel

Jack, sit with me?

Jack

Of course.

Hazel

Can I…tell you something?

Jack

Shoot.

Hazel

I still haven’t really made any friends here.

Hazel

Most of the time, I just sit by myself and read my book.

Jack

Oh. Well, I mean, you’re new to school. It takes time.

Hazel

Yes, I am still new.

Hazel

You’re right. I haven’t gotten to know people that well yet.

Jack

At least you’re starting to know me.

Hazel

You’re the first one I’ve started getting to know. Which I actually don’t mind at all.

Hazel

We get along great.

Jack

I think so.

Hazel

I’m not keeping you from your friends, am I?

Jack

No. They won’t care.

Jack

I usually only hang out with Brady.

He looks over at a table where Brady and a couple of other guys are sitting.

He points him out to Hazel.

Hazel

That’s him? With the red hair?

Jack

Yeah, we’ve been friends since elementary school.

Hazel

Is he your best friend?

Jack

I guess he’s my best friend. Never thought about it like that.

Jack

He’s a good guy, though. He always looks out for me.

Hazel

You and Brady seem to be rare exceptions. Most of the other guys I’ve met…

Jack

I know. But it’s also different with girls and guys.

Jack

You’ve got to be careful.

Jack

Why are you looking like that?

Hazel

You’ll just laugh at me.

Jack

No, I won’t. Tell me what.

Hazel

OMG, I’m starving. That’s what.

Jack

You’re holding an apple and a cheese stick.

Jack

Is that your whole lunch?

Hazel

Yeah.

Jack

Will those be enough?

Hazel

I thought they would be, but my mind keeps obsessing over chocolate Pop Tarts.

Hazel

I’m addicted to those things.

Jack

You know they sell those in the school store, right? The little food mart next to the bookstore.

Hazel

What? No way. I always pass it, but I never look inside.

Jack

Wait right here. I’ll get some.

Hazel

You don’t have to! I’ve got these.

Jack

Go ahead and eat those, and I’ll get the Pop Tarts.

Jack

We can share them.

Jack

Stay here, so no one takes our spot. I’ll be right back.

Jack goes to the school store and on his way back, he gets a text.

Hazel

Jack!

Hazel

Hurry!

Jack

You can’t be that hungry. It didn’t take me that long.

Jack

Don’t die. 🙂 Eat the apple.

Hazel

It’s Mike.

Jack

What?

Hazel

Mike came over to our table.

Hazel

With all his friends, too!

Hazel

He keeps telling me to break up with you.

Jack

What an asshole!

Jack

I’m on my way.

Hazel

He’s being stupid. He keeps saying you’re too poor to date.

Hazel

I hate this guy so much!

Hazel

Please hurry!

Jack arrives, ready to face off against Mike.

Hazel

You’re here!

Jack

Mike, what the hell, man?

Mike

Yeah, I should say the same to you.

Mike

I told you to back off yesterday.

Hazel

What is he talking about?

Jack

That’s enough, Mike.

Mike

He didn’t tell you?

Hazel

What happened?

Jack

It doesn’t matter.

Mike

I told him at his dumb ass trailer home yesterday…

Jack

Dude, stop.

Hazel

Is…is that why you were busy? Mike went to your house?

Jack

Can we talk about it later?

Jack turns to Mike.

Jack

Listen, she’s not into you and you’re being an asshole.

Jack

Just stop. Seriously.

Mike

What? She can’t know the truth?

Mike

You embarrassed about your house?

Mike

Because I would be.

Mike

You may as well light a match and set it on fire.

Jack

Whatever.

Mike

Hazel, you seriously want to go out with this guy? He’s got nothing to offer.

Mike

He’s going nowhere.

Mike

You should come out with me this afternoon.

Hazel

No way! Not after what you did the other day.

Mike

You’re the one that jumped out of my car.

Hazel

You told me to get out if I wasn’t going to kiss you.

Mike

That’s ridiculous! You took me too literally. I was just joking.

Mike

You shouldn’t have jumped out.

Hazel

You drove off when I did! I was lucky Jack came by.

Mike

Meeting Jack was the unluckiest thing that could happen to you.

Mike

Hazel, don’t go out with this guy…

Mike grabs Hazel by the arm.

Hazel

Let me go!

Mike

Are you crazy? Just come talk to me without this guy around.

Hazel wrenches her arm free. Mike lunges after her again, trying to grab her a second time.

Mike

I just want to talk to you. I won’t hurt you.

Hazel

Then stop grabbing me!

Jack lunges, catching Mike square in the jaw.

Then he serves up another punch on the other side of Mike’s face.

Hazel

Jack!

Mike and his two friends dive in, punching Jack.

And Jack goes down.
