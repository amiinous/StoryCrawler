Not me.

As the two continue to argue,

Neither Trevor nor Deirdre sees Helen and Matt.

They flatten themselves against the side of the cabin,

Inching closer to hear every word.

Helen pulls out her phone, and covering the light, dials 911.

Deirdre

Listen, how about we strike a deal?

Deirdre

I’ll give you a few more minutes of life, if you tell me...

Deirdre

How?

Deirdre

How did you figure all this out?

Now Trevor catches sight of Helen and Matt edging closer to Deirdre.

Helen catches Trevor’s eye, and gives him a slight nod,

Silently begging Trevor to keep Deirdre talking.

Trevor

Do you really want to know?

Deirdre

Yes! I thought I hid my tracks well.

Trevor

Not as well as you thought.

Trevor

First, I noticed—

With one fluid motion, Helen lunges at Deirdre,

Knocking the gun out of her hand.

Simultaneously, Matt grabs Deirdre and points his own gun at her.

Two hours later, Deirdre is in jail.

She has confessed to hiring a hit man to kill Frank Cassidy.

And then killing the hit man after she refused to pay him.

Helen and Matt have brought Trevor back to be reunited with his mother and Chloe.

After the tears, joy and excitement wane a bit,

Curiosity takes hold.

Trevor explains that he saw Deirdre stealing from the lodge

And then, after following her in his car,

Saw her lose all the money at a nearby casino.

Anna interrupts him.

Anna

Why didn’t you just go to the police with what you found out?

Trevor

I was about to. But I felt I had to tell Frank first.

Trevor

But he refused to believe that Deirdre had done anything wrong.

Trevor

He got mad at me for accusing her of stealing.

Trevor

After we argued, he walked out of the office and out the back door toward the lake.

Trevor

I followed him.

Trevor

I wanted to tell him that I had no choice but to go to the police.

Trevor

But before I could get to him, this guy comes up and shoots him down!
