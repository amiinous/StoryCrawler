Why?

but then you end up asking for forgiveness and now wanting to be buddies?

and bc I've never really met you in person before but...

Emma smiled. But then quickly realized her inability to trust Jeremy.

Confusion enveloped her as she wondered whether or not he was being real.

Jeremy and Emma had met before. Once - a long time ago.

He just doesn't remember their encounter.
