Law School Romance

Sadie O’Riley – a beautiful redheaded law school student – eyed the boy in a suit across the party.

He was devilishly handsome but she didn’t know him.

She flicked a lock of hair from her eyes as she opened another bottle.

She was bartending for the fundraiser she had organized. She whipped out her phone.

Sadie

Who’s that cutie in the suit in the corner?

Matt

Who knows … I’m kinda busy!

Her friend Matt was swaying in a corner chatting up a brunette. He was more than tipsy.

Sadie

I don’t think he’s a law student

Matt

Well pull down ur shirt and go talk to him!!

Sadie

Can’t, working!

Matt

Ugh ur the worst

Matt slowly waltzed over to the boy.

Wine spilled over the glass Sadie was pouring into as she strained to see.

They seemed to be having a conversation. Matt pointed at her.

The boy glanced over to her. Sadie groaned. Matt giggled and shuffled away.

Sadie

WHAT did you just tell him?!

Matt

Don’t get ur panties all twisted

Matt

His nametag says Proctor

Sadie

That’s a weird name

Matt

Whatevs I’m done being ur slave

Sadie

Wingman not slave dummy

A smartly dressed girl asked Sadie for a glass of soda. A blue badge flashed on her breast.

It said Proctor.

Sadie hit her forehead.

Sadie

U idiot, Proctor isn’t his name – he’s working at the event!

Matt

Oh one of those university employees making sure it’s all good

Sadie

Yeah. So what did you tell him??

Matt

I guess that u thought he was cute

Sadie didn’t know if she wanted to laugh or cry.

Sadie

WORST. WINGMAN. EVER.

Her curiosity got the best of her though.

Sadie

So?? What did he say?

Matt

‘Thanks for letting me know’

Matt

I think he’s a douchebag

Sadie

I think ur right. Why are the pretty ones always the horrible ones as well?

Matt

Cuz we drink alot

Sadie was about to send a cute drinking emoji when someone coughed.

She looked up straight into the boy’s green eyes.

Boy

Can I ask for a ginger beer?

Sadie

How about a glass of wine?

Boy

Can’t, as a Proctor at this event I’m not allowed

He paused.

Boy

I’m Jacob by the way.

Sadie was suddenly aware of her sweaty self. She had been working hard.

Sadie

I’m Sadie, one of the organizers

Jacob

You’ve done a great job

As she handed him the glass, their fingers touched briefly.

He paused, looking at her.

She played with her hair.

He looked like he was about to say something when an inebriated professor nearly slammed into the bar.

Drunk Professor

Hey Sadie my love, give us another!

Ugh.

Jacob was gone, back to his post.

When the party was over, he thanked her once more.

She asked if he would be working at the Law Revue the following week.

No.

Just before he left, he turned and looked at her. And smiled.

Sadie

Did he tell u anything else about him??

Matt

Who?

Sadie

Goddamn Matt were u that drunk? Jacob!

Matt

Nah. If he didn’t invite u out who cares

Sadie

I tried googling ‘Jacob proctor’

Matt

Whaddya find?

Sadie

Lots of photos of bald men with the last name Proctor

Matt

Lol. I’ll find u someone new

It was the last night of Law Revue. Sadie was tired.

There had been dances and songs, sex jokes and boozed up students rubbing against each other.

There had been proctors, but no Jacob.

She was sitting on a bench just breathing.

Her heart nearly stopped when a familiar voice spoke into her ear.

Voice

I know you

Sadie

Jacob! I thought u weren’t working this event

Jacob

So did I. But I had to correct a mistake

Sadie

What mistake?

Jacob

Not asking you for your number

Sadie forgot she was tired.

Sadie

Why didn’t you?

Jacob

Your drunk friend came to me and tried to set us up

Sadie

Ah Matt. Yeah, sorry about that

Jacob

I thought it was a prank …

Sadie

I thought you were just too much of a pussy to ask

Jacob

Possibly … I’m pretty shy

Jacob

It wasn’t very enticing

Sadie

Well I’m sorry that I wasn’t enticing

His eyes lit up. He seemed to enjoy her fire

Jacob

Oh YOU were, but your friend was doing you a disservice

Ugh. Mental note: never ever use Matt as wingman again. Especially when he’s drinking.

Jacob

I tried googling you but ‘Sadie law revue’ came up with nothing

The irony.

Sadie

So you signed up to work just so that you would see me again?

Jacob

Pretty much

He smiled.

She smiled.

Jacob

So drink tomorrow?

Sadie

Yessir!

Jacob

One condition though

Sadie

What’s that?

Jacob

Matt stays at home this time.

She flipped her hair back.

Sadie

Deal!
