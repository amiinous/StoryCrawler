Textually Frustrated - Episode 1

Unknown

Hey, I had a good time tonight. Bars aren’t usually my thing…

Unknown

But it was great meeting you.

Ember wonders whether to answer the mystery texter…

And then decides to entertain herself.

Ember

Yeah. I had fun too.

Unknown

I’m glad to hear that. I’ll admit, I had a hard time figuring out if you liked me.

Ember

I totally like you! I was just a little distracted by the Santa stripper contest.

There’s a long pause.

Unknown

Ha. You’re funny.

Ember chuckles to herself.

Ember

Oh. I think you were in the bathroom for that part. You missed a good show.

Ember

Contestant #3 won even though #1 had a pop, lock, and drop move—

Ember

I think it was rigged.

There’s another long pause.

Unknown

I’m confused. You seem different—

Unknown

Are you okay?

Ember

I’m totally messing with you. You have the wrong number.

Ember

😂 Sorry. I couldn’t resist.

Unknown

Oh crap! Hang on, lemme check the paper she wrote it on.

Unknown

860-593-7121?

Ember

Yeah, that’s me. She gave you a fake number. Sorry, dude—

Ember

Guess your bar babe wasn’t into you.

Unknown

I’m a girl, not a dude.

Unknown

Anyway, sorry to bother you. Hope I didn’t interrupt your night.

Ember

Actually, you’re the most exciting thing that’s happened in a while lol.

Unknown

Wow. And I thought my life was sad. I’m Wren, btw.

Ember

I’m Ember. Sorry your date didn’t work out. Then again, the whole bar thing is kind of cliché…

Ember

I mean, what kind of engagement story would that make?

Ember

“We met on a magical night, filled with the scent of sweat and old vomit…”

Wren

Hahaha

Ember

“I peered across the room and there she was with cigarette smoke billowing around her. Our gazes locked and time stopped…”

Ember

“Until the drunk guy next to her spilled his drink down her shirt.”

Wren

Lmao. Too bad you weren’t my real date—

Wren

You sound more interesting than she was.

Ember

I guarantee I’m more interesting.

Ember looks down at the IV in her arm. Her chest is suddenly full.

Ember

I doubt I’m your type though.

Wren

Why would you think that?

Ember

Never mind.

Wren

Oh jeez. It’s midnight! You work night shift or something?

Ember

I’m always up. It’s hard to sleep where I’m currently living.

There’s another long pause.

Wren

You’re making me curious about you…

Wren

But I need to crash. I work a 12-hour shift tomorrow.

Ember

Ok. Sorry again about your date.

Wren

It’s all good. I’ll try not to bother you again.

Ember

Bother away!

Ember

Seriously. My life is boring. I liked talking to you.

Wren

🙂

Wren

Goodnight.

Ember

🌙

The next morning, Ember stares at her phone, wondering if she should text Wren.

She spent all night thinking about her.

Deciding she’ll sound too desperate, she tries to ignore the hums and beeps of the dialysis machine next to her and goes back to sleep.

Five minutes later, the sound of an incoming text wakes her.

Wren

Sorry if I’m bothering you. I can’t get you out of my head.

Ember smiles down at her phone.

Wren

Any chance you’re still bored? 😛

Ember

Always.

Ember

So where you working your 12-hr shift?

Wren

The hospital. I’m a nurse.

Ember closes her eyes, trying not to be disappointed.

She doesn’t exactly need more medical people in her life.

Wren

Your area code is local. You live in CT?

Ember

Yeah.

Wren

You’re not gonna tell me where, are you?

Ember

Maybe at some point 🤷‍ 🤫

Wren

That’s ok. I’m gonna figure you out eventually.

Ember

Why? Why me?

Wren

You’ve got me intrigued. I love puzzles.

Ember

Ha. Good luck then 👍 😉

Wren

Hint?

Feeling brave, she snaps a picture of her right hand waving at the camera and sends it.

She’s careful to keep her left hand with the IV out of the frame.

Wren

Hmm…that wallpaper in the background looks familiar. Not the kind of picture I wanna see though…

Wren

How about your face?

Heart beating in her chest, Ember decides to go for it.

She snaps a photo of half her face then sends it.

Wren

Daaaaamn. You’re beautiful!

Ember

😊

Ember

Your turn.

Wren sends a photo.

Ember

Jeez. I don’t understand why that girl at the bar gave you a fake number!

Ember

You’re seriously gorgeous.

Wren

🙂 You’re sweet. And I know that wallpaper but I can’t place it right now.

Wren

Another hint?

Ember

Let’s get to know each other first.

Wren

Ok. What can I tell you?

Wren

How about where I work — here’s a pic.

Wren sends a picture of a standard Emergency Department.

Ember

Been there done that.

Wren

You’re a nurse too?

Ember

No.

Wren

Doctor?

Ember

Definitely not.

Wren

Hmm. Familiar with the ER but not in the medical field…

A nurse walks into Ember’s room, holding a dixie cup.

Ember’s head snaps up at the sound of her voice.

Nurse

Medication time.

Ember’s cell phone pings.

Wren

Ambulance driver?

Ember

No. Gotta go — bbs.

A half hour later, Ember texts Wren.

Ember

Any more guesses?

Wren

Do you run the animal-assisted therapy program?

Wren

I could see you bringing the dogs around the floor 🐶

Ember

I wish! But no. No pets for me.

Wren

No pets? Idk what I’d do without my cat.

Ember

I had a fish once. It didn’t live long.

Ember

Poor Chicken.

Wren

You named your fish Chicken?? 😂

Ember

It seemed like a good idea at the time.

Wren

I really wish I could see you.

Ember

Want another photo?

Wren

No. In person.

Ember sighs and looks at machines around her.

Wren

We should go for coffee.

Wren

I promise I’m not a serial killer.

Ember

That’s exactly what a serial killer would say.

Wren

We can meet somewhere public.

Ember

Idk. I’ll think about it.

Wren

🙂 I’ll win you over.

Wren

I haven’t even brought out my A game yet.

Ember

I’ll have to brace myself for your charm.

Wren

Damn straight 😉

Ember

There’s nothing straight about me.

Wren

Lmao. That makes two of us.

There’s a long pause. Ember feels nauseas. She wonders…

Is it a bad idea to put her body through this emotional stress, given the current state of her health?

And besides, what are the chances this thing between them won’t end in heart break?

Wren

Hey, I have another guess.

Ember

Yes?

Wren

What if you don’t WORK in a hospital?

Wren

Have you been a patient in one?

Ember’s heart races. She thinks for a moment about how best to respond.

Ember

Who hasn’t?

Wren

😏 I’m getting closer, aren’t I?

Ember

No comment.

Wren

Heh. Okay my break is over. Back to work!

A young, female aide delivers lunch to Ember. The food’s bland like always, but she forces it down.

Ember

Hope your lunch was yummier than mine 🤢 Ttyl!

Wren

Tty soon! Very soon, if I have any say in it.

Wren

Btw, I have another guess. Later though…

Ember grins at her phone.

The aide chuckles.

Aide

Are you talking to a crush or something?

Ember startles and places her phone face down on the bed.

Ember

What? No!

Aide

Okay, okay. It’s just…I’m sure that’s exactly how I look when my boyfriend texts me.

Ember

How…how do I look?

Aide

All googly-eyed.

Ember blushes.

Aide

It’s good. I see a lot of depression around here. It’s nice to see someone smiling.

The reminder of her location makes Ember frown.

The aide looks around the room.

Aide

You on the list for a transplant?

Ember nods.

Aide

Well, good luck. Let me know if you need anything—

The aide winks.

Aide

I’ll hook you up with extra Jell-O next time if you want.

Ember

Thanks.

Ember looks back at her phone, wondering if it would be easier on them both if she and Wren…

If she got it over with and blocked her number.
