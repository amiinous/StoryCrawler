Flipping the Mattress - Episode 3

Two days later, Zain has just helped Callie move her couch and some other furniture.

Of course she needed to repay him –

Especially since she has made clear that their relationship is about bartering their services.

They’re lying in bed together, on the preferred firm side of the mattress.

Zain is slowly stroking Callie’s forehead.

Zain

You’re so beautiful.

Callie

Mmmmmmmm. Thank you.

Callie

And you have wonderful equipment.

Zain

I’m glad you like it.

Zain

Callie, I was wondering…

Callie

Mmmmm. What?

Zain

Would you like to come over for dinner on Saturday night?

Zain

I make a mean cassoulet.

Callie props herself up on her elbow and looks searchingly at Zain.

Callie

Zain, that kind of sounds like a date.

Callie

Are you saying you want to be more serious?

Zain

Maybe.

Zain

Well, yes.

Zain

And yes, I’m asking you out on a date.

Callie

Zain, no. You’re ruining this.

Zain

Why don’t you want to go on a date with me?

Zain

Neither of us is seeing anyone.

Zain

I mean…you aren’t seeing anyone, are you?

Callie

No.

Zain

So why not see if this thing between us can be more serious?

Callie

Because I like what we have.

Callie

No strings. No hurt feelings. Just great sex.

Zain

But what if one of us starts dating someone else?

Callie

We’ll cross that bridge when we come to it.

Callie

For now let’s just have fun.

Callie

I mean, it doesn’t get more convenient than this.

Zain looks hurt.

Then he gets up and starts putting on his clothes.

Zain

I… I think I’ll go home now.

Callie

I’m sorry, Zain.

Zain

I’m sorry too.

Callie

I just…I need to be really clear about where I stand.

Zain

You’ve made yourself very clear.

Zain

Goodnight, Callie.

Callie

Bye, Zain.

One week later, Callie gets a text.

Zain

Do you know anything about TV’s?

Callie

What do you mean?

Zain

I mean, my TV isn’t working.

Zain

I thought that since you’re so good with computers…

Zain

You might be good with TV’s, too.

Callie

I’ve been known to fix a TV or two in my time.

Zain

Would you mind coming up and taking a look?

Callie

I’ll be right there.

Ten minutes later, Callie is in Zain’s apartment.

Zain

I hope you can fix it.

Zain

The big game is about to start.

He leads her to the TV.

Zain

See? Even when I turn it on, the screen is blank.

Callie presses a single button and the picture appears on the screen.

Zain

You’re a miracle worker!

Callie

It was an easy fix.

She takes a step closer to him, examining his face carefully.

Callie

Too easy.

Callie

I have a feeling you switched it to that different mode on purpose.

Zain

I’m so busted.

Callie

I knew it!

Zain

I’ll admit it was a ploy.

Zain

Because I missed you.

Zain

I mean…I missed the sex.

Callie

I’ve been missing the sex too.

Callie

But you remember what I said.

Zain

I remember.

Callie

I don’t want anything serious.

Zain

I know. And the more I think about it, the more I realize you’re right.

Zain

We have a good thing, and it’s best to keep it casual.

Callie

I’m so glad you feel that way.

Callie

The thing is, I just graduated from college, so I feel like I have my whole life to be serious with someone.

Callie

I don’t want to be tied down…yet.

Zain

Neither do I.

Zain

Except if the ties are made of silk.

Zain

And I’m wearing a blindfold.

Callie

Zain!

Callie

I had no idea!

Callie

Did you want to try that?

Zain

Maybe…

Zain

But tonight, since you fixed my TV…

Callie

Yeah, right.

Zain repeats himself, louder this time.

Zain

Since you fixed my TV,

Zain

This night should be about want YOU want.

Zain

What do you want, Callie?

Zain

You know I owe you…

Zain

Big time.

Callie

I know you do.

Callie

And I need you to take care of your debt right now.

Callie

How about that thing you did last time?

Callie

That swirly thing…

Callie

That’s what I want.

Zain

Then that’s what you’re getting.

Zain

And then…

Zain

I’m going to surprise you.

Zain

And I won’t be stopping there.

Zain pulls Callie toward him and leads her into the bedroom.

Callie

Wait!

Zain

What?

Callie

I thought you wanted to watch the big game.

Zain

No game could be as exciting as THIS.

He pulls her to him, capturing her mouth in a kiss, and they fall onto the bed.

Two hours later, Callie has a sudden idea.

Callie

You know, Zain.

Zain

What?

Callie

I don’t like where my couch is after all.

Zain

After I worked so hard to move it?

Callie

Yes. I’m afraid it’s in a terrible spot.

Callie

Can you help me move it back where it was?

Callie

Maybe tomorrow night?

Zain

I’ll be there.

Zain

But first… let me finish repaying my debt for tonight.

Callie

Mmmmmmmm.
