Bedroomies - Episode 1

Adam

Did you leave something on the sofa before you went on your trip?

Jenna plops into an airport lounge seat, her pulse pattering.

Jenna

What do you mean?

Adam

I found this cute little rubber thing that’s connected to a battery.

Adam

Huh. It also vibrates.

Jenna

OMG.

Adam

Imagine my surprise…

Adam

When I came back from my trip to find this special gift from my roomie waiting for me!

Adam

What’s it for anyway?

Jenna

Stop teasing!!!

Jenna

You know damned well what it’s for.

Jenna

Can you just put it away and forget this happened?

Adam

But I have so many questions…

Adam

When do you use this? How often?

Jenna

Adam…

And who do you think about when you use it?

Adam

Ok. No more teasing. Purged from my mind.

Jenna

Who do you think I’m thinking about?

Jenna

Hah! Just joking.

Adam

Were you really?

Jenna

Yeah…

Jenna

Sorry. I shouldn’t have joked.

Jenna

I know the fastest way to alienate a roomie is to do exactly that.

Adam

But what if that roomie is constantly out of town when you’re out of town?

Adam

What if you don’t see him?

Adam

And what if he’s moving out after his condo is refurbished in a little over a week?

Adam

Why did you stop typing? What were you just about to say?

Jenna

The same kind of thing I would have said to you a few years ago in college.

Jenna

You mercilessly teased me then too.

Jenna

Usually about when I’d get a boyfriend.

Adam

That’s because you never seemed interested in anything but math.

Adam

I was trying to get you out of your shell.

Jenna’s skin flushes.

Jenna

Hey would you look at that—my plane is boarding.

Adam

Don’t dodge this.

Adam

You need to explain that comment you made.

Adam

Who DO you think about when you use that bunny?

Jenna

No one.

Adam

Jen…

Jenna

Jeez, Adam, what do you want me to say?

Jenna

That I’m thinking of YOU?

Adam

Why not me?

Jenna

Because you’re…you! And I’m me!

Adam

I don’t even know what the hell that means.

Jenna

It means we’re coworkers who share a living space for now.

Jenna

You’re a former frat boy and I’m the nerd who tutored you.

Adam

All I hear are excuses.

Jenna

Huh?

Adam

You can’t tell me you’ve never walked by my room and thought about me.

Adam

Because I’ve done that with you.

Jenna nearly drops the phone.

Adam

Last week you came home from a trip and went to sleep just as I was getting out of bed.

Adam

I imagined your gorgeous black hair spread over the pillow.

Adam

I thought about what you might be wearing and hoped you didn’t have on anything at all.

Jenna

STOP teasing me. Guys like you don’t…you know.

Adam

What?

Jenna

You’ve got a lot of women to choose from, all right?

Adam

And you still think of yourself as a nerd no guy ever notices.

Adam

You really have no idea, do you?

Jenna

I can’t believe we’re having this conversation.

Adam

Do you really want me to stop?

Another minute drags by.

Adam

You don’t!

Adam

Listen—what if I made you a promise?

Jenna

What?

Adam

That if you let me talk to you like this…

Adam

Then if we run into each other in your place, none of this will ever come up.

Adam

Our schedules don’t allow us to be home at the same time to interact anyway, but still.

Jenna

Are you proposing that we completely ignore your recent discovery?

Jenna

Because I’m SO up for that.

Adam

That’s not what I’m proposing at all.

Adam

I want you to tell me all about last night.

Jenna peers around her in the crowded lounge as boarding starts.

Jenna

Why should I do that?

Adam

Because I want to see this side of you.

Adam

And we’re halfway across the country from each other, so there’s nothing to lose.

Jenna smiles as heat consumes her, years of fantasies rushing her.

Adam

😈

What the hell?

Jenna

Last night, I might’ve poured myself a glass of amaretto over ice and put on a movie.

Adam

Which one?

Jenna

Really? You want those nitty gritty details?

Adam

I want everything.

Jenna

Whatever. It was something from Netflix’s steamy section.

Adam

Of course it was. And…?

Jenna

Um…are you asking what I was wearing?

Adam

Yes I am.

A few loud heartbeats mark the seconds.

Jenna

Well, I have this nightie. It’s white, sweet, and innocent. A baby doll gown.

Adam

Where is it?

Jenna

DON’T YOU DARE!

Adam

Lol. No worries. I won’t go into your room.

Adam

I’m sitting on the sofa right now, waiting for you to continue.

Adam

And I’m still waiting…

Jenna

This is really strange to be telling this to you in the middle of hundreds of people.

Does that turn you on?

Is that a yes?

Okay. Yes.

Nice to hear. Go on.

Jenna

About my nightie?

Yeah. Because you wear a lot of business suits these days.

Adam

Tell me how it felt to wear something hot like that.

Jenna

You’re going to make me say things I don’t usually say to anyone, aren’t you?

Adam

Damn straight I am.

Adam

I always knew there was a little monster behind the nerd who tutored me in college.

Adam

Show her to me, Jen.

Jenna can end this right now…

But something’s telling her to let it get even hotter…
