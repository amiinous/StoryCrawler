Tough it Out - Episode 5

Jax

Lynn — seriously, what the hell?

Lynn puts Drake back in the seat.

Lynn

I can explain.

Foxy

This better be good.

Foxy

I can’t believe this — I trusted you.

Lynn

And you can still trust me.

Jax

You have five minutes—

Lynn

I’ll only need two.

Lynn nods to Drake.

Lynn

I was taking him out of the chair because we don’t need him anymore.

Lynn

I know who’s supplying those other guys with their drugs.

Jax

Who is it?

Jax

I’m gonna make them wish they were dead.

Lynn

Exactly.

Lynn

That’s why we need to target the distributor — not this guy.

Lynn

This guy no longer matters.

Lynn

The group he belongs to doesn’t even matter.

Jax

What?

Lynn glances at Foxy.

She doesn’t want to out her friend…

But the alternative — letting Drake die - would kill her.

They’ve been through so much together.

And she…cares about him. More than she’s willing to admit.

He had saved her life on the last mission.

Now it was time for her to save his.

Lynn

Because there’s someone dirty — right in this room.

Lynn

Isn’t there, Foxy?

Foxy looks surprised and steps back.

Foxy

I don’t know what you’re talking about.

Lynn holds up the ring.

Lynn

You got this ring as a gift, didn’t you?

Lynn

It was a gift from your boyfriend…

Lynn

The one who’s orchestrating this little turf war.

Foxy glances around.

Foxy

I don’t know what you’re talking about.

Lynn

Your boyfriend gave you this ring, didn’t he?

Foxy

Yeah, so?

Lynn

He gave it to you as a thank you for helping him establish his group in L.A.

Lynn

You pretended to be one of us and led us in the wrong direction…

Lynn

While he expanded his territory and sold his rapture on our streets.

Foxy

What? That’s — that’s not true.

Lynn turns to Jax.

Lynn

You had a feeling about her.

Lynn

Remember what you said to me?

Lynn

Watch out for Foxy — after all, foxes work alone.

Lynn

You said foxes are cunning and heartless.

Lynn

And now here she is — helping someone else sell rapture…

Lynn

Right in our territory.

Foxy

No, you have it all wrong.

Foxy

I didn’t know about the rapture. He was using me to—

Jax

You little bitch.

Jax

Get her!

Chaos ensues but before anyone can touch her…

Foxy jumps over Jax and hurries out the door.

Jax

You two, come with me.

Jax

The rest of you, stay here with Lynn and kill the prisoner.

Lynn

Kill him?

Jax

Yes, we want to send a message to anyone who tries to sell in our territory.

Jax

Nobody fucks with me.

Lynn whips out her phone as Jax and his entourage leave.

Lynn

Ruby — send backup. STAT!

Ruby

What’s going on?

Lynn

I found Drake.

Lynn glances up from her phone to see Drake being secured to the chair once more.

Her eyes widen —

Two of the toughest members, Jagger and Brock, each have their hands on him.

Lynn

They’re going to kill him!

Ruby

Hang tight. The cavalry is coming.

Lynn

Hurry!

Panic seizes Lynn’s chest as she hears Brock’s menacing voice fill the room.

Brock

How should we kill ‘em boss?

Jagger

I vote for nice and slow.

Lynn

No.

Lynn pockets her phone.

Lynn

We need to wait.

Brock

Why? You heard Jax’s orders.

Jagger takes out a knife.

Jagger

I’m gonna cut him first.

Lynn

No.

Lynn realizes she’s hopelessly outnumbered.

There are at least ten other gang members in the room.

Jagger runs the blade across Drake’s cheek.

Drake stiffens in pain as blood drips down his skin.

Lynn

Shit.

Lynn pulls out her gun.

Lynn

Nobody move.

Brock

She’s got a gun…in THIS room?

Brock

But the boss doesn’t want guns…

Everyone turns toward Lynn.

Jagger

Who the hell are you?

Lynn

The bitch who’s about to arrest you.

Lynn shows him her badge.

Lynn

Nobody move.

Brock

She’s a goddamn Fed — get her!

The gang quickly overtakes Lynn.

Several shots go into the air, but no one is hit.

The gang ties up Lynn and puts her next to Drake.

Drake whispers to her.

Drake

That was stupid.

Drake

You should have let me die.

Lynn

Shut up.

Drake

It’s like talking to Diana all over again.

Lynn

Nobody’s going to die.

Lynn

This isn’t over yet.

Drake

Don’t do anything stupid.

She flexes her fingers as Jagger approaches her with a knife.

Lynn

I told you — I’m not like Diana.

Lynn

I’m tougher than that.

Lynn

I can outlast this.

Jagger leans in and places the knife on her cheek.

Jagger

You know, I never liked you.

Lynn

Oh yeah? I never liked you, either.

Lynn head-butts Jagger causing him to drop the knife in surprise.

Drake catches it between his tied hands, before it hits the floor.

He uses it to undo the zip ties around their wrists.

Lynn

C-1 o’clock

Drake recognizes the command for jump and catapults himself over the gang member directly in front of him.

Lynn

AA-6 o’clock

He spins around, assaulting the gang member with two quick crossbody punches to the chin.

Drake

Lynn! B on your 3!

Lynn spins to her right, swinging her leg out in a kick.

After she takes out her assailant at the knees, she dives in for an attack.

Both Drake and Lynn rely on their training code as they protect each other’s backs.

Together, they kill the gang members one by one.

As soon as the last gang member falls, backup arrives.

Lynn

You’re a little late, guys.

Ruby

Jesus. Couldn’t you save someone for me to hurt?

Drake stumbles to the ground.

Lynn rushes to his side.

Lynn

Does anybody know first aid?

Lynn

We need some help over here!

Two men come forward as Ruby calls for an ambulance.

Ruby

We can take it from here.

Lynn steps back as the men do their best to patch Drake up.

After securing make-shift bandages, they give Lynn and Drake some privacy.

Drake

You shouldn’t have blown your cover like that.

Lynn

I had no choice. You were going to die.

Drake

Yeah, but you risked your life doing it.

Drake

And I’d rather I die than you.

Lynn

Don’t say that.

Drake

Why not? It’s the truth.

Lynn

Well, it doesn’t matter now. Both of our covers are blown.

Drake

No, YOUR cover is blown.

Lynn

You can’t possibly be thinking about going back into the field right now.

Lynn

You look like shit.

Drake

I have to go back.

Drake

Now that we’ve located Foxy, I can draw the Honduras supplier out of seclusion.

Drake

We’re about to shut this whole thing down.

Drake

I know it.

Lynn stares at him for a long moment.

Then she takes off her fox ring and hands it to him.

Lynn

You’re right.

Drake

I am?

Drake

I mean, of course I am.

Lynn

If we want any chance of catching this Honduras supplier, you have to go back.

Drake curls his fingers around the ring.

Lynn

Don’t do anything stupid.

Drake

You mean like risk my life?

Drake

It’s part of the job. You know that.

Lynn works her jaw, but says nothing.

The door bursts open as the paramedics rush in.

Tears sting Lynn’s eyes.

She steps back as they work on Drake.

This mission is so dangerous.

How will Drake survive next time, when Lynn isn’t there to watch his back?

He’s going to die out there alone — and there’s nothing she can do about it.

Frustrated, she paces away.

There has to be some way to help him.

And damn it, she’s going to find it.
