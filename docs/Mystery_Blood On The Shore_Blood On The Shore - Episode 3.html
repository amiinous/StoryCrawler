Blood On The Shore - Episode 3

Helen

I…I need to sit down.

Chloe

There’s a bench right here, Mom.

They sit down and look at each other as sirens flash nearby.

Chloe

I’m sorry.

Chloe

I didn’t mean to blurt it out right now.

Chloe

I know this isn’t exactly the best timing…

Helen

Are you… are you sure you’re actually pregnant?

Chloe

I took the test three times.

Chloe

And with three different brands.

Helen

I noticed you were putting on weight…

Helen

But of course I didn’t say anything.

Helen

How far along are you?

Chloe

Three and a half months.

Helen

And you’re sure Trevor is the father?

Chloe

Mom! It’s not like I’m sleeping around.

Chloe

Trevor was my first.

Chloe

God, I can’t believe I’m telling you this.

Helen

This is quite a shock, Chloe.

Helen

As far as I knew, you were just friends.

Chloe

We were, we are…

Chloe

I don’t know!

Chloe

If you want to know the truth…

Chloe

We were sick of being virgins!

Just then a BMW pulls up, cutting off their conversation.

Chloe

It’s Deirdre Lane!

Chloe

Oh, I feel terrible for her.

Deirdre parks her car at a crazy angle and gets out.

She appears to be in shock.

As a cop, Helen has seen this reaction before after a family member is killed.

Deirdre walks toward the bench in a daze and sits down next to Chloe and Helen.

Chloe

Oh Ms. Lane, I’m so sorry!

Deirdre

This all just feels so surreal. Who would do such a thing?

Deirdre

Frank was a saint—after my mother died, he was so good to me.

Deirdre

I considered him my father, not my stepfather.

Helen

I’m so sorry for your loss, Ms. Lane.

Deirdre

Thank you.

Deirdre

Do they have any idea who might have done this?

Chloe

Um…

Helen

The police are searching the area now.

Deirdre

If they don’t find out who did this soon

Deirdre

I’m going to hire my own private detective.

The doors of the lodge swing open and Frank Cassidy’s body is rolled out on a stretcher.

Deirdre walks up to it and asks for permission to see her stepfather’s face.

When the paramedics pull back the sheet, she lets out a small gasp.

Deirdre

Oh my God.

Deirdre

We’ll find who did this, Dad.

Helen and Chloe watch paramedics load the body into the back of an ambulance.

Then they see Deirdre slowly walks toward the lodge.

Helen

Honey, I think we need to go home now.

Chloe

Okay. I’ll follow you.

Helen

No. We can pick up your car tomorrow.

Helen

I want you to come with me.

Helen

We have a lot to talk about.

Chloe

Okay, Mom.

Helen and Chloe are silent for the first five minutes of the drive home.

Finally, Helen addresses her daughter.

Helen

Chloe, I don’t even know where to begin.

Helen

But let me try.

Helen

They still have sex education in high school, right?

Chloe

Yes, Mom.

Helen

And we talked about this a few years ago—the whole birth control thing.

Helen

You do remember about birth control?

Chloe

Of course I do, Mom.

Helen

I know people have sex at your age.

Helen

And honestly, I wasn’t expecting you to wait…

Helen

But WHY DIDN’T YOU USE BIRTH CONTROL?!!

Chloe

We did, Mom. We did.

Helen

So what—did the condom break?

Chloe

No.

Chloe

But we only had one.

Chloe

And…we didn’t want to stop after we did it just once.

Helen

For God’s sake, Chloe. I can’t believe this!

Chloe

Mom, come on!

Chloe

I mean, you had ME when you were around my age, remember?

Chloe

Do you regret it?

Helen takes Chloe’s hand.

Helen

No. You’re the best thing that ever happened to me, Chloe.

Helen

But I remember when I got pregnant.

Helen

And how suddenly, all my plans changed.

Helen

I ended up getting married before I was ready.

Helen

And then divorced.

Chloe

I wish you and Dad…

Helen

Let’s not go there right now, Chloe.

Helen

The point is, I want what’s best for you.

Helen

Including a career that you love.

Helen

You’re enrolled at the U of M this fall.

Helen

And you’ve always dreamed of becoming an architect.

Chloe

I know.

Helen

Having a baby now would make that a lot harder.

Helen

So, what are you going to do?

Chloe

Mom, I don’t know.

Chloe

I can’t make any decisions until I find out what happened to Trevor.

Chloe

What if he’s dead, Mom?

Chloe

And if he’s alive…

Chloe

What if he ends up in jail?

Helen

I know, honey. It’s a scary thought.

Helen

But had you…told him you were pregnant?

Chloe

No. I couldn’t.

Chloe

I…I was afraid of what he would say.

Chloe

And now…who knows when I’ll see him again!

Helen

I know.

Helen

But regardless of what happens, we’ll figure it out.

Helen

You know I’m here for you.

Helen

Always.

Chloe

Thanks, Mom.

They pull into the driveway and walk silently into the house.

The first thing Helen does is open a bottle of red wine.

She pours a healthy amount into a wine glass and sinks into the couch.

Chloe falls into an armchair.

Chloe

Can I have some?

Helen

Chloe!

Helen

There are two major reasons you can’t have a glass of wine.

Helen

One, you’re under the legal drinking age, and…

Chloe

Mom, I was joking!

The both start laughing uncontrollably.

Helen

Oh my god. We shouldn’t be laughing at a time like this.

Chloe

I know. But it feels so good.

Helen

I have to say, it does.

Helen

Oh!

Chloe

What is it?

Helen

I forgot to tell you Anna is coming here tonight.

Helen

Yes. She’s worried sick about Trevor.

Chloe

Poor Anna.

Chloe

This whole situation must be even harder since she’s a single mother, too.

There is a silence.

Chloe

You do realize…

Chloe

That if I have this baby…

Chloe

That you and Anna will be the grandmothers.

Helen

Me? A grandmother?

Helen

I’m only 37!

Helen

Here I was worried about you not being ready for this.

Helen

Maybe I’M the one who’s not ready!

Chloe

Mom…

Helen

What?

Chloe

Do you think I should tell Anna that I’m pregnant with Trevor’s baby?

Helen

No.

Chloe

But I kind of want to tell her.

Chloe

It might help her somehow.

Chloe

It could help her get through this.

Helen

No, don’t do that.

Helen

I don’t think she’s going to be in any state to digest that information right now.

Helen

YOU don’t even know what to do with the information yet.

Chloe

Okay, I guess you’re right.

Chloe

Oh, I wish I knew where Trevor was.

Chloe tries texting him again for the umpteenth time.

Still no response.

Helen and Chloe turn on the TV for a distraction.

They doze off in the middle of a show, and are interrupted when the doorbell rings.

Helen shakes herself awake and answers it,

And she finds her best friend in front of her.

Anna

Oh, Helen!

Anna

Have they found him?

Helen

No, Anna. I’m sorry.

Anna

Oh, I was hoping against hope.

Anna

Do the police still think he killed that man?

Helen

I’m afraid so.

Anna

Oh my God. Whether Trevor’s dead or alive, it’s always bad news.

Anna

I just can’t take it!

She drops herself onto the couch in a state of exhaustion.

Helen puts her arm around her and tries to comfort her.

Chloe does too.

Chloe

I know he didn’t do it, Anna.

Chloe

Mom is going to find Trevor and clear his name.

Helen

I’m going to try. I’ll do the best I can.

Helen

Now Chloe, don’t you think you should go to bed?

Helen

You’ve had a rough day.

Chloe

I’m not tired.

Chloe

Do you mind if I stay up with you and Anna?

Chloe

They said we didn’t have to come into work tomorrow.

Helen

Well, okay.

Helen

Anna, you got here really fast.

Anna

I went 80 just about the whole way.

Anna

It’s a miracle I didn’t get pulled over.

Helen

Do you want some wine?

Anna

Do I!

Helen

Red or white?

Anna

You have to ask?

As Helen pours another glass of red wine, the doorbell rings again.

Helen

What…?

Chloe

Oh my God—is it Trevor?!

Anna

No, I don’t think so.

Anna

Helen, please don’t be mad.

Anna

I thought he could help…

Helen

Oh no.

Chloe opens the door.

Standing on her welcome mat is Helen’s ex-husband, Matt Olsson.

Chloe

DAD!!!
