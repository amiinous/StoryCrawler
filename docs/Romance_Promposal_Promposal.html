Promposal

Daisy

I’m not sure I want to go through with this...

Kim

Aw come on, Daisy. Mitch put together this whole thing for you.

Daisy

It just seems really dumb. Mitch and I are dating already —

Daisy

Isn’t it obvious that we’re gonna go to prom together?

Daisy

Really — I don’t see why he has to make a big show of asking me.

Kim

Because it’s romantic and he cares about you…

Kim

Shouldn’t that be enough?

Daisy

Ugh, I don’t know…

Daisy

Part of me doesn’t even want to go at all. Prom is stupid.

Kim

But you have to go! Please? We’ve already got some good momentum going —

Kim

You’re on clue two already!

Kim

Plus you have me helping you. Mitch appointed me and everything.

Kim

Please? Pretty please will you do it?

Daisy

Fine — but I’m not gonna like it.

Kim

Hooray! Okay, tell me what the clue says.

Daisy

We’re supposed to go where we had our first kiss…

Daisy

But I don’t have my car today. And it’s kinda far away…

Daisy

It was under a tree at the cemetery.

Kim

That’s where you guys first kissed? What a couple of creeps.

Daisy

Shut up. Can you give me a ride there?

Kim

Sure, but we have to hurry —

Kim

I’m supposed to be at work in an hour. I’ll be there soon.

After Kim picks up Daisy, the girls rush over to the cemetery.

Daisy shows Kim the old oak tree under which she and Mitch first kissed…

And then, tucked up in between the trunk and one of its giant branches…

She spots a rolled-up piece of parchment.

She has to climb the tree to get to it, but once she does, she reads it aloud.

Daisy

Find the insects that live inside my digestive organ whenever I’m with you.

Kim

That was the clue?

Daisy

Yeah. Isn’t it obvious? The insect is a butterfly —

Daisy

He gets butterflies in his stomach whenever he sees me.

Kim

You got THAT from THOSE words?

Kim

Geez, you and Mitch really do belong together.

Daisy smiles but then her brow wrinkles.

Daisy

But wait…I don’t get it. Where am I supposed to find butterflies?

Daisy

They could literally be in any garden.

Kim

Yeah, but is there one specific place that KEEPS a bunch of butterflies?

Kim

Like, that feeds and cares for them?

Daisy still looks perplexed.

Kim

Come on, Daisy — you know this one. Maybe other animals live there also?

Daisy

Oh! The zoo! I totally forgot that they had a butterfly house.

Kim

Exactly. Come on — we’ve got to hurry!

The friends rush over to the zoo. But as soon as they get to the gate…

They realize that it’s closed.

Daisy spots a janitor clearing out the trash.

She clutches the bar of the gate, pressing her face up against it.

Daisy

Please, sir — will you let us in?

Janitor

No can do. Zoo closes at 5pm.

Daisy

But my boyfriend —

Kim puts her hand on Daisy’s arm.

Kim

It’s okay. I can just tell you the next clue.

Daisy

No. You just said it —

Daisy

Mitch is the perfect guy for me.

Daisy

And if he wants to give me a stupid scavenger hunt to ask me to prom…

Daisy

Then that’s EXACTLY what’s gonna happen.

Daisy takes a few steps back from the gate…

And then she turns around and runs toward it at full speed.

Kim

Daisy, I don’t…

But Daisy has already catapulted herself over the iron fence.

Within moments, the janitor starts chasing after her —

But he isn’t nearly as quick as Daisy.

She disappears out of view. Kim grasps the bars, anxiously awaiting her return…

And then she sees her —

Dashing down the pathway. She jumps over the fence, sails in the air…

And lands angrily on the side of her ankle.

She collapses on the ground.

Daisy

Oww!

Kim

Oh my god, Daisy, are you okay?

Daisy

I don’t know…let me try to get up…

Daisy

Oww!

Kim rushes next to Daisy’s side, slipping her arm below Daisy’s to support her weight.

Up close, she can see the tears welling in Daisy’s eyes.

Through the iron fence, the janitor shakes his fist.

Janitor

Serves you right! You had no business sneaking into my zoo!

The girls just shake their heads and walk away.

Then Daisy turns to Kim. When she speaks, her voice is small.

Daisy

Can you just take me home?

Daisy

I knew this wasn’t a good idea —

Daisy

Maybe Mitch and I are too different.

Daisy

Maybe we don’t want the same things…

Kim

Daisy, come on —

Daisy

No, seriously. I just REALLY hurt myself trying to play this game.

Daisy

Maybe he should date someone who’s more up to his speed.

Kim

Up to his SPEED? Daisy, that ridiculous —

Kim

Wait, what do you have in your hand?

Daisy

This? It’s the clue.

Kim

So you managed to get the clue, even though the zoo was locked?

Daisy

Yeah, but my ankle…

Daisy

I think I need to see a doctor.

Kim smiles. It’s a big grin.

Kim

I can take you to your doctor.

Daisy

Stop it. Why are you grinning like that?

Kim

Read the clue, Daisy.

Daisy sighs. She opens the parchment clasped in her hand.

Daisy

Go to the place where my cardiac chambers were pounding when I spoke to you for the first time.

Daisy looks up at Kim.

Daisy

He WANTS me to go to the doctor’s office?

Kim laughs.

Kim

See?! You’ve got to admit there’s a least a tiny bit of fate involved.

Kim

And look, we’re at the car already. Let’s just finish this out.

Daisy

Ugh, fine.

Kim opens the passenger door and lowers Daisy into the car. They start to drive.

Daisy

Seriously, Kim. How much more of this?

Kim

You’ll just have to see.

The girls gingerly navigate their way into the waiting room of the doctor’s office.

Once inside, Kim gives Daisy a quick hug and heads back out the door to go to work.

Exhausted, Daisy plops into the nearest seat —

And then she sees Mitch.

The concern on his face brings a fresh batch of tears to her eyes.

She finally gets it — it’s never been about HIS scavenger hunt…

It’s always been about giving her an incredible experience. It’s been about her.

Mitch

Daisy, are you hurt?

Daisy

Yeah, I fell on my ankle — during your scavenger hunt. I’m sorry.

Mitch

Why would you be sorry?

Daisy

Because you wanted it to be perfect, for me — and now it’s not.

Daisy

I feel like I ruined it. I let you down.

Mitch

Daisy, you could never do that. I’m the one who let you down —

Mitch

I should have just asked you —

Mitch

I mean, I should have TOLD you…

Daisy

What?

Mitch

That I love you.

Mitch

I’ve wanted to tell you forever…but you DO give me butterflies.

Mitch

Look, this whole plan was dumb — can we forget it happened?

Mitch

Will you go to prom with me?

Daisy

Wait, you love me?

Mitch smiles at her.

Mitch

I do.

Daisy

We’ve…never said that before.

Mitch

I know. I guess there’s something about this waiting room that makes me brave around you.

Mitch

Remember, six months ago, when I finally worked up the courage to talk to THE Daisy Mills from Cyruss High?

Mitch

We were both sick and gross that day, but it was the first time we were in a room just the two of us.

Daisy laughs.

Daisy

Then I’ll be brave too.

Daisy

I love you too, Mitch.

Daisy

I love all your stupid scavenger hunts and unnecessary displays of affection —

Daisy

Because they always make me feel adored.

Mitch’s grin widens.

Mitch

Does that mean we can hobble around together at the prom?

Daisy sighs.

Daisy

I guess it does.

Daisy

As long as I’m with you.
