My Fake Boyfriend - Episode 2

The next morning, Jack sees a few texts from his best friend, Brady.

Brady

Hey, Jack! You awake yet, man?

Brady

Where in the world have you been?

Brady

I stopped by your place last night, but you weren’t there. Couldn’t get you on the phone either.

Brady

…

Brady

Are you even alive?

Jack

Yeah, alive.

Jack

But don’t have time to talk. I’ve got to drive soon.

Brady

There’s almost an hour before school starts. Where are you going?

Jack

Long story…I promised to give a ride to this girl.

Brady

Ha! What?

Brady

Is that why you were gone yesterday?

Jack

Yeah. Like I said, long story there.

Jack

But you should know, she asked me a favor.

Jack

Some guys were picking on her at school, so she asked me to be her fake boyfriend.

Jack

Just until they back off.

Brady

…

Brady

And you told her no, right?

Jack

Why would I say no?

Brady

Because that kind of thing never works out.

Jack

There’s nothing to work out. She asked me for a favor.

Jack

So don’t ruin it by telling anyone.

Brady

What’s in it for you? You like her?

Jack

Don’t start.

Brady

You totally like her.

Brady

Otherwise, you’d realize this plan was dumb.

Jack

You don’t know what you’re talking about.

Brady

I’ve known you since we were kids. You don’t think I can tell when you like someone?

Brady

You bailed on me yesterday.

Brady

At least you’re not denying your feelings.

Jack

Look, I’ve got to go. I had to tell you because you’d know something was weird.

Jack

I can’t have you saying anything that messes this up.

Brady

I won’t ruin it, but I’m telling you…this never works.

Brady

Anyway, good luck. Hope she’s worth it.

Jack

I think she is.

Three hours later, during fifth period class, Hazel texts Jack.

Hazel

Thanks again for the ride this morning.

Hazel

Do you think everyone believed it when we were holding hands walking in?

Jack

I haven’t asked anyone, but I don’t think they would have questioned it.

Hazel

Well I think our plan is working.

Jack

Really? Why?

Hazel

Because usually, when I walk into class, there’s a boy that tugs my hair. Like in elementary school.

Hazel

Only today I planned to tell him about you.

Hazel

But before I could, someone else told him to not mess with me, because my “boyfriend” would get him.

Hazel

And then he stopped 🙂

Jack

That’s good!

Hazel

Yeah, maybe if your next class is near mine, we can hang out and talk until the bell rings.

Jack

We should.

Jack

Should I stop texting during your class?

Hazel

No, it’s okay. It’s just French class.

Hazel

No tests until next week, and I know the material.

Jack

You know French already?

Hazel

I really wanted to learn last summer.

Hazel

So I watched a thousand YouTube videos.

Jack

Do you want to go to France?

Hazel

Yeah. I like learning languages.

Jack

I’m taking Japanese.

Hazel

🙂 Do you like it? That was the other language I wanted to learn.

Jack

I like it. I can teach some to you if you’d want.

Jack

But I only know a little. So far at least.

Jack

What do you do in French class if you already know the material?

Hazel

I usually just doodle or work on my junk journal.

Jack

Junk journal?

Hazel

I collect little bits of wallpaper or like movie tickets or just interesting photos. Then I put them in a journal together.

Hazel

It’s kind of like collage art.

Jack

So you collect paper? Interesting.

Hazel

Nerdy, I know 🤓

Jack

No, it’s cool. Where do you find the paper?

Hazel

At antique shops, mostly. Or thrift markets.

Jack

I should take you downtown sometime.

Jack

Market Street.

Jack

There are a lot of antique shops down there.

Hazel

You’d take me?

Jack

I’d love to. I like going down there.

Hazel

Do you buy anything?

Jack

Not really, but I like looking.

Hazel

I like looking, too. :)

Hazel

We’re a lot alike.

Jack

Seems like it…

Hazel

Yeah. We should keep this fake boyfriend thing going.

Hazel

Maybe tomorrow we should do the same thing.

Jack

Maybe we should…you know…

Hazel

Maybe we should kiss in the hallway tomorrow.

Jack

Of course.

Jack

I mean…I wouldn’t say no to that.

Hazel

Or do you think it’s too obvious?

Jack

Honestly, if I were going to kiss you, I’d rather it be for real.

Jack

I was going to say…

Hazel

That we're taking it too far?

Hazel

I’m sorry. I’m probably making you uncomfortable.

Jack

No, no!

Jack

I’m not uncomfortable.

Jack

I just…

Hazel

I don’t want to push you into anything you don’t want. Okay?

Jack

You’re not.

Jack

I meant what I said.

Hazel

What do you mean?

Jack

I mean…the kissing.

Hazel

Which part?

Jack

I really want to.

Jack

But…maybe we should talk about it. After school?

Hazel

Yes. Want to go to the playground we were hanging out in yesterday?

Jack

I’ve got to check in with a teacher after school, but I can give you a ride.

Jack

Let’s meet by the parking lot.

Hazel

Okay. I’ll wait 🙂

After the final bell, Hazel waits for Jack by the parking lot.

She tries to keep to herself, but it isn’t long before a bunch of guys come up to her.

Panicking, she reaches for her phone and texts her fake boyfriend.

Hazel

Jack! Are you still at school?

Jack

Yeah. Still here. I should be in the parking lot in five minutes.

Hazel

Mike is here. He’s trying to get me to go with him.

Hazel

He won’t leave me alone.

Hazel

I told him I’ve got a boyfriend, but he thinks I’m making it up.

Hazel

He keeps following me.

Jack

Where are you?

Hazel

In the parking lot, next to the gym.

Jack

I’ll be there in two minutes. Don’t go anywhere with him.

Hazel

Please hurry!
