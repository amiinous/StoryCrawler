For the Love of Pete - Episode 3

The next day, Hayley sits on a bench at the dog run waiting for Travis and Pete.

She’s wearing skinny jeans, sandals and a print cami top.

Her hair is gently curled and loose over her shoulders.

She gets a text from Travis.

Travis

I just found a space.

Travis

Be there in a few.

Hayley

Okay. I just got here.

Hayley

See you both soon.

While she waits, she opens the picture Travis sent the night before.

It’s Pete asleep with a stuffed squirrel that Travis picked up at a pet shop.

The caption reads Nite, Mom.

She gets a text from her friend Nicole.

Nicole

Why aren’t you at work?

Nicole

I just called there looking for you.

Hayley

I’m meeting Travis.

Nicole

Who’s Travis? Spill!

Hayley

I already did. He’s the guy I’m sharing Pete with.

Nicole

Why are you meeting him? To make an exchange?

Hayley

No.

Nicole

Why then?

Nicole

I thought you said he was a jerk trying to steal your baby away from you.

Hayley

I was wrong.

Nicole

Oh, no! Don’t tell me you’re falling for him!

Nicole

Hayes, don’t do it! Remember Jeff.

Hayley

I’m not falling for him!

Hayley

But I think he’s different, Nic. Really.

Nicole

You don’t even know him!

Hayley

But Jack did. And Jack loved him.

Hayley

That has to mean something.

Hayley

And he’s really good with Pete.

A text comes in from Travis.

Travis

I’m almost there.

Hayley

Nic, gotta go.

Hayley

Talk later.

A few minutes later, Hayley spots Travis and Pete.

Travis is wearing black jeans, boots and a t-shirt.

She hurries toward them and bends down to pet Pete.

Hayley

Little Chubs! I’ve missed you!

Hayley stands up.

Travis

You look nice.

Hayley

Thanks, so do you.

They stare at each other for a long while until Hayley blushes.

Hayley

Let’s take Pete inside and let him play.

They enter the park.

Hayley

Look Pete, there’s Pip!

They sit together on the bench and watch Pete run off to play with the Great Dane.

Hayley turns to Travis.

Hayley

I wanted to thank you for coming all the way in just to let me see Pete.

Travis

I know you missed him, so…

Hayley

You’re being really great about this.

Hayley

I know it’s going to be hard to have to drive in every month.

Hayley

I wish I had a car so I could ease the burden.

Hayley

For whatever reason, Jack wanted us both to have Pete.

Hayley

I want to make this joint custody thing work.

Hayley

So I’ve decided that Pete and I can take the train to Connecticut when it’s my turn to drop him off.

Travis

No, that’s not necessary.

Hayley

I don’t mind. It’s probably a scenic trip.

Travis

Actually, it is.

Hayley

I don’t mind sharing Pete with you, if you want to know the truth.

Hayley

I thought I would.

Hayley

But you’re making it fun.

Travis

Am I?

Hayley

Yes…and it’s nice to have someone else in Pete’s life who loves him as much as I do.

Travis

I agree.

Hayley

So what do you say?

Travis

Okay, let’s give it a go, but no trains.

Travis

I don’t mind the drive.

Travis

You should take it with me sometime.

Hayley

Deal.

She holds out her hand to shake on it.

She’s blushing.

Hayley

I mean…

Still holding Hayley’s hand, Travis pulls her in gently and presses his lips to hers.

The touch of his mouth sends electric currents through her veins.

It’s a brief kiss but they both withdraw from it breathless.

Travis

I’m sorry. I’ve been wanting to do that…well, since a little after we met.

Travis stumbles backward a bit.

Travis

Sorry, but my head is spinning a little.

Hayley

Mine too.

Travis

I didn’t think I’d…

He stops short.

Travis

Hey, where’s Pete?

Hayley

What?!

They both leap from the bench. They look around at the other dogs.

Pete isn’t there!

They call him. The rising panic in their voices draws attention from the other visitors.

Hayley

Travis, you don’t think someone…

Travis

We’ll find him. But we need to move now.

Travis

There’s no time for fear, okay?

Hayley

You’re right.

Hayley

Let’s go.

They run through the park, calling out for Pete.

Soon everyone in the park is looking for him.

Ten minutes later Hayley wipes tears from her eyes.

Hayley

This can’t end like this.

Hayley

With both of us losing him.

Travis

It won’t. Everyone in the park is helping. We’ll find him.

Hayley

This is my fault. If you hadn’t come in today…

Travis

Hey, stop it. It’s no one’s fault.

Travis

We’re going to find him.

Travis

He lost Jack. We’re all he has left.

A young man from the park interrupts them.

Young Man

Are you the couple with the stolen dog?

Travis

Yes.

Young Man

A group of us found him, I think—

Travis smiles at Hayley.

Travis

I told you.

She wants to throw her arms around him but turns to the young man instead.

Hayley

Show us.

They follow the stranger to a small crowd gathered around the suspicious man from the run the other day.

He’s claiming Pete is his.

But an indignant woman, a dog park regular who knows better, has wrested Pete away from him.

She is holding Pete close to her chest.

Two big men are holding the dognapper until the police get there.

Hayley

Pete!

The woman holding Pete can’t contain his chubby, wriggling body when he sees Hayley and Travis.

He jumps from her arms toward Hayley and Travis.

A man from the crowd

Well, it’s clear the dog belongs to those two.

Travis and Hayley share a smile.

They’re reunited with Pete amid tears and licks and laughter.

The dognapper is arrested.

He had recognized Travis and planned to ask him for ransom in exchange for Pete.

Travis and Hayley thank the people who aided in the search.

Hayley

Oh, I was so worried about you, Pete!

Hayley

I just want to kiss you for days!

Travis

In that case, I should get myself kidnapped.

Hayley

You don’t have to go to such extremes for a kiss from me.

Travis smiles, and bends his head to kiss Hayley, who is holding Pete.

But his phone ringing in his pocket stops him.

He answers it, then turns away and converses in a low tone.

He returns the phone to his pocket and looks at Hayley.

His face is suddenly pale.

Travis

That was my mom.

Travis

My dad has pneumonia.
