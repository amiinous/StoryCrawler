The Floor Is Lava

Paul

Dude, how funny was it when we played The Floor is Lava in the movie theatre last night?

Tom

HI-larious.

Tom

Remember when you almost knocked over that lady…

Tom

When you jumped on the ticket machine?

Paul

Maybe that was funny for YOU.

Paul

I legitimately thought her boyfriend was going to kill me.

Paul

And he was a big dude.

Tom

Lol. I downloaded the Floor is Lava app from the app store.

Paul

Oh yeah?

Tom

Yeah, it’s pretty good.

Tom

Not as good as playing in person though.

Paul

Well, yeah. But I’m bored, so I’ll try it.

Paul

What level have you gotten to?

Tom

6, lol. I just started playing.

10 minutes later

Paul

Level 10.

Tom

No way--already?

Tom

Did you find a trick or something?

Paul

No man, I’m just the master.

Paul

I guess I instinctively know where to jump.

Tom

Yeah, yeah.

Tom

I’ll beat you soon.

5 minutes later

Tom

Level 17.

Paul

There’s no way you’re NOT cheating.

Paul

You just beat your best record by 7 levels?

Paul

AND that quickly??

Tom

Yup. Who’s the master now?

Paul

Dude, it’s on. I’m going to obliterate you.

15 minutes later

Paul

Dude, I need your help.

Paul

Seriously.

Paul

I have a HUGE problem.

Tom

What’s up?

Paul

I got up to Level 32…

Tom

Shut up, man. There’s no way you got that high.

Paul

No, you shut up. Let me type.

Tom

Ok.

Paul

I got to Level 32 and it got REALLY hot in here.

Paul

Like, dude, I know it’s summer and everything…

Paul

But I started sweating bullets.

Tom

Ok, so…??

Paul

There’s more.

Paul

I looked down and…

Paul

Shit, you’re not going to believe this.

Paul

Then the floor was LITERALLY lava.

Tom

WHAT??

Paul

I know. Maybe it was because I got to such a high level…

Paul

Or maybe something happened that wore down the barrier between our universe…

Paul

And the universe in the app…

Paul

But the floor LITERALLY turned to lava in my basement.

Tom

Come on, don’t be ridiculous.

Paul

Dude, I’m NOT kidding.

Paul

I was sitting in my wheelie desk chair while I was playing…

Paul

And somehow, I guess the lava current was hotter by the back right wheel…

Paul

That’s when my chair started to tilt and sink.

Tom

WHAT?! That’s nuts.

Paul

That’s what I’m SAYING.

Paul

The floor is LITERALLY lava.

Tom

Holy shit, what are you going to do?

Paul

Well, the lava was melting my desk chair…

Paul

But I was able to kinda push myself along the current…

Paul

By dragging myself along the ping pong table.

Paul

The force of that movement propelled me toward the couch.

Paul

It looked sturdier than the chair, so I got up there.

Tom

Ok…

Paul

Yeah. But now the lava is starting to eat at the legs of the couch…

Paul

Holy shit, holy shit…

Paul

What the hell do I do?!

Tom

How would I know??

Tom

I’ve never been around lava before!

Tom

I’m still trying to wrap my head around how this even happened.

Paul

Dude, it happened.

Paul

For every second it takes you to believe that…

Paul

I’m literally sinking into LAVA.

Tom

This is so ridiculous.

Paul

Dude, just come over -- please?

Paul

I need help.

Tom

Ok man, I’m coming over.

Paul

Thanks.

Paul

I’m so glad you live only a couple blocks away.

Tom

Yeah, I’m gonna run there as fast as I can.

Paul

Thank god you run track.

Tom

Yeah -- see, I told you it wasn’t a loser sport.

Paul

Dude, no one on the track team even drinks. It’s a loser sport.

Tom

OMG, that’s it! Drinks!

Tom

Do you think you can climb up on the bar from the couch?

Tom

It might be less flammable -- it seems even sturdier than the furniture

Paul

I don’t know… it’s kinda far…

Paul

I might have to jump.

Paul

What the hell -- I’m doing it!

Paul

This couch is going down any second.

Tom

Ok, good luck man.

Tom

Let me know when you’ve made it there.

3 minutes later

Paul

Made it! But dude…

Paul

I almost fell in.

Paul

And then a spark of lava shot up and hit me on the leg.

Paul

Oh god, it burns...

Tom

Shit, shit.

Tom

What the hell are you going to do?

Paul

I don’t know…

Paul

Owww, OMG, shit!

Tom

Can you run it under cold water?

Tom

I feel like that’s what my mom usually does when I get burnt.

Paul

Dude, there’s no cold water.

Paul

If there was, I’d pour it on all this lava.

Tom

Wait, isn’t there a sink right at your bar?

Paul

Yeah, but it isn’t working.

Paul

That’s the first thing I tried -- I think all the heat from the lava messed it up.

Tom

You tried it before?

Paul

Yeah…

Tom

But I’m the one who gave you the idea to go to the bar!

Tom

And since you lied about going there first…

Tom

I think you lied about something else.

Tom

You’re pranking me.

Tom

Although it isn’t a very good prank because I figured it out.

Paul

What are you talking about? The floor IS lava.

Tom

Paul, it’s not lava.

Tom

I got to your house. I can see into your basement window.

Tom

Haha, I can even see that you’re literally crouched on the bar though.

Tom

Nice touch.

Paul waves at Tom through the window, beaming from ear to ear.

He jumps off the bar and walks across the carpeted floor…

To let his friend in through the sliding doors in his basement.

When Tom walks into the air conditioning, Paul claps him on the shoulder and says…

Paul

I still got you.

Paul

You ran all the way over here, you know.

Tom

Nah, maybe I just wanted some of your mom’s homemade ice pops.

Paul

Haha, ice pops after I’ve been battling a floor of lava all afternoon?

Paul

That sounds good to me.

And the boys race each other up the stairs,

Because Paul’s mom usually only makes one cherry ice pop.

It’s their favorite flavor -- and the color of lava.
