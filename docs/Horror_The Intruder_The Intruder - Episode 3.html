You’re in a video game, remember?

NO!

I told you before about the trolls.

KILL???

Dad, I don’t know about this.

You can do this, Eddie.

Take a deep breath.

Okay.

I am.

The first thing you have to do is find a rock.

Great!

Then start moving slowly toward the cabin.

Are you sure?

Yes. Now listen. I’m not done.

Dad

One of the trolls will go into the forest to investigate, and the other one will have its back turned.

Once the first troll disappears into the forest, make a dash for the cabin.

I can’t!

NOW!

Eddie?

Eddie??
