Inked - Part 1: THE DIARY

Ethan

I called you three times, Brooke.

Ethan

Why aren't you answering your phone?

Ethan

I know you're at the cabin with your family, but I need your help.

Brooke

Hey babe. I'm here.

Brooke

I can't get a good cell signal on the mountain, but your texts come through eventually.

Brooke

God, I freaking hate family vacations in the middle of nowhere.

Brooke

I'd rather be at the pool next to you all summer.

Brooke

In a bikini.

Ethan

Brooke, stop.

Ethan

I need your help with something serious.

Brooke

What's wrong?

Ethan

I gotta tell you something—something bad.

Ethan

I should've told you sooner.

Brooke

What? Are you OK?

Ethan

No.

Ethan

Something's wrong with me.

Ethan

I think I'm cursed.

Brooke

Cursed?

Brooke

What are you talking about?

Ethan

You know how I've been wanting to get a tattoo?

Brooke

Yeah. Why?

Ethan

I found a sketch of a cool symbol in my dad's office.

Ethan

It was in an old book on his desk, next to all his museum curation research.

Ethan

It looked like some kind of occult drawing.

Brooke

And?

Ethan

I took a photo of the symbol with my phone and went over to Needle Point a few days ago.

Ethan

Right after you left for the cabin.

Brooke

Whoa. Wait.

Brooke

You actually got inked?

Ethan

Yeah. On my bicep.

Brooke

That’s hot.

Brooke

Why didn't you tell me?

Ethan

I was gonna text you a photo, but then… things went wrong.

Brooke

Is the tattoo infected?

Ethan

No.

Ethan

I don’t know how to explain this but…

Ethan

My brain feels weird.

Brooke

Huh?

Ethan

Like, kind of mushy.

Ethan

I feel like I'm in a daze.

Ethan

And I keep getting nosebleeds.

Brooke

Are you sick or something?

Ethan

I told you, Brooke—I'm cursed.

Ethan

None of this crap went down 'til after I got that tattoo.

Ethan

I need you to believe me.

Brooke

I don't believe in curses, Ethan.

Ethan

I didn’t before this either.

Ethan

But I'm being serious. I swear.

Ethan

I had a freaking seizure at 4 in the morning.

Brooke

OMG!

Brooke

Babe, you need to go to the hospital.

Ethan

I did.

Ethan

I got home around 7 o'clock.

Brooke

Are you OK?!

Ethan

The doctors found no evidence of a seizure.

Ethan

I don't know why, but they said I'm fine.

Ethan

And I know I'm not.

Brooke

Because of a curse?!

Ethan

Because of the tattoo.

Brooke

Ethan.

Brooke

Stop.

Ethan

I've been hallucinating, too.

Brooke

WHAT?!

Brooke

And the doctors seriously said you're OK?!

Ethan

I couldn't tell them about the visions or they'd lock me in the psych ward—wouldn't they?

Ethan

But I have them. I keep seeing things.

Brooke

Like what?!

Ethan

Dolls.

Ethan

A collection of porcelain dolls.

Ethan

I saw them all around me in frilly white dresses.

Ethan

But they had NO EYES, Brooke.

Brooke

Excuse me?!

Ethan

It looked like someone poked their eyes out with an ice pick or something.

Ethan

And all these spiders came crawling out through the holes.

Brooke

Ethan, please...

Brooke

You're freaking me out.

Brooke

I think you need a psychiatrist.

Ethan

I don't need a shrink.

Ethan

I need to get to the bottom of this crap.

Brooke

I know. But I'm scared, babe.

Ethan

Me too.

Brooke

Why did you get a tattoo of some strange occult symbol you'd never seen before?

Ethan

I don't know. It looked cool.

Ethan

I guess it was stupid.

Ethan

But I didn't think it was actually an occult curse!

Brooke

Why didn't you tell me about all this earlier?

Ethan

I didn't want you to think I was a freak.

Brooke

I don't think that, babe.

Ethan

Well, wait… there's more.

Brooke

What?

Ethan

I took some sleeping pills two nights ago.

Ethan

I wanted to ward off the visions somehow.

Brooke

Did it work?

Ethan

Yeah, but you know how I have that app that tracks my sleep patterns?

Brooke

Yeah…

Ethan

You know how if I talk in my sleep, the app records it?

Brooke

Yeah… and??

Ethan

I spoke Latin in my sleep.

Ethan

Around 2 AM.

Brooke

You don't even know Latin, Ethan.

Brooke

You were probably just mumbling nonsense.

Ethan

No.

Ethan

It was the exact same phrase over and over again.

Ethan

The next morning, I looked online 'til I got the spelling right.

Ethan

I kept saying, "Faciam ut mei memineris."

Brooke

What does that mean?

Ethan

"I'll make you remember me."

Brooke

Why would you say that?

Ethan

I have no clue.

Ethan

Please help me, Brooke.

Ethan

I need you.

Brooke

Of course.

Brooke

I'm gonna see if my mom will let me leave early.

Brooke

Then we're gonna talk about therapy, OK?

Ethan

OK. We'll see.

Brooke

What does your dad think about all of this?

Brooke

I mean, you saw the symbol in his book, yeah?

Ethan

He doesn't know about any of it.

Brooke

You didn't tell him you went to the hospital?

Brooke

That you had a seizure and hallucinations?!

Ethan

I can't tell him about it, Brooke.

Ethan

He'd have me locked in the asylum, same as the doctors would.

Brooke

But he knows you got inked, right?

Ethan

No.

Ethan

You don't need parental consent when you're 18.

Ethan

And after I stayed in the ER overnight, I said I'd slept over at a friend's house.

Ethan

But when I got home, I went into his office.

Ethan

I casually asked him about the book I'd seen on his desk.

Brooke

And?

Ethan

He said it's a diary from the early 1800s.

Ethan

A guy named Quentin Finch gave it to him.

Ethan

Finch found it in an old box in his attic, full of family relics and junk.

Ethan

He thought my dad might wanna look at the diary for the museum.

Brooke

Whose diary was it?

Ethan

A woman named Della Dossett.

Ethan

But apparently Finch doesn't know who she was.

Ethan

And neither does my dad.

Brooke

What else is in the diary?

Ethan

It looked like nothing but scribbles and sketches.

Ethan

I couldn't get any more information outta my dad.

Ethan

He said he had to get to work.

Ethan

And then he took the diary with him.

Brooke

Shit.

Ethan

Brooke, I’m seeing them again.

Ethan

Eyeless dolls… full of spiders…

Brooke

Holy shit, Ethan!

Ethan

I know…

Ethan

What the hell am I going to do?

Brooke

We. It’s what we’re going to do. We’re in this together.

Brooke

Since you really don’t want to go to your dad…

Brooke

We’ll find Quentin Finch.

Brooke

We look through that old box in his attic.

Brooke

And we’ll get some fucking answers.
