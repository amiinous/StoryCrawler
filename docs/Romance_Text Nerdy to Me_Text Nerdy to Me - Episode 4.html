Text Nerdy to Me - Episode 4

Tiffany grins as Erin comes back into the living room.

Tiffany

How was your playtime with Mister Wonderful?

Erin

What makes you think we were playing?

Tiffany

Oh, I don’t know.

Tiffany

The closed door.

Tiffany

The moaning noises—

Erin

I was NOT moaning.

Tiffany

Whatever you say.

Tiffany

If you’re finished playing with your new toy…

Tiffany

It’s time for us to pack up and catch our flight back to Seattle.

Tiffany

Our wild weekend is over.

Tiffany

And don’t forget…

Tiffany

You have Charlie waiting for you at home.

Erin grimaces.

Erin

Thanks for the reminder.

Tiffany

It’s not too late to give me the hottie on your phone!

Erin

In your dreams.

Erin

Besides, he’s not real.

Erin

He’s just a guy who met some other girl in a bar one night.

Tiffany

But he DID make you howl like a monkey.

Tiffany

That’s got to count for something.

Erin glowers at Tiffany.

Erin

You said it yourself.

Erin

We can’t actually meet.

Erin

The phone is all we can have.

Tiffany

Unless you fess up.

Erin

And possibly ruin everything?

Erin

That is not going to happen.

Erin couldn’t chance losing him.

Not after what they shared.

But how on earth was she going to make this work?

Five hours later, Erin and Tiffany arrive at SeaTac Airport.

Tiffany glances around baggage claim.

Tiffany

I don’t see Kimmy.

Tiffany

What color did you say her hair is these days?

Erin

Who knows? She was thinking about going bubblegum pink when I left.

Tiffany

Honestly, the teal was my favorite.

Tiffany

It’s funny. For sisters, you sure are different.

Erin

This again?

Tiffany

Just sayin’.

Tiffany

You’re a stick in the mud.

Tiffany

She’s a wild child.

Erin

I am not a stick in the mud!

Tiffany

You are. What’s the craziest thing you’ve ever done?

Erin

Partying my short-term memory away in Vegas?

Erin

Dirty texting a complete stranger?

Tiffany sniffs.

Tiffany

That doesn’t count.

Tiffany

I know you — you’re just going to get home…

Tiffany

And just slip back into your SAFE life with Charlie.

Erin

That’s not fair.

Tiffany

Isn’t it? I’ve known you for years. You do it every time.

Erin

Do what?

Tiffany

Nest whenever you’re in a relationship.

Tiffany

Even when you realize the guy’s not right for you, you stay.

Erin

I’m loyal.

Tiffany

You need to be loyal to yourself.

Erin

Things are okay with Charlie. He’s a good guy.

Tiffany

Do you hear yourself? Things are OKAY?

Tiffany

You deserve better than okay.

Erin

Oh look. There she is. Kimmy! Here!

Kimmy races over and everyone hugs.

Kimmy

How was Vegas?

Erin

It was all right.

Kimmy

No fun?

Erin

We had a little fun.

Tiffany

Aren’t you gonna tell Kimmy about your new boyfriend?

Erin

Damn it, Tiff!

Kimmy perks right up.

Kimmy

New boyfriend? You better spill.

Erin

He’s not real.

Tiffany

He’s very real.

Tiffany

But he texted her thinking he was texting someone else.

Erin

He’s. Not. Real.

Tiffany

Yeah, she’s SO uninterested in him…

Tiffany

She won’t even give me his number.

Tiffany

Some kind of friend.

Kimmy

He sounds yummy.

Kimmy

Does this mean we’re dumping Charlie?

Tiffany

Is that a hint of hope I hear in your voice?

Kimmy forces a smile.

Kimmy

You know how much I LOVE Charlie.

Tiffany

Right.

Kimmy

Oh, and speaking of him…

Kimmy

He’s been calling the house.

Erin

What? Why? He knew when I was getting back in.

Tiffany

And notice how he rushed to the airport to pick you up.

Erin frowns.

Erin

He was busy.

Tiffany

I bet Ab-Man would have come to pick you up.

Kimmy

Ab-Man?

Tiffany

The phone hottie?

Kimmy

How do you know he has abs?

Tiffany

Selfie.

Tiffany

Go on. Show her.

Erin

I’m not showing her.

Tiffany

Worried she’ll want him too?

Tiffany turns to Kimmy, winking.

Tiffany

You can wait til she’s asleep and then steal her phone.

Erin

Stop it. Just stop it.

Erin’s phone buzzes. It’s Charlie.

Tiffany

You going to answer that?

Tiffany

Or should we pretend we missed his call…

Tiffany

And go eat clam chowder on the pier instead?

Erin hesitates, but only for a second.

She’s not ready to face Charlie yet…

Or deal with the sinking suspicion that Kimmy and Tiffany might be right.

It might be time to reevaluate…everything.

Erin

Let’s go to the pier.

Kimmy

Excellent!

The friends enjoy a lovely, simple dinner overlooking Elliott Bay.

They focus only on their laughter and there’s no talk of real or imaginary men.

Erin sips her wine.

Erin

God, I love this.

Kimmy

The sunset’s beautiful, isn’t it?

Tiffany

I’m pretty sure she was talking about the wine.

Erin

The sunset’s pretty too.

Erin’s phone buzzes — again.

She checks it and turns it over.

Kimmy sighs.

Kimmy

Charlie?

Erin

Yeah.

Kimmy

Why are you still with him?

Erin

Why do you care?

Kimmy

Because you’re my sister.

Kimmy

And I love you.

Kimmy

And every time he calls you, you look…

Erin

What?

Kimmy

Disappointed.

Tiffany

Of course she’s disappointed. It’s not Ab-Man.

Kimmy

See what I mean? You should be with someone else.

Kimmy

Someone who…

Erin frowns.

Erin

Who what?

Tiffany

Makes you happy?

Erin

Charlie makes me happy.

Erin’s brow furrows.

He did. Didn’t he?

Kimmy reaches across the table to squeeze her sister’s hand.

Kimmy

Someone who makes you ACTUALLY happy.

Kimmy

You act like a Stepford wife when you’re with him.

Tiffany

More like Children of the Corn.

Tiffany

Silent and staring.

Kimmy

I disagree. The Children of the Corn don’t smile.

Tiffany

And Erin smiles?

Kimmy

Yes, but it’s that fake, god-it-hurts-so-much-to-move-my-lips kind of smile.

Tiffany

I still like the Children of the Corn reference—

Erin

Will you two stop?

Kimmy

Point is, I never see you laugh with Charlie.

Tiffany

Me neither.

Erin

I do so laugh with Charlie.

Kimmy

Last Thanksgiving, you made a hilarious joke about the stuffing.

Kimmy

And he just stared at you like you were speaking Klingon.

Erin

Maybe it wasn’t a funny joke.

Kimmy

It was hilarious. Everyone laughed.

Kimmy

Everyone but him.

Kimmy

He just sent you THE LOOK.

Erin

There is no LOOK.

Kimmy

And you instantly sobered after that.

Tiffany

I’ve been noticing that too.

Erin

What are you talking about?

Tiffany

You’re…squelching yourself. For him.

Erin

I’m not.

Surely that wasn’t why she felt a tightening band around her chest whenever she thought of Charlie.

And a future with him.

A forever…

Kimmy

Whether you admit it or not, since you’ve been with him, you don’t seem happy.

Tiffany

You haven’t seemed happy for a while.

Erin takes a swig of wine to hide her frown.

The truth was, she wasn’t happy.

Not really.

Certainly, not as elated as Will had made her feel in just a few texts.

Tiffany

I think the problem is…

Kimmy

She’s too loyal.

Tiffany

Too loyal?

Erin

I’m sitting right here.

Kimmy

She IS too loyal. Always has been.

Kimmy

She feels she shouldn’t criticize her boyfriends.

Tiffany

And where’s the fun in that?

Erin

I can hear you.

Kimmy

It took her forever to lose Tom Montague.

Erin

Hey — Tom was nice.

Kimmy

Please. Tom was wrong for you too.

Erin

But he was nice.

Tiffany

A guy can be plenty nice, and still suck out your soul.

Kimmy

And face it. You have a radar for those kinds of guys.

Erin

I do not.

Tiffany

You do.

Kimmy

And then you hold on to them way too long.

Erin

I do not!

Kimmy

Ask yourself, why are you still with Charlie?

Erin

I don’t know.

Tiffany

Is it only because it’s comfortable?

Erin

Will you two shut up?

Kimmy

It’s time for you to find someone who’s right for you.

Kimmy

How about your new boyfriend?

Erin

I said he’s not my boyfriend!

Tiffany

I bet he wants to be.

Erin

Guys, this is ridiculous. How do we even know he’s right for me?

Erin

I’ve never even met the guy.

Kimmy

He did text like he promised.

Kimmy

That’s nice.

Erin

That’s HORNY.

Erin

Besides, he didn’t text me.

Erin

Or at least he didn’t know he was texting me.

Erin

He texted some other random chick…

Erin

Some chick he THINKS is me.

Tiffany

Erin is right.

Tiffany

He’s probably not nice at all.

Tiffany

I’ll take him.

Erin

No!

Tiffany

Come on. It’s not like it’ll work out with him.

Kimmy

What do you mean?

Tiffany

She didn’t tell him it was a wrong number.

Tiffany

When they meet in person, he’ll be pissed.

Kimmy

Not if she tells him now.

Erin

I’m not doing that.

Erin

When he sees I’m not this gorgeous nymph…

Erin

He’ll drop me like a hot potato.

Kimmy leans forward.

Kimmy

But Erin, what if he doesn’t?

Erin

What?

Kimmy

What if he doesn’t dump you?

Kimmy

What if he IS the perfect guy for you?

Erin

Don’t be silly. What are the chances of that?

Kimmy grins.

Kimmy

I have a feeling.

Kimmy

You know about me and my feelings.

Kimmy

Take my advice — tell the guy the truth.

Kimmy

Honestly, what do you have to lose?
