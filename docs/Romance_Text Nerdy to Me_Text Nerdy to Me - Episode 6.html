The next evening, Erin is quivering with nerves as she arrives at the Space Needle.

She scans the crowd waiting for the elevator to the top…

And spots a tall, handsome man with a white carnation in his lapel.

Her pulse thuds.

Her throat goes dry.

She approaches him, but a sleek, gorgeous woman gets there first.

He kisses her and Erin’s heart plummets.

But then deep, sexy voice surrounds her…

Man

Fancy meeting you here.

Erin whirls around.

A delicious-looking man in an Italian suit stands in front of her.

There is no carnation in his lapel.

For the second time that night, Erin’s mood sinks.

Erin

I’m sorry. I’m meeting someone.

The man winks.

Man

I know.

He produces a bouquet of white carnations.

Man

I’ve been waiting for you.

Man

Man

Isn’t that how the saying goes?

Erin

Will?

The mystery man pushes up his sleeve to reveal her phone number.

Will

Erin thumbs at the other guy with the carnation.

Erin

Him?
