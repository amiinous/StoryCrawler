Ensnare - Episode 9

After Will listens to Angie’s story about Kyle and the Source, he texts her urgently.

Will

You need to go tell your father.

Angie

I can’t. They’ll kill him instantly.

Angie

They’ll kill all of us.

Will

Do you really think they’re as powerful as they say they are?

Will

That they actually have secret service agents in their back pocket?

Angie

I don’t have any reason to doubt them.

Will

Then you need to threaten them back.

Angie

With what?

Angie

They’re powerful enough to keep influencing the government — even if I expose them.

Angie

So, I can’t exactly threaten them with taking the “secret” out of their society.

Angie

They’re invincible, and they know it.

Will

Then we need to get to someone who’s not invincible.

Will

We need to get to Kyle.

Angie

I’ve tried.

Angie

He doesn’t care.

Angie

I can’t believe I thought he loved me.

Angie

I feel so stupid.

Will

Angie, he sounds like a sociopath. He made himself into the person he thought you’d trust.

Will

He tricked you into caring about him.

Will

NONE of this is your fault.

Angie

Thanks Will. Hearing that helps.

Will

Well, it’s the truth.

Will

And it also sounds like this society has put a lot of time and energy into Kyle.

Will

They’re protecting this “plan” that they have for him above all else, right?

Angie

Right.

Will

That means he’s valuable to them.

Will

And it also means he’s their weak point.

Angie

I guess…

Will

He’s only one person, Ang. He’s not invincible.

Will

A secret society with an unknown number of anonymous members might be powerful.

Will

But one person?

Will

One person can crack if you find the right pressure point.

Will

So — what’s Kyle’s weakness?

Angie

I don’t know. Up until now, he didn’t seem to have any flaws.

Angie

He always did and said the right thing.

Will

And that didn’t bore you to death?

Angie

😏 It was starting to.

Will

All right, we’ll get back to listing your boyfriend requirements once I know you’re safe.

Will

You said that every member knows a secret about every other member that will completely destroy their reputation, right?

Will

Do you know Kyle’s secret?

Angie

No. I didn’t get far enough into the process to learn everyone’s secrets.

Angie

And they won’t trust me with them now.

Angie

I told them I wouldn’t join their evil, political-bullshit cult.

Will

That’s my girl.

Will

The Source has been texting you, right?

Angie

Yes.

Will

So, it stands to reason that they communicate with Kyle the same way?

Angie

Yeah, but I don’t have their number. It always comes up as UNKNOWN.

Will

We don’t need their number.

Will

But I do need Kyle’s.

Angie

Why?

Will

Because I’m going to hack into his phone and see what they’ve been talking about.

Will

If we’re lucky, I’ll be able to dig up the secret Kyle wants to keep buried.

Will

If we’re really lucky, it’ll be good enough to give you some leverage on him.

Angie

You’re a genius.

Will

I won’t let them hurt you, Angie.

Will

I don’t care what it takes.

Will

Even if I have to change my name so we can go into hiding together.

Will

I won’t let them get to you.

Angie

I know.

Angie

Hurry.

An hour later, Angie is pacing her bedroom. Her gaze keeps darting over to her locked door.

Will

I’ve found something.

Will

Something big.

Angie

Thank God.

Angie

What is it?

Will

Pictures of a car accident from a year ago.

Will

A hit and run.

Will

There’s a body underneath the car.

Angie

Send me the pictures.

Will sends the pictures. Angie’s stomach turns.

She has to force herself not to vomit.

Will

Is that Kyle’s car?

Angie

Definitely.

Will

There are other pictures I didn’t send.

Will

Pictures of the body being cremated.

Will

And screenshots of texts from the Source telling Kyle they’ve taken care of his “problem.”

Will

They also said they’ll be holding the photos as collateral.

Angie

What kind of collateral?

Will

I don’t know.

Will

Maybe your boy gave off the impression that he didn’t want to stay loyal to the society.

Will

Or maybe they wanted extra insurance to make sure he’d follow their instructions.

Angie

Don’t call him “my boy.”

Angie

He’s nothing to me.

Will

It’s okay to feel hurt, you know.

Will

He did betray you.

Will

And you care about him.

Angie

Not anymore.

Will

Angie, are you sure you don’t want to play along for a few more days?

Will

Give yourself more time to make sure this is what you really want to do?

Will

I mean, you are talking about going up against the people who run our entire government, here.

Angie

No. Send me everything, including Kyle’s text chain with the Source.

Angie

This ends tonight.
