Trevor and the Virgin - Episode 3

Jaws drop open, and I’m pretty sure the music screeches to a stop.

Did Trevor just call me his girlfriend!?

He’s beaming down at me and I’m glaring up at him.

Martha

He’s kidding, everyone.

Martha

We’re just friends.

Trevor

Oh stop being so coy, Mar Mar.

Trevor

We’re in love!

I look at him in disbelief: Mar Mar?!

That damn dimple appears again as he fights a smile.

Martha

Umm, excuse us for a minute.

I drag him back toward the door.

Martha

What the hell do you think you’re doing?!

He leans against the door jamb, grinning at me.

Trevor

Just trust me.

Trevor

If people think we’re together…

Trevor

They’re going to start wondering who you are.

Trevor

Which means that they’ll be interested.

Martha

But if Drew thinks I’m taken he’ll never talk to me!

Trevor looks away, as if he’s suddenly uncomfortable.

He runs a hand through his thick brown hair.

Trevor

Martha, you’re very naive.

Trevor

It’s BETTER if Drew thinks you’re with someone — especially me.

Martha

Why especially you?

Trevor

He’s very competitive with me.

Trevor

And I know him better than you do.

Trevor

I’m trying to warn you about what type of person he is—

Martha

And who is going to believe this anyway?

I wave a finger between us.

Martha

Have you ever even had a girlfriend?!

Trevor makes a face like he’s just bitten into a lemon.

Trevor

Uh no.

Trevor

Guys like me don’t do girlfriends…

Trevor

We do one night stands.

I want to ask what he means by “guys like him”…

But a shadow suddenly falls over us.

My heart skips a beat when I look up and see Drew.

My god, he’s so handsome.

Sandy blond hair, bright blue eyes, and a wide smile that could make a girl’s knees weak.

Trevor

Drew, what’s up man?

Drew

Nothing much. Good to see you, Trev.

Drew’s eyes are bright as he turns to look down at me.

Drew

And you brought a friend.

Drew

You look familiar but…I’m not sure we’ve met.

I tamp down my disappointment.

Martha

We were lab partners.

Martha

Your sophomore year.

He nods cheerfully.

Drew

That’s right!

Drew

Man, that class was a joke, wasn’t it?

Drew

I didn’t learn a thing.

I don’t agree, but decide I don’t care.

Drew is actually talking to me!

I think of rule number one and make eye contact with him. Then I say…

Martha

I didn’t either.

Martha

…But I had a great time working with you.

His grin widens as he looks into my eyes.

One second…

Two seconds…

Trevor

SO — where’s the beer?

…Trevor interrupts, a bit loudly in my opinion.

Drew

Outside in the coolers.

Drew

Say, why don’t you two grab a few and come to the dining room?

Drew

We’re getting a game of Drink or Dare going.

Martha

Sounds like fun.

Martha

We’ll be there!

Drew

Sweet.

Drew winks at me before he walks away.

Trevor sighs — probably at the sight of my red cheeks.

He pulls me outside to the deck and stares at me as he reaches into the cooler.

Trevor

Martha, I’m going to guess you’ve never played Drink or Dare before.

Martha

No.

Martha

But how hard can it be?

Trevor

It’s just…these games can get a little intense.

Martha

I don’t mind.

Martha

This is my first real college party and Drew has finally noticed me.

Martha

I want to have fun.

Trevor

This is your first party?!

Martha

Yes!

Trevor

But…

He shakes his head.

Trevor

Damn, you keep surprising me.

Martha

That’s what I’m known for.

Martha

Keeping men on their toes.

I meant for him to laugh at my joke, but he doesn’t.

Trevor

Look, I get you want to have fun.

Trevor

I just want you to know what you’re getting into.

Martha

If Drew’s playing, I’m playing.

Trevor

Martha—

Martha

—You don’t have to come if you don’t want to.

I grab a beer from his hand and head back inside.

He’s not the only one who’s getting surprised tonight.

Trevor Holt is definitely different from what I expected.

After a few moments, he follows me.

When I get to the dining room I see Tilla among the players…

My confidence falters.

Martha

Oh great.

Martha

Red lipstick is here.

Tilla’s eyes narrow dangerously as she takes in Trevor and me.

Martha

Now she definitely knows I was lying to her earlier.

Trevor

Who cares?

Trevor

Just ignore her.

Trevor

That’s what I do.

Martha

That hasn’t seemed to have worked for you yet!

But I try to do just that as I take my seat.

Drew starts explaining the rules and then announces he’s going first.

My pulse quickens as I crack open my beer.

I can’t believe I’m actually doing this!

Drew

Tilla, drink or dare?

Tilla crosses and then uncrosses her long legs.

Tilla

I’m feeling thirsty, Drew.

Tilla

Drink.

Drew scans the crowd and grins when he lands on Trevor.

Drew

Drink with Trevor.

I feel Trevor’s body tense beside me.

His face is unreadable as he grabs a whiskey shot from the table.

He tosses it back and holds the liquid in his mouth.

She bends down, puts both hands on his cheeks…

And “drinks” the shot from his mouth.

I’m shocked by the bawdy display.

Even more so when she locks eyes with me as she does it.

I feel an unexpected pang of annoyance and look away.

A few moments later, Tilla saunters back to her seat.

Tilla

Yumm.

Tilla

Now, it’s my turn.

Her eyes find mine as soon she turns around.

I gulp loudly.

Tilla

Martha, drink or dare?

Martha

Umm…

After the performance she just put on, I go with what I feel is the safer bet.

Martha

…Dare.

Tilla’s eyes light up mischievously.

Tilla

I dare you to take off your panties…

Tilla

And put them in the middle of the table…for the rest of the game.

Trevor stiffens beside me.

He’s no doubt thinking of my granny panties.

I look at Tilla and can’t help but wonder…

Did she see my panties on my bed earlier?

She smiles evilly at my hesitation.

Yeah, she definitely saw them.

Martha

Umm…

Tilla

Come on Martha, what are you waiting for?

Trevor leans down to whisper in my ear.

Trevor

You don’t have to—

I hold up a hand.

Martha

Well, I can’t do that, Tilla.

Martha

Because you see, I’m not wearing any.
