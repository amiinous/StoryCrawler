The Best Mistakes - Episode 9

Skye holds her breath as she waits for a response.

It comes seconds later.

Caleb

Really? Of course I’ll meet you.

Skye

Good.

Caleb

Skye, I’m sorry if I caused you any problems.

Skye

You didn’t. No more apologizing.

Skye

You didn’t do anything wrong.

Caleb

I’m glad you’re giving us a chance. I can’t wait to see you.

Skye

Me too.

And she means it.

She hesitates, but finally starts typing.

Skye

I can’t meet you, Scott.

Scott

Tomorrow then.

Skye

No, never.

Scott

Is this because of Caleb?

Skye

It’s because of you.

Scott

What’s that supposed to mean?

Skye

It means I’m tired of your games.

Scott

I’m not playing games. I want to see you.

Skye

Because you hate the idea of Caleb being with me.

Scott

This is crazy. Of course I hate the idea of you with Caleb.

Scott

Just like you’d hate it if I was with Millie or Vivian or any of your friends.

Scott

Not wanting you with him doesn’t change the fact that I miss us.

Skye

I can’t do this again.

Skye

I’m tired of lies. I want someone I can trust.

Scott

So you’re going out with my shitty roommate?

Skye

It’s not any of your business. Not anymore.

Scott

Skye!

Skye

Goodbye, Scott.

Skye erases all of his texts and breathes a sigh of relief.

Later, as Skye walks to the bar to meet Caleb, she writes Millie.

Skye

I made up my mind.

Millie

And?!

Skye

And you’re going to be happy.

Millie

Yes! Go #TeamCaleb!

Skye

🙂

Millie

What made you decide?

Skye

So many things. But mostly — it just felt right.

Millie

Well, I’m glad you’re finally going for the nice guy.

Skye

Hey! I’ve dated nice guys before.

Millie

No comment.

Skye

Haha.

Millie

Seriously though, I’m really happy for you. I always thought you and Caleb would make a great couple.

Skye

I guess if I’m honest…deep down, I did too.

Millie

I knew it!

Skye

Go ahead, say it.

Millie

I told you so!!!

Skye

You did. I mean, I loved Scott. I really did.

Skye

And some days he was amazing. But others…

Millie

Yeah, I remember.

Millie

But through it all, Prince Charming aka Prince Caleb was there.

Skye

He really was. After Scott and I would fight, he’d be the one to make me feel better.

Skye

He’d make me laugh.

Skye

When Scott would make me feel ugly, Caleb was the one who made me feel beautiful.

Skye

He was always there for me.

Millie

And tell me again why you stuck with Scott for so long?

Skye

Because those good moments were really good. But they weren’t enough.

Millie

You deserve better.

Skye

I think I finally found it.

Millie

You found it a long time ago. You’re just finally realizing it.

Skye arrives outside the bar.

She sees Caleb through the window.

He doesn’t see her.

Skye

Better late than never.

Millie

You can say that again.

Skye

I’m meeting him now at Wine and Kisses.

Millie

Now?!!

Skye

Yeah, I just got here.

Millie

Then why are you still talking to me?! Go!!

Skye

Wish me luck.

Millie

You don’t need it. You’ve got this.

Skye puts her phone away and walks into the bar.

Her pulse is already quickening.
